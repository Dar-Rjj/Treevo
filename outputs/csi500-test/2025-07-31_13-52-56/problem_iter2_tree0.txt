│       └── Assign value: 1 if day t volume > 2 * 30-day average, else 0

│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
│       └── Subtract Low from High
└── Calculate High-Low Range Momentum
    ├── Use High-Low Range over N periods
    │   ├── Calculate N-period rolling window
    │   │   └── Store the range in each period
    └── Calculate the difference between latest and earliest range
        └── Subtract the first range in the window from the last
├── Combine Factors
    ├── Multiply the 30-day Momentum by the Volume Shock indicator
    └── Add the High-Low Range Momentum
        └── Interpretation: The combined factor aims to capture strong price momentum with significant volume surges, along with the trend strength indicated by the high-low range, potentially signaling a continuation or reversal trend.
