Integrated Price and Volume Shock with High-Low Range Momentum
├── 30-Day Price Momentum
│   ├── Get the close price of day t (Ct)
│   ├── Get the close price of day t-30 (Ct_30)
│   └── Calculate: (Ct - Ct_30) / Ct_30
├── Volume Shock
│   ├── Get the volume of day t (Vt)
│   ├── Get the average volume of the last 30 days (excluding day t) (Avg_Vt_30)
│   └── Assign value: 1 if Vt > 2 * Avg_Vt_30, else 0
├── High-Low Range Momentum
│   ├── Use High price of day t (Ht)
│   ├── Use Low price of day t (Lt)
│   ├── Calculate: Ht - Lt for each period over N periods (R_t, R_t-1, ..., R_t-N+1)
│   └── Calculate: (R_t - R_t-N+1) / R_t-N+1
└── Combine Factors
    ├── Multiply 30-day Momentum by Volume Shock
    └── Add High-Low Range Momentum
