Volume-Weighted and Momentum-Based Alpha Factor

│   ├── Obtain VWAP

│   ├── Incorporate Previous Day's Cumulative VWAP Deviation

│       └── Cum_VWAP_Dev = VWAP_Dev + Previous Cum_VWAP_Dev
├── Compute Price Momentum
│   ├── Select Historical Period (e.g., 20 days)
│   ├── Calculate Return Over the Period
│   │   └── Price_Momentum = (Close_t / Close_(t-n)) - 1

│   ├── Define Lookback Window (e.g., 20 days)
│   ├── Calculate Standard Deviation of Log Returns
│   │   └── Log_Returns = log(Close_t / Close_(t-1))
│   └── Determine Volatility

├── Assess Volume Trend
│   ├── Choose Time Frame (e.g., 20 days)

│   └── Measure Relative Volume
│       └── Rel_Volume = Volume / Volume_MA
└── Synthesize Alpha Factor
    ├── Combine Cumulative VWAP Deviation
    ├── Integrate Price Momentum
    ├── Include Volatility
    ├── Factor in Volume Trend
    └── Create Final Alpha Signal
        └── Alpha_Signal = f(Cum_VWAP_Dev, Price_Momentum, Volatility, Rel_Volume)
