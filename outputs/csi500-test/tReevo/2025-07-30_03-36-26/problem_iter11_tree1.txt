Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP) Deviation Factor
├── Calculate Daily VWAP
│   ├── Calculate Total Volume
│   │   └── Sum of Day's Volumes
│   ├── Calculate Total Dollar Value
│   │   └── Sum of (Volume * Close Price)
│   └── Divide Total Dollar Value by Total Volume
├── Calculate VWAP Deviation
│   ├── Use VWAP
│   ├── Use Close Price
│   └── Subtract VWAP from Close Price
├── Calculate Cumulative VWAP Deviation
│   ├── Use VWAP Deviation
│   ├── Use Previous Day's Cumulative VWAP Deviation
│   └── Add VWAP Deviation to Previous Day's Cumulative VWAP Deviation
├── Integrate Short-Term Momentum
│   ├── Choose Short-Term Momentum Period (e.g., 5 days)
│   ├── Calculate Short-Term Momentum
│   │   └── (Close Price t - Close Price t-5) / Close Price t-5
│   └── Combine with Cumulative VWAP Deviation
│       └── (Cumulative VWAP Deviation + Short-Term Momentum)
├── Integrate Medium-Term Momentum
│   ├── Choose Medium-Term Momentum Period (e.g., 10 days)
│   ├── Calculate Medium-Term Momentum
│   │   └── (Close Price t - Close Price t-10) / Close Price t-10
│   └── Combine with Cumulative VWAP Deviation
│       └── (Cumulative VWAP Deviation + Medium-Term Momentum)
└── Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Use Cumulative VWAP Deviation
    ├── Use Short-Term Momentum
    ├── Use Medium-Term Momentum
    └── Adaptive Weighting
        ├── Assign Weights Based on Volatility
        │   └── If Volatility is High, Increase Weight on Short-Term Momentum
        │   └── If Volatility is Low, Increase Weight on Medium-Term Momentum
        └── Combine Weighted Factors
            └── Final Alpha Factor = (w1 * Cumulative VWAP Deviation) + 
                                      (w2 * Short-Term Momentum) + 
                                      (w3 * Medium-Term Momentum)
