Price Momentum and Volume Spike Indicator
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Compute Average Close Price (Last 10 Days)
│   │   ├── Use Close price (t-1 to t-10)
│   │   └── Sum and Divide by 10
│   └── Compare with Current Close Price
│       ├── Use Close price (t)
│       └── Subtract Average Close Price
└── Detect Volume Spike
    ├── Compute Average Volume (Last 10 Days)
    │   ├── Use Volume (t-1 to t-10)
    │   └── Sum and Divide by 10
    └── Identify Spike
        ├── Use Volume (t)
        └── Compare with Average Volume
            ├── Subtract Average Volume
            └── Check if Difference is Significant
                ├── If Difference > 2 * Average Volume, then Spike = 1
                └── Else, Spike = 0
