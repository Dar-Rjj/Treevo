Crossover: Weighted Close to Open Momentum Adjusted by Price Volatility and Volume-Weighted Breakaway Momentum
├── Calculate Close-to-Open Return
│   ├── Use Close price (Ct)
│   └── Use Open price (Ot)
├── Calculate Daily Volatility
│   ├── Use High price (Ht)
│   ├── Use Low price (Lt)
│   └── Subtract and take Absolute Value
├── Calculate Momentum
│   ├── Use Close prices of the last 5 days (Ct, Ct-1, Ct-2, Ct-3, Ct-4, Ct-5)
│   └── Calculate the sum of the differences
├── Identify Price Breakaways
│   ├── Define High Breakaway
│   │   ├── Use High Price of day t (Ht)
│   │   ├── Use High Price of day t-1 (Ht-1)
│   │   └── Is High Breakaway = Ht > Ht-1 and (Ht - Ht-1) / Ht-1 > 0.05
│   └── Define Low Breakaway
│       ├── Use Low Price of day t (Lt)
│       ├── Use Low Price of day t-1 (Lt-1)
│       └── Is Low Breakaway = Lt < Lt-1 and (Lt-1 - Lt) / Lt-1 > 0.05
├── Volume-Weighted Score
│   ├── If High Breakaway and Low Breakaway are false
│   │   └── Volume-Weighted Score = 0
│   ├── If High Breakaway is true
│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t (Vt)
│   │   └── Volume-Weighted Score = (Ct - Ot) * Vt
│   ├── If Low Breakaway is true
│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t (Vt)
│   │   └── Volume-Weighted Score = -(Ct - Ot) * Vt
├── Combine Factors
│   ├── Multiply Close-to-Open Return by Volume (Ct - Ot) * Vt
│   └── Divide by Daily Volatility
├── Momentum Adjusted
│   ├── Use Calculated Momentum
│   └── Subtract from Combined Factor
└── Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Add Volume-Weighted Score and Momentum Adjusted Factor
    └── Final Alpha Factor = Sum(Volume-Weighted Score + Momentum Adjusted Factor, last n days)
