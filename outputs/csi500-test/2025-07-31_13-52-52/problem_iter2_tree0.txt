```txt
Volume-Weighted Average Momentum Over High and Low Prices
├── Calculate Daily Price Movement
│   ├── Use Close Price of day t (Ct)
│   ├── Use Open Price of day t (Ot)
│   └── Price Movement = Ct - Ot
├── Identify Price Breakaways
│   ├── Define High Breakaway
│   │   ├── Use High Price of day t (Ht)
│   │   ├── Use High Price of day t-1 (Ht-1)
│   │   └── Is High Breakaway = Ht > Ht-1 and (Ht - Ht-1) / Ht-1 > 0.05
│   └── Define Low Breakaway
│       ├── Use Low Price of day t (Lt)
│       ├── Use Low Price of day t-1 (Lt-1)
│       └── Is Low Breakaway = Lt < Lt-1 and (Lt-1 - Lt) / Lt-1 > 0.05
├── Volume-Weighted Score
│   ├── If High Breakaway and Low Breakaway are false
│   │   └── Volume-Weighted Score = 0
│   ├── If High Breakaway is true
│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t (Vt)
│   │   └── Volume-Weighted Score = (Ct - Ot) * Vt
│   ├── If Low Breakaway is true
│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t (Vt)
│   │   └── Volume-Weighted Score = -(Ct - Ot) * Vt
├── Calculate High-to-Low Price Percentage Change
│   ├── Use High price of day t (Ht)
│   ├── Use Low price of day t (Lt)
│   └── High-to-Low Percentage Change = (Ht - Lt) / Lt
├── Combine Volume-Weighted Score with High-to-Low Percentage Change
│   ├── If Volume-Weighted Score is not zero
│   │   └── Combined Score = Volume-Weighted Score + (High-to-Low Percentage Change * Vt)
│   ├── If Volume-Weighted Score is zero
│   │   └── Combined Score = 0
└── Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Accumulate Combined Score over n days
    └── Final Alpha Factor = Sum(Combined Score, last n days)
```
