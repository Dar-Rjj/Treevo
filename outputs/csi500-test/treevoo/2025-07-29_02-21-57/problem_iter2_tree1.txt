```txt
Crossover Momentum and Breakout Indicator
├── Calculate Price and Range Momentum
│   ├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   │   ├── Use Close price of today
│   │   └── Use Close price N days ago (e.g., 20 days)
│   │       └── Subtract to get the raw momentum value
│   └── Calculate Daily Range
│       ├── Use High price
│       └── Use Low price
│           └── Subtract Low from High
│   └── Combine Momentum and Range
│       ├── Add the raw momentum value to the daily range
│       └── Store as Combined Momentum
├── Apply Exponential Moving Average (EMA) Smoothing
│   ├── Choose a smoothing factor (e.g., 0.3)
│   ├── Initialize EMA with the combined momentum value
│   └── Update EMA using the formula: EMA_today = (Combined_Momentum * alpha) + (EMA_yesterday * (1 - alpha))
├── Identify Breakouts
│   ├── Compare Today's Close to Yesterday's High
│   └── Compare Today's Close to Yesterday's Low
│       ├── If Close is higher than Yesterday's High
│       │   └── Mark as an Upward Breakout
│       └── If Close is lower than Yesterday's Low
│           └── Mark as a Downward Breakout
├── Calculate Breakout Strength
│   ├── For Upward Breakouts
│   │   ├── Calculate the difference between Today's Close and Yesterday's High
│   │   └── Divide by 20-Day Average Daily Range
│   └── For Downward Breakouts
│       ├── Calculate the difference between Yesterday's Low and Today's Close
│       └── Divide by 20-Day Average Daily Range
├── Cumulate Breakout Strength
│   ├── Sum all positive Breakout Strengths over the past 10 days
│   └── Sum all negative Breakout Strengths over the past 10 days
│       └── Subtract the sum of negative strengths from the sum of positive strengths
└── Combine EMA and Breakout Strength
    ├── Multiply EMA smoothed combined momentum by the cumulative breakout strength
    └── Store as the final Crossover Momentum and Breakout Indicator
```
