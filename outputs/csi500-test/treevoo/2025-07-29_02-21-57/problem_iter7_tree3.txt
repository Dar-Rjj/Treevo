```txt
Calculate True Range
├── Get High, Low, and Previous Close prices
│   ├── Use High
│   ├── Use Low
│   └── Use Previous Close (if available, otherwise use Close)
├── Calculate TR1: High - Low
├── Calculate TR2: Abs(High - Previous Close)
├── Calculate TR3: Abs(Low - Previous Close)
└── True Range: Max(TR1, TR2, TR3)

Calculate 14-Period Average True Range (ATR)
├── Sum of True Ranges over the last 14 periods
└── Divide by 14

Calculate Intraday High-Low Spread
├── Use High price
└── Use Low price

Calculate Close-to-Open Return
├── Use Close price
└── Use Open price

Calculate Price Momentum
├── Get Current Close price
├── Get Close price from 14 days ago
└── Calculate Momentum: (Current Close - Close 14 days ago)

Weight by Volume
├── Use Volume
└── Multiply Volume by Momentum

Combine Indicators
├── Intraday High-Low Spread
├── Close-to-Open Return
└── Weighted Momentum
    ├── Average the three indicators
    └── Subtract the average from the Weighted Momentum
        ├── If result is positive, it indicates strong positive intraday momentum
        └── If result is negative, it shows the stock may be weakening

Generate Alpha Factor
├── Divide Weighted Momentum by ATR
└── Add ATR Momentum to Combined Indicators
```
