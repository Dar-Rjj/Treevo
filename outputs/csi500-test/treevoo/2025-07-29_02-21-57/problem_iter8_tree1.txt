```txt
Calculate True Range
├── Get High, Low, and Previous Close prices
│   ├── Use High
│   ├── Use Low
│   └── Use Previous Close (if available, otherwise use Close)
├── Calculate TR1: High - Low
├── Calculate TR2: Abs(High - Previous Close)
├── Calculate TR3: Abs(Low - Previous Close)
└── True Range: Max(TR1, TR2, TR3)

Calculate 14-Period Average True Range (ATR)
├── Sum of True Ranges over the last 14 periods
└── Divide by 14

Calculate Intraday High-Low Spread
├── Use High price
└── Use Low price

Calculate Close-to-Open Return
├── Use Close price
└── Use Open price

Calculate Price Momentum
├── Get Current Close price
├── Get Close price from 14 days ago
└── Calculate Momentum: (Current Close - Close 14 days ago)

Weight by Volume
├── Use Volume
└── Multiply Volume by Momentum

Calculate Volume-Weighted Average Price
├── Use Open, High, Low, Close prices
└── Use Volume
    ├── Multiply each price by its corresponding volume
        ├── For Open price
        ├── For High price
        ├── For Low price
        └── For Close price
            ├── Sum the products
            └── Sum the volumes
                ├── Divide the sum of products by the sum of volumes
                └── Get a single value (Volume-Weighted Average Price for the day)

Combine Indicators
├── Intraday High-Low Spread
├── Close-to-Open Return
├── Weighted Momentum
├── Volume-Weighted Average Price
    ├── Average the four indicators
    └── Subtract the average from the Weighted Momentum
        ├── If result is positive, it indicates strong positive intraday momentum
        └── If result is negative, it shows the stock may be weakening

Adjust Momentum by ATR
├── Divide Weighted Momentum by ATR

Generate Alpha Factor
├── Add ATR Adjusted Momentum to Combined Indicator Adjustment
└── Include Volume-Weighted Average Price in the final factor
```
