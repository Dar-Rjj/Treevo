Explore Price Patterns and Volume Adjusted Momentum
├── Calculate Moving Averages
│   ├── Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Close Prices
│   │   ├── SMA_10 = Sum(Close[i] for i in range(t-9, t+1)) / 10
│   │   └── SMA_20 = Sum(Close[i] for i in range(t-19, t+1)) / 20
│   └── Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Close Prices
│       ├── EMA_10 = (Close[t] * (2 / (1 + 10))) + (EMA_10[t-1] * (1 - (2 / (1 + 10))))
│       └── EMA_20 = (Close[t] * (2 / (1 + 20))) + (EMA_20[t-1] * (1 - (2 / (1 + 20))))
├── Analyze Volatility
│   ├── Calculate True Range
│   │   └── TR = Max(High[t], Close[t-1]) - Min(Low[t], Close[t-1])
│   └── Calculate Average True Range (ATR)
│       └── ATR_14 = (Sum(TR[i] for i in range(t-13, t+1)) / 14)
├── Identify Breakouts
│   ├── New Highs or Lows
│   │   ├── If High[t] > Max(High[i] for i in range(t-5, t)), then New_High = True
│   │   └── If Low[t] < Min(Low[i] for i in range(t-5, t)), then New_Low = True
├── Measure Momentum
│   ├── Rate of Change (ROC) of Close Prices
│   │   └── ROC_12 = (Close[t] - Close[t-12]) / Close[t-12]
│   └── Relative Strength Index (RSI)
│       └── RSI_14 = 100 - (100 / (1 + (Average Gain / Average Loss)))
│           ├── Average Gain = Sum(Max(Close[i] - Close[i-1], 0) for i in range(t-13, t+1)) / 14
│           └── Average Loss = Abs(Sum(Min(Close[i] - Close[i-1], 0) for i in range(t-13, t+1)) / 14)
├── Assess Volume Changes
│   └── On-Balance Volume (OBV)
│       └── OBV[t] = OBV[t-1] + (Volume[t] if Close[t] > Close[t-1] else (-Volume[t] if Close[t] < Close[t-1] else 0))
├── Evaluate Trading Volume
│   └── Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
│       └── VWAP = Sum((High[i] + Low[i] + Close[i]) / 3 * Volume[i] for i in range(t-1, t+1)) / Sum(Volume[i] for i in range(t-1, t+1))
├── Calculate Short-Term Volume Weighted Moving Average (VWMA)
│   ├── Daily Close Prices
│   └── Volume Weighting
│       ├── Use Volume
│       └── Multiply Volume with Price
└── Calculate Long-Term Volume Weighted Moving Average (VWMA)
    ├── Daily Close Prices
    └── Volume Weighting
        ├── Use Volume
        └── Multiply Volume with Price
├── Calculate 5-Day Volume Adjusted Momentum
│   ├── Compute 5-Day Close Price Return
│   │   ├── Use Close price of day t-5
│   │   └── Use Close price of day t
│   └── Calculate 5-Day Volume Ratio
│       ├── Sum volume from day t-4 to day t
│       └── Divide by Average 5-Day Volume
└── Combined Crossover and Momentum Signal
    ├── Compare Short-Term VWMA and Long-Term VWMA
    │   ├── If Short-Term VWMA > Long-Term VWMA, generate Buy Signal
    │   └── If Short-Term VWMA < Long-Term VWMA, generate Sell Signal
    ├── Evaluate Volume Adjusted Momentum
    │   ├── If Volume Adjusted Momentum is positive, generate Buy Signal
    │   └── If Volume Adjusted Momentum is negative, generate Sell Signal
    └── Combine Signals
        ├── If both VWMA Crossover and Volume Adjusted Momentum are Buy, strong
