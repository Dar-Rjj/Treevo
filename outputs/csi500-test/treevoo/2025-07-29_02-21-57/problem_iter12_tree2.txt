```txt
Price Momentum Indicator with Volume Consideration
├── Calculate Raw Momentum
│   ├── Close price of today
│   └── Close price N days ago (e.g., 20 days)
│       └── Subtract to get the raw momentum value
├── Volume Weighted Momentum
│   ├── Volume of today
│   └── Volume N days ago (e.g., 20 days)
│       └── Multiply raw momentum by today's volume, then divide by sum of volumes over N days
└── EMA Smoothing
    ├── Set smoothing factor (alpha, e.g., 0.3)
    └── Compute EMA: EMA_today = (volume_weighted_momentum * alpha) + (EMA_yesterday * (1 - alpha))
```
