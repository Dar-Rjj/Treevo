│   └── Sum all negative Breakout Strengths over the past 10 days
│       └── Subtract sum of negative strengths from sum of positive strengths
├── Calculate Intraday High-Low Spread
│   └── Subtract Low from High
├── Calculate Close-to-Open Return
│   └── Compute (Close - Open) / Open
├── Combine Indicators
    ├── Sum Intraday High-Low Spread, Close-to-Open Return, and Cumulated Breakout Strength
    └── Evaluate the result
        ├── If result is positive, it indicates strong positive intraday and breakout momentum
        └── If result is negative, it shows the stock may be weakening
