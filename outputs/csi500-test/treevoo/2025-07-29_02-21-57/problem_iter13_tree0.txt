```txt
Calculate True Range
├── Get High, Low, and Previous Close prices
│   ├── Use High
│   ├── Use Low
│   └── Use Previous Close (if available, otherwise use Close)
├── Calculate TR1: High - Low
├── Calculate TR2: Abs(High - Previous Close)
├── Calculate TR3: Abs(Low - Previous Close)
└── True Range: Max(TR1, TR2, TR3)

Calculate 14-Period Average True Range (ATR)
├── Sum of True Ranges over the last 14 periods
└── Divide by 14

Calculate Intraday High-Low Spread
├── Subtract Low from High

Calculate Close-to-Open Return
├── Subtract Open from Close

Calculate Price Momentum
├── Subtract Close price 14 days ago from Current Close

Weight by Volume
├── Multiply Volume by Momentum

Calculate Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
├── Multiply each price by its corresponding volume
    ├── For Open price
    ├── For High price
    ├── For Low price
    └── For Close price
        ├── Sum the products
        └── Sum the volumes
            ├── Divide the sum of products by the sum of volumes

Adjust Momentum by ATR
├── Divide Weighted Momentum by ATR

Generate Alpha Factor
├── Add ATR Adjusted Momentum to VWAP
```
