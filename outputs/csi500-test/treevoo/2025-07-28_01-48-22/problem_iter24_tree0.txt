```txt
Alpha Mining Thought Tree
- Technical Indicators as Alpha Factors
  - Moving Averages
    - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
      - SMA of closing prices over 5, 10, 20, and 50 days
    - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
      - EMA of closing prices over 5, 10, 20, and 50 days
    - Difference between EMAs
      - EMA(10) - EMA(5), EMA(20) - EMA(10), EMA(50) - EMA(20)
  - Momentum Indicators
    - Rate of Change (ROC)
      - ROC of closing prices over 5, 10, 20, and 50 days
    - Relative Strength Index (RSI)
      - RSI with a 14-day period using closing prices
  - Volume-based Indicators
    - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
      - OBV using closing prices and volume
    - Volume Rate of Change (VROC)
      - VROC with a 5, 10, 20, and 50 day period
- Price Pattern Indicators as Alpha Factors
  - High-Low Range
    - High - Low of the day
  - Close-Open Range
    - Close - Open of the day
  - Breakout Indicator
    - Number of days since the last 52-week high
- Volatility Indicators as Alpha Factors
  - True Range
    - Max(High - Low, Abs(High - Previous Close), Abs(Low - Previous Close))
  - Average True Range (ATR)
    - ATR over 14 days using the true range
- Combined Indicators as Alpha Factors
  - SMA Crossover
    - SMA(5) > SMA(20) and SMA(20) > SMA(50)
  - EMA Crossover
    - EMA(5) > EMA(20) and EMA(20) > EMA(50)
  - OBV and Volume Confirmation
    - OBV is increasing and volume is above its 50-day moving average
  - RSI and Momentum Confirmation
    - RSI < 30 and ROC(5) is positive
- Seasonal and Timing Indicators as Alpha Factors
  - Month of the Year
    - Binary indicator for specific months (e.g., January, July, etc.)
  - Day of the Week
    - Binary indicator for specific days (e.g., Monday, Friday, etc.)
  - Quarter of the Year
    - Binary indicator for specific quarters (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- Fundamental and Market Sentiment Indicators as Alpha Factors
  - Trading Volume Spike
    - Volume is more than 2 times the 50-day moving average
  - Large Price Movement
    - Absolute daily return (Close/Previous Close - 1) > 0.05
  - High Volume and Price Movement
    - Volume > 2 * 50-day moving average and absolute daily return > 0.05
```
