[Alpha Mining for Future Stock Returns]
- [Price Action Factors]
  - [Momentum Indicator]
    - [Simple Moving Average Crossover]
      - [Short-term SMA (5 days)]
      - [Long-term SMA (20 days)]
      - [Crossover Signal: Short-term SMA > Long-term SMA]
    - [Calculate Short-Term Return]
      └── Compute (Close_t / Close_{t-5}) - 1
    - [Calculate Long-Term Return]
      └── Compute (Close_t / Close_{t-20}) - 1
    - [Daily Log Return]
      └── Apply Logarithmic Difference on Close Price
    - [20-Day Momentum]
      └── Sum Last 20 Days' Log Returns
    - [Combine Momentum Components]
      ├── Intraday Momentum Component
      │   └── (Close - Open) / Open * Volume
      ├── Historical Momentum Component
      │   └── Close - Close_10_days_ago
      └── Sum of Components
          └── Intraday Momentum + Historical Momentum
    - [Smooth Combined Momentum]
      └── Apply Simple Moving Average (SMA) over 5 days
    - [Adjust Smoothed Momentum]
      ├── Average Daily Range
      │   └── Sum(Daily High-Low Range, 10 days) / 10
      ├── Volatility Multiplier
      │   └── 1 / (Average Daily Range + 1)
      └── Adjusted Smoothed Momentum
          └── Smoothed Momentum * Volatility Multiplier
- [Volume Analysis Factors]
  - [Volume Trend Indicators]
    - [Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)]
      - [Cumulative Volume * Close Price]
      - [Total Cumulative Volume]
      - [VWAP = (Cumulative Volume * Close Price) / Total Cumulative Volume]
    - [Volume-Price Indicator]
      └── 0.5 * (Volume_t - Volume_{t-1}) + 0.3 * Avg High-Low Range (5 days) + (Close_t - Close_{t-1})
    - [Volume Spikes]
      ├── Standard Deviation of Volume over N Days
      └── Compare Current Volume with Mean Volume
          └── If Current Volume > (Mean Volume + K * Standard Deviation), set indicator to 1, else 0
- [Market Strength and Sentiment]
  - [Relative Strength Index (RSI)]
    - [Calculate Up Periods and Down Periods over 14 Days]
    - [Average Gain and Loss]
    - [RS Value = Average Gain / Average Loss]
    - [RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))]
  - [Money Flow Index (MFI)]
    - [Typical Price = (High + Low + Close) / 3]
    - [Raw Money Flow = Typical Price * Volume]
    - [Positive and Negative Money Flow]
    - [Money Flow Ratio = Positive / Negative]
    - [MFI = 100 - (100 / (1 + Money Flow Ratio))]
  - [Relative Strength]
    └── Subtract Long-Term Return from Short-Term Return
  - [Multiply Relative Strength by Volume Spike Indicator]
    └── Compute (Relative Strength) * Volume Spike Indicator
- [Volatility Measure]
  - [True Range Calculation]
    - [Current Day High - Current Day Low]
    - [Previous Day Close - Current Day Low]
    - [Current Day High - Previous Day Close]
    - [Max of the three as True Range]
  - [Average True Range (14 days)]
  - [High-Low Range Over Average]
    ├── High-Low Range
    │   └── High - Low
    └── Divide by Average High-Low Range
- [Final Factor]
  ├── Adjusted Smoothed Momentum
  ├── Volume-Price Indicator
  ├── Multiply Relative Strength by Volume Spike Indicator
  └── High-Low Range Over Average
