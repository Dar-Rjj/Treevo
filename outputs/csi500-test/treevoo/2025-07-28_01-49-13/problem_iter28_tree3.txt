- Explore trend and momentum factors
  - Calculate daily return
    - (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1}
  - Calculate cumulative returns over different periods
    - Sum of daily returns over 5 days, 20 days, 60 days
- Analyze price patterns
  - Calculate moving averages
    - Simple moving average of close prices over 5, 20, 60 days
  - Calculate the distance between high and low
    - High_t - Low_t
- Assess volatility
  - Calculate standard deviation of daily returns
    - Standard deviation of daily returns over 5, 20, 60 days
- Evaluate volume indicators
  - Calculate change in volume
    - Volume_t - Volume_{t-1}
  - Calculate relative volume
    - Volume_t / Average(Volume_{t-20:t})
- Combine multiple indicators
  - Create a composite factor
    - Weighted sum of selected individual factors
- Consider price/volume relationships
  - Calculate on-balance volume
    - Cumulative sum of (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) * Volume_t
  - Calculate volume-weighted average price
    - (High_t + Low_t + Close_t) / 3 * Volume_t / TotalVolume
- Investigate trading range
  - Calculate average true range
    - Average of (|High_t - Low_t|, |High_t - Close_{t-1}|, |Low_t - Close_{t-1}|) over 14 days
  - Calculate percentage price oscillator
    - (EMA(Close, 12) - EMA(Close, 26)) / EMA(Close, 26)
- Analyze intraday trading activity
  - Calculate intraday range
    - (High_t - Low_t) / Open_t
  - Calculate opening gap
    - (Open_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1}
- Examine market sentiment
  - Calculate relative strength index
    - 100 - (100 / (1 + (Average gain / Average loss) over 14 days))
  - Calculate money flow index
    - 100 - (100 / (1 + (Positive money flow / Negative money flow) over 14 days))
- Investigate liquidity
  - Calculate bid-ask spread
    - (Ask_t - Bid_t) / (Ask_t + Bid_t)
  - Calculate turnover ratio
    - Volume_t / SharesOutstanding
- Evaluate order flow
  - Calculate order imbalance
    - (Buy orders - Sell orders) / Total orders
  - Calculate order book depth
    - Sum of (Volume at each price level) within a certain price range
