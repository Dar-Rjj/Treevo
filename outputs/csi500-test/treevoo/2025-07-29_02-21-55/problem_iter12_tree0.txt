Alpha Factor Thought Tree
- Base Alpha Factors
  - Price Momentum
    - Daily Return: (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1}
      - Extend to 5-day Momentum: (Close_t - Close_{t-5}) / Close_{t-5}
  - Volume-based Indicators
    - Average Volume: Sum(Volume, t-4 to t) / 5
      - Volume Ratio: Volume_t / Average Volume
  - Price Range
    - Daily Price Range: (High_t - Low_t)
      - Expand to 20-day Average Daily Price Range: Average(Daily Price Range, t-19 to t)
  - Open-Close Pattern
    - Intraday Direction: (Close_t - Open_t) / Open_t
      - Introduce a threshold for significant intraday movement: If |Intraday Direction| > 0.05 then 1 else 0
  - Candlestick Patterns
    - Bullish Engulfing
      - Condition 1: Close_{t-1} < Open_{t-1}
      - Condition 2: Close_t > Open_t
      - Condition 3: Close_t > Open_{t-1} and Open_t < Close_{t-1}
  - Trading Volume and Price Correlation
    - Correlation between 5-day Returns and 5-day Volume Changes
      - Use Pearson's correlation coefficient
- Advanced Alpha Factors
  - Trend Following
    - Moving Average Crossover: MA(Close, 50) - MA(Close, 200)
      - Enhance with 5-day smoothing of the crossover signal
  - Volatility Adjusted Momentum
    - Divide Daily Return by 20-day Average True Range (ATR)
  - Volume Spike with Price Increase
    - Volume Spike: If Volume_t > 1.5 * Average Volume
    - Coupled with significant price increase: If (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1} > 0.02
- Hybrid Alpha Factors
  - Combining Momentum and Volume
    - Weighted Momentum: (Daily Return * Volume Ratio) + ((Close_t - SMA(Close, 20)) / SMA(Close, 20))
  - Multi-pattern Analysis
    - Presence of both bullish engulfing and high volume spike in the last 3 days
- Market Sentiment Factors
  - Cumulative Money Flow
    - Money Flow: (Typical Price_t - Typical Price_{t-1}) * Volume_t
      - Typical Price: (High + Low + Close) / 3
      - Sum(Money Flow, t-19 to t)
- Seasonal and Cyclical Factors
  - Monthly Seasonality
    - Average of the same month return over the last 5 years
  - Day of Week Effect
    - Friday Close vs. Monday Open
