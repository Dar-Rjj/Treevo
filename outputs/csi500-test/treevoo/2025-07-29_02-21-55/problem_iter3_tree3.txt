Price Momentum and Volume Confirmation with Price Oscillation Integration
├── Calculate Price Momentum

│   ├── Use Volume

│   └── Compute Volume Ratio
│       ├── Compare with 1

│       │   └── Set to 1
│       ├── Compare with < 1
│       │   ├── Volume Ratio < 1
│       │   └── Set to -1
│       └── Otherwise

    ├── Use High and Low prices

        ├── Compute 20-day Maximum Range

        │   └── Min of (Day t to t-19 Low price)
        ├── Compute 20-day Actual Range
        │   ├── Day t High price - Day t-19 Low price
        │   └── Subtract 20-day Maximum Range
        └── Determine Oscillation Score
            ├── Compare 20-day Actual Range with 20-day Maximum Range
            │   ├── If 20-day Actual Range > 50% of 20-day Maximum Range
            │   │   └── Set to 1
            │   ├── If 20-day Actual Range < 20% of 20-day Maximum Range
            │   │   └── Set to -1
            │   └── Otherwise
            │       └── Set to 0
