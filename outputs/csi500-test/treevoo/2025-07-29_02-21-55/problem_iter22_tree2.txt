```txt
Alpha Mining for Stock Returns Prediction
- Technical Indicators
  - Price Momentum
    - Rate of change (ROC) of close prices from 21 days ago to today
  - Volume Analysis
    - Volume ratio: current day's volume divided by the average volume of the past 20 days
- Volatility Measures
  - Historical Volatility
    - Standard deviation of daily returns over the last 30 days
  - True Range
    - Average true range (ATR) over the past 14 days, calculated as max(high-low, high-previous close, previous close-low)
- Relative Strength Index (RSI)
  - RSI based on 14-day period to gauge overbought/oversold conditions
- Trend Following
  - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
    - Short-term EMA (12 days) minus long-term EMA (26 days), with a signal line (9-day EMA of the MACD)
- Pattern Recognition
  - Bullish Engulfing Pattern
    - Today's open price is below yesterday's close, and today's close is above yesterday's open
  - Bearish Engulfing Pattern
    - Today's open price is above yesterday's close, and today's close is below yesterday's open
- Breakout Detection
  - Days since the last new 52-week high or low
  - Distance from the 52-week high or low expressed as a percentage of the current price
- Order Flow Imbalance
  - Net tick volume (NTV): difference between the current day's amount and the previous day's, normalized by the current day's volume
- Liquidity Indicators
  - Frequency of touches to the daily high or low within a 14-day window, counting how many times it reaches the high or low
- Seasonal Effects
  - Return on the same calendar day across years
  - Monthly return effect, measuring the average return for each month of the year
```
