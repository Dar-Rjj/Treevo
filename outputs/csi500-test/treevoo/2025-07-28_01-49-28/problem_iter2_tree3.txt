Volume-Adjusted Momentum and High-Low Spread Indicator (VAMHLI)
├── Calculate Daily Returns
│   ├── Use Today's Close
│   └── Use Yesterday's Close
│       └── Subtract Yesterday's Close from Today's Close
├── Calculate Volume Ratio
│   ├── Use Today's Volume
│   └── Use Yesterday's Volume
│       └── Divide Today's Volume by Yesterday's Volume
├── Combine Returns and Volume Ratio
│   ├── Multiply Daily Returns by Volume Ratio
│   └── Sum Over a Fixed Lookback Period (e.g., 20 days)
├── Calculate High-Low Spread
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
│       └── Subtract Low from High to get Spread
├── Incorporate Volume
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Multiply Spread by Volume
│       └── Label as Volume-Adjusted High-Low Spread
├── Combine Adjusted Returns and Volume-Adjusted High-Low Spread
│   ├── Add Volume-Adjusted High-Low Spread to Combined Returns
│   └── Label as Combined Indicator
└── Finalize VAMHLI
    ├── Calculate the Average of Combined Indicator
    └── Subtract the Overall Average Return (over the same lookback period) from the Combined Indicator Average
