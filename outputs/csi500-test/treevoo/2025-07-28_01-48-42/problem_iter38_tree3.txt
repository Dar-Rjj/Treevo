```txt
Performing alpha mining to generate novel, interpretable factors for predicting future stock returns
- Utilize price and volume data
  - Calculate momentum indicators
    - Simple Moving Average (SMA) over different periods
      - SMA of closing prices
      - SMA ratio: Short-term SMA / Long-term SMA
    - Exponential Moving Average (EMA) over different periods
      - EMA of closing prices
      - EMA ratio: Short-term EMA / Long-term EMA
    - Rate of Change (ROC) over different periods
      - ROC of closing prices
      - ROC of high prices
      - ROC of low prices
  - Calculate trend indicators
    - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
      - MACD line: 12-period EMA - 26-period EMA of closing prices
      - Signal line: 9-period EMA of MACD line
      - MACD histogram: MACD line - Signal line
    - Directional Movement Index (DMI)
      - Positive Directional Indicator (+DI): 14-period EMA of the difference between current high and previous high
      - Negative Directional Indicator (-DI): 14-period EMA of the difference between previous low and current low
      - Directional Indicator (DX): 100 * |+DI - -DI| / (+DI + -DI)
      - Average Directional Index (ADX): 14-period EMA of DX
  - Calculate volatility indicators
    - True Range (TR)
      - TR: max(current high - current low, abs(current high - previous close), abs(current low - previous close))
      - Average True Range (ATR): 14-period EMA of TR
    - Bollinger Bands
      - Middle band: 20-period SMA of closing prices
      - Upper band: Middle band + 2 * 20-period standard deviation of closing prices
      - Lower band: Middle band - 2 * 20-period standard deviation of closing prices
  - Calculate volume-based indicators
    - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
      - OBV: Adjusted by the relationship between the current close and the previous close
    - Chaikin Money Flow (CMF)
      - CMF: 20-period sum of (close - low) - (high - close) / (high - low) * volume / 20-period sum of volume
  - Integrate multiple indicators
    - Composite Momentum Indicator (CMI)
      - CMI: Combination of SMA ratio, EMA ratio, and ROC
    - Composite Trend Indicator (CTI)
      - CTI: Combination of MACD, ADX, and DMI
    - Composite Volatility Indicator (CVI)
      - CVI: Combination of ATR and Bollinger Band width
    - Composite Volume Indicator (CVoI)
      - CVoI: Combination of OBV and CMF
  - Generate final alpha factor
    - Final Alpha Factor (FAF)
      - FAF: Weighted sum of CMI, CTI, CVI, and CVoI
  - Explore additional novel indicators
    - High-Low Ratio (HLR)
      - HLR: 14-period EMA of (high - low) / (high + low)
    - Price Volume Trend (PVT)
      - PVT: PVT(t-1) + (current_close - previous_close) / previous_close * current_volume
    - Volume-Weighted Close (VWC)
      - VWC: (high + low + close) / 3 * volume
  - Combine novel indicators with existing composite indicators
    - Enhanced Composite Momentum Indicator (ECMI)
      - ECMI: CMI + HLR
    - Enhanced Composite Trend Indicator (ECTI)
      - ECTI: CTI + PVT
    - Enhanced Composite Volatility Indicator (ECVI)
      - ECVI: CVI + VWC
  - Generate enhanced final alpha factor
    - Enhanced Final Alpha Factor (EFAF)
      - EFAF: Weighted sum of ECMI, ECTI, ECVI, and CVoI
```
