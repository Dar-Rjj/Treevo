Volume-Weighted Momentum Adjusted by Open-Close Spread and High-Low Range
├── Calculate Daily Open-Close Price Spread
│   └── Subtract Close price from Open price to get Spread
├── Calculate Momentum
│   ├── Determine Lookback Period (e.g., 10 days)
│   └── Compute difference between current and average close
├── Adjust Momentum by Absolute Daily Open-Close Spread
│   └── Multiply Momentum by the absolute value of the Open-Close Spread
├── Calculate Daily High-Low Range
│   └── Subtract Low price from High price to get Range
├── Adjust Momentum by High-Low Range
│   └── Divide Adjusted Momentum by the High-Low Range
└── Weight Adjusted Momentum by Volume
    └── Multiply the final adjusted momentum by the daily volume
