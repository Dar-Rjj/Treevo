```txt
Volume-Weighted Adjusted Momentum
├── Daily Open-Close Spread
│   └── Spread = Open - Close
├── Momentum
│   ├── Lookback Period (e.g., 10 days)
│   └── Momentum = Current Close - Average(Close, Lookback)
├── Adjusted Momentum
│   └── Adjusted Momentum = Momentum / |Spread|
└── Volume-Weighted Adjusted Momentum
    └── Weighted Momentum = Adjusted Momentum * Volume
```
