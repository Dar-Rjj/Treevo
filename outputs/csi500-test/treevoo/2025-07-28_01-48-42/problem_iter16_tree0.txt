    - Formula: (Close_t - SMA(Close, 200)) / SMA(Close, 200)

  - Sub-Thought: Measure Volume Change
    - Formula: (Volume_t - Volume_(t-1)) / Volume_(t-1)
  - Sub-Thought: Calculate Amount Flow
    - Formula: (Amount_t - Amount_(t-1)) / Amount_(t-1)

  - Sub-Thought: Price to Volume Ratio
    - Formula: (Close_t - Close_(t-1)) / (Volume_t - Volume_(t-1))

    - Formula: Sum(Close * Volume) / Sum(Volume)
