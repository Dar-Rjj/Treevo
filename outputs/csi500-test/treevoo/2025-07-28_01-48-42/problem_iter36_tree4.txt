Alpha Mining Thought Tree
- Basic Alpha Factors
  - Price Momentum
    - Calculate the return from t-1 to t
    - Calculate the return from t-5 to t
    - Calculate the return from t-20 to t
  - Volume Momentum
    - Calculate the change in volume from t-1 to t
    - Calculate the change in volume from t-5 to t
    - Calculate the change in volume from t-20 to t
  - High-Low Range
    - Calculate the high-low range for day t
    - Calculate the average high-low range over t-5 days
    - Calculate the average high-low range over t-20 days
- Volatility-Based Alpha Factors
  - Price Volatility
    - Calculate the standard deviation of daily returns over t-5 days
    - Calculate the standard deviation of daily returns over t-20 days
  - Volume Volatility
    - Calculate the standard deviation of daily volume changes over t-5 days
    - Calculate the standard deviation of daily volume changes over t-20 days
- Technical Indicators
  - Moving Averages
    - Calculate the 5-day moving average of closing prices
    - Calculate the 20-day moving average of closing prices
    - Compare the 5-day moving average to the 20-day moving average
  - Exponential Moving Averages
    - Calculate the 5-day exponential moving average of closing prices
    - Calculate the 20-day exponential moving average of closing prices
    - Compare the 5-day exponential moving average to the 20-day exponential moving average
- Relative Strength Indicators
  - Relative Price Strength
    - Calculate the relative strength of the stock compared to a benchmark index over t-5 days
    - Calculate the relative strength of the stock compared to a benchmark index over t-20 days
  - Relative Volume Strength
    - Calculate the relative strength of the stock's volume compared to the average market volume over t-5 days
    - Calculate the relative strength of the stock's volume compared to the average market volume over t-20 days
- Trend and Reversal Indicators
  - Trend Continuation
    - Calculate the number of consecutive days the price has been increasing
    - Calculate the number of consecutive days the price has been decreasing
  - Reversal Patterns
    - Identify hammer or hanging man patterns in the last 5 days
    - Identify bullish or bearish engulfing patterns in the last 5 days
- Market Sentiment Indicators
  - Money Flow Index (MFI)
    - Calculate the 5-day MFI
    - Calculate the 14-day MFI
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
    - Calculate the 5-day OBV
    - Calculate the 20-day OBV
- Custom Alpha Factors
  - Custom Momentum
    - Combine price and volume momentum to create a custom momentum score
  - Custom Volatility
    - Combine price and volume volatility to create a custom volatility score
  - Custom Trend
    - Combine trend continuation and reversal indicators to create a custom trend score
- Interaction and Hybrid Alpha Factors
  - Combine Price Momentum and Volume Momentum
    - Multiply the 5-day price momentum by the 5-day volume momentum
  - Combine Moving Averages and Relative Strength
    - Add the 5-day moving average difference to the 5-day relative price strength
  - Combine Volatility and Custom Momentum
    - Subtract the 20-day price volatility from the custom momentum score
