Alpha Mining Thought Tree
- Calculate Momentum Indicators
  - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
    - SMA(5): 5-day simple moving average of close prices
    - SMA(20): 20-day simple moving average of close prices
  - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
    - EMA(5): 5-day exponential moving average of close prices
    - EMA(20): 20-day exponential moving average of close prices
  - Price Change
    - Daily Return: (close price - previous day's close price) / previous day's close price
    - Weekly Return: (current week's close - previous week's close) / previous week's close
- Assess Volatility Indicators
  - True Range: max[(high - low), abs(high - close(t-1)), abs(low - close(t-1))]
  - Average True Range (ATR): 14-day simple moving average of the true range
  - Historical Volatility: standard deviation of daily returns over the past N days
- Evaluate Volume Factors
  - Volume Difference: current day volume - previous day volume
  - Volume Over SMA: current day volume / 20-day simple moving average of volume
  - Volume Relative to Close: (close - open) / (volume * close)
- Analyze Trend Reversal and Breakout Indicators
  - Bollinger Bands
    - Upper Band: SMA(20) + 2 * Standard Deviation of Close(20)
    - Lower Band: SMA(20) - 2 * Standard Deviation of Close(20)
    - %B Indicator: (close - Lower Band) / (Upper Band - Lower Band)
  - Donchian Channels
    - Upper Channel: highest high over the last 20 days
    - Lower Channel: lowest low over the last 20 days
- Identify Market Sentiment and Support/Resistance Levels
  - Pivot Points
    - Pivot Point: (High + Low + Close) / 3
    - R1, S1, R2, S2, R3, S3 resistance and support levels using pivot point formulas
  - Accumulation/Distribution Line
    - A/D Line: cumulative sum of (close - previous close) / (high - low) * volume
- Incorporate Advanced Pattern Recognition
  - Inside Bars: high of current bar < high of previous bar and low of current bar > low of previous bar
  - Outside Bars: high of current bar > high of previous bar and low of current bar < low of previous bar
  - Engulfing Patterns: bullish or bearish engulfing patterns by comparing current and previous candlestick bodies
  - Doji Candles: small real body with equal length upper and lower shadows
- Explore Additional Technical Indicators
  - Relative Strength Index (RSI)
    - RSI(14): 14-day relative strength index
  - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
    - MACD Line: 12-day EMA of close - 26-day EMA of close
    - Signal Line: 9-day EMA of the MACD line
    - MACD Histogram: MACD line - Signal Line
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
    - OBV: cumulative sum of volume adjusted for price direction
