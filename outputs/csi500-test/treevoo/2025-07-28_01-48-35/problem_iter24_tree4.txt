```txt
Volume-Weighted Intraday Momentum and Composite Price Trends
├── Volume-Weighted Intraday Return
│   └── (High - Low) / Low * Volume
├── Short-Term Price Change
│   └── Close[t] - Close[t-5]
├── High-Low Spread
│   └── High - Low
├── Daily Return
│   └── (Close - Open) / Open
├── Momentum Indicators
│   ├── Short-term: (Close[t] - Close[t-7]) / Close[t-7]
│   ├── Medium-term: (Close[t] - Close[t-21]) / Close[t-21]
│   └── Long-term: (Close[t] - Close[t-63]) / Close[t-63]
├── True Range
│   └── max(High - Low, abs(High - Close[t-1]), abs(Low - Close[t-1]))
├── Relative Strength
│   └── Close[t] / Close[t-21]
├── Money Flow
│   ├── Money Flow Multiplier: (Close - (High + Low) / 2) / (High - Low)
│   ├── Money Flow Volume: Money Flow Multiplier * Volume
│   └── Money Flow Index: sum(Money Flow Volume for 14 days) / sum(Volume for 14 days)
├── Trend Direction
│   ├── Positive Directional Movement (+DM): High[t] - High[t-1] if High[t] > High[t-1] and High[t] - High[t-1] > Low[t-1] - Low[t] else 0
│   ├── Negative Directional Movement (-DM): Low[t-1] - Low[t] if Low[t-1] > Low[t] and Low[t-1] - Low[t] > High[t] - High[t-1] else 0
│   └── Directional Indicator (DI): SMA(+DM or -DM, 14) / ATR
├── Volume Ratio
│   └── Volume[t] / SMA(Volume, 21)
├── Weighted Average of Momentum Indicators
│   └── (Short-term * 0.3 + Medium-term * 0.5 + Long-term * 0.2)
├── Price-Volatility Ratio
│   └── (Close[t] - Close[t-21]) / ATR
└── Final Alpha Factor
    └── (Short-Term Price Change + High-Low Spread + Daily Return + Weighted Average of Momentum Indicators + Relative Strength + Money Flow Index + Directional Indicator + Price-Volatility Ratio) / 8 * Volume Ratio
```
