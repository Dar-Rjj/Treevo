```txt
Volume Adjusted Price Momentum (VAPM)
├── Calculate 5-day Price Change
│   ├── Subtract Close price at t-5 from Close price at t
└── Adjust for Volume Activity
    ├── Calculate 5-day Volume Change
    │   ├── Subtract Volume at t-5 from Volume at t
    ├── Volume Trend Indicator
    │   ├── If 5-day Volume Change > 0
    │   │   └── Assign +1
    │   └── Else
    │       └── Assign -1
    ├── Final VAPM Factor
    │   ├── Multiply 5-day Price Change by Volume Trend Indicator
    │   └── Divide by Average Volume over 20 days (t-20 to t-1)
```
