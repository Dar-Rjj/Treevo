```txt
[Factor Construction]
- [Basic Factors]
  - [Price Momentum]
    - Calculate the percentage change in close price over the last 5 days.
  - [Volume Surge]
    - Measure the ratio of current day's volume to the average volume over the past 20 days.
  - [Volatility]
    - Calculate the standard deviation of daily log returns over the past 20 days.
- [Complex Factors]
  - [Price and Volume Trend Alignment]
    - Combine the percentage change in close price over the last 5 days with the trend in daily volume, indicating a strong positive or negative movement when both are increasing or decreasing, respectively.
  - [Trading Range Breakout]
    - Identify instances where the close price breaks out of its 10-day trading range (highest high and lowest low) as a signal of significant market sentiment.
  - [Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP) Deviation]
    - Compute the VWAP for the past 10 days and calculate the difference between today's close and this VWAP, using this as an indicator of value and potential reversal points.
- [Pattern Recognition]
  - [Candlestick Patterns]
    - Detect common patterns like bullish and bearish engulfing, hammer, and doji formations using open, high, low, and close data over a short period.
  - [Breakaway Gaps]
    - Look for gaps at the opening of the trading day that aren't filled by the end of the day, suggesting a strong move and continuation pattern.
```
