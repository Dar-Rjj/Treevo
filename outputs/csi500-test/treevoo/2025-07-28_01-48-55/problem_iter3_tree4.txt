Price Momentum with Volume and Price Volatility Adjustment
├── Calculate Price Momentum

├── Adjust Momentum by Average Volume
│   ├── Calculate Average Volume over 20 Days
│   │   ├── Sum Volume from day t-19 to day t
│   │   └── Divide by 20
│   └── Adjust Momentum
│       ├── Multiply Price Momentum by Average Volume
│       └── Divide by Current Volume
└── Introduce Price Volatility Adjustment
    ├── Calculate Price Volatility
    │   ├── Calculate the standard deviation of Close prices from day t-20 to day t
    └── Final Adjustment
        ├── Multiply Adjusted Momentum by (1 / Price Volatility)
        └── This aims to penalize high volatility stocks, giving more weight to stable momentum signals
