```python
def heuristics_v2(df):
    # Intraday Reversal Calculation: (High - Low) - (Open - Close)
    intraday_reversal = (df['high'] - df['low']) - (df['open'] - df['close'])
    
    # Volume Change Calculation: Today’s Volume - Yesterday’s Volume
    volume_change = df['volume'] - df['volume'].shift(1)
    
    # Composite Factor: Intraday Reversal * Volume Change
    composite_factor = intraday_reversal * volume_change
    
    return composite_factor
```
