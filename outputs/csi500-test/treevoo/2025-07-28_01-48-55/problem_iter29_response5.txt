```python
import pandas as pd

def heuristics_v2(df):
    """
    This function generates an alpha factor by considering the momentum of the close price and the volume.
    The alpha factor is calculated as the ratio of the 10-day percentage change in close price to the 10-day
    percentage change in volume. This factor may help predict future stock returns by capturing the trend
    of price and volume movements.

    :param df: A pandas DataFrame with index (date) and columns (open, high, low, close, amount, volume).
    :return: A pandas Series indexed by (date) representing the factor values.
    """
    # Calculate 10-day percentage change in close price
    close_pct_change = df['close'].pct_change(10)
    
    # Calculate 10-day percentage change in volume
    volume_pct_change = df['volume'].pct_change(10)
    
    # Compute the alpha factor as the ratio of the two percentage changes
    alpha_factor = close_pct_change / volume_pct_change
    
    return alpha_factor
```
