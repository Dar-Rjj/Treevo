- Price Momentum Factors
  - Short-Term Momentum: (Close_t / Close_(t-5)) - 1
  - Medium-Term Momentum: (Close_t / Close_(t-20)) - 1
  - Long-Term Momentum: (Close_t / Close_(t-60)) - 1
- Volume Factors
  - Average Volume Change: (Volume_t / AVG(Volume_[t-5:t])) - 1
  - Volume Spike: IF(Volume_t > 2 * AVG(Volume_[t-5:t]), 1, 0)
- Price-Volatility Factors
  - Daily Volatility: sqrt(sum((Close_t - Open_t)^2) / N) for [t-N:t]
  - High-Low Spread: (High_t - Low_t) / Close_t
- Trend Strength Indicators
  - Directional Movement: SUM(IF(Close_t > Close_(t-1), 1, 0)) - SUM(IF(Close_t < Close_(t-1), 1, 0)) over [t-5:t]
- Relative Strength Factor
  - Relative Strength vs. Market: (Close_t / Close_Market_t) / (AVG(Close_[t-20:t]) / AVG(Close_Market_[t-20:t]))
- Liquidity Indicator
  - Turnover Ratio: Volume_t / Amount_t
- Technical Pattern Recognition
  - Bullish Engulfing: IF(Close_t > Open_t AND Open_t < Low_t AND Close_t > High_t AND Close_(t-1) < Open_(t-1), 1, 0)
  - Bearish Engulfing: IF(Close_t < Open_t AND Open_t > High_t AND Close_t < Low_t AND Close_(t-1) > Open_(t-1), 1, 0)
- Trade Activity
  - Large Trade Count: COUNT(TradeSize_t > Q3(TradeSize)) over [t-5:t], where Q3 is the third quartile of trade sizes in the last 5 days
- Market Sentiment
  - Sentiment Score: AVERAGE(SentimentSignal_t) over [t-5:t], assuming a sentiment score from an external source or model
- Economic Cycle Awareness
  - GDP Growth Rate Impact: IF(GDP_growth_rate_latest > 0, 1, -1)

- Macroeconomic Events

- Corporate Actions
