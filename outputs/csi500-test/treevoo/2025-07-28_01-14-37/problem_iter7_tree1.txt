Relative Strength and Momentum Indicator with Enhanced Volume-Weighted Price Movement
├── Calculate Momentum
│   └── Difference between current close and 10-day lagged close
├── Calculate 50-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
│   └── Apply exponential smoothing to close prices over last 50 days
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Price
│   ├── Multiply High, Low, and Close by Square Root of Volume

├── Calculate Total Weighted Volume
│   └── Sum the square root of volumes
└── Calculate Relative Strength

    └── Divide the result by (Volume-Weighted Price / Total Weighted Volume)
