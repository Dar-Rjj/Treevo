```txt
Integrated Volatility-Adjusted Momentum with Trend, Volume, and Cumulative Return Confirmation
├── Calculate High-to-Low Range
│   ├── High - Low
├── Compute Weighted Momentum
│   ├── (High + Low + 2 * Close) / 4 * Volume
│   └── (Close - Previous Close) / ((High + Low + 2 * Close) / 4 * Volume)
├── Adjust for Volatility
│   ├── Weighted Momentum / High-to-Low Range
├── Determine Trend Direction
│   ├── Short-Term MA (e.g., 5-day) of Close
│   ├── Long-Term MA (e.g., 20-day) of Close
│   └── +1 if Short-Term > Long-Term, -1 otherwise
├── Confirm with Volume
│   ├── Volume Change: Current Volume - Previous Volume
├── Calculate Daily Return
│   ├── (Close - Lagged Close) / Lagged Close
├── Calculate Cumulative Return
│   ├── Sum of Daily Returns over a specified window (e.g., 5 days)
├── Adjust by Average Volume
│   ├── Sum of Volumes over the same window / Window size
│   └── Adjust Cumulative Return * Average Volume
├── Adjust by Price Volatility
│   ├── Standard Deviation of Close Prices over the same window
│   └── Final Adjustment: (Cumulative Return / Standard Deviation) + 1
├── Calculate Intraday Volatility
│   ├── Average High-Low Range over n Days
├── Calculate Weighted Momentum
│   ├── Log Return: log(Current Close / Close m Days Ago)
│   ├── Sum of Weighted Log Returns
│   │   ├── Apply Exponential Weights to Log Returns
│   │   └── Sum the Weighted Log Returns
│   └── Adjust by Intraday Volatility: Sum of Weighted Log Returns / Intraday Volatility
└── Final Factor
    ├── (Adjusted Volatility-Adjusted Momentum * Volume Change * Trend Direction) + (Adjusted Cumulative Return * Trend Direction)
```
