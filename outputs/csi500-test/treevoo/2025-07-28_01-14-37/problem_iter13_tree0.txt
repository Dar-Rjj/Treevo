```txt
Hybrid Volatility-Adjusted Momentum with Volume and Trend Confirmation
├── Calculate High-to-Low Range
│   ├── High price - Low price
├── Calculate Close Price Change
│   ├── Current Close - Previous Close
├── Compute Weighted Momentum
│   ├── (High + Low + Close) / 3 * Volume
│   └── Close Price Change / ((High + Low + Close) / 3 * Volume)
├── Adjust for Volatility
│   ├── Weighted Momentum / High-to-Low Range
├── Determine Trend Direction
│   ├── Short-Term Moving Average (e.g., 5-day) of Close
│   ├── Long-Term Moving Average (e.g., 20-day) of Close
│   └── +1 if Short-Term > Long-Term, -1 otherwise
└── Confirm with Volume
    ├── Volume Change: Current Volume - Previous Volume
    └── Final Factor = (Adjusted Volatility-Adjusted Momentum * Volume Change * Trend Direction)
```
