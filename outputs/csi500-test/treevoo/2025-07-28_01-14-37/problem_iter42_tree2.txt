Thought: Explore Price Patterns
- Sub-thought: Calculate Moving Averages
  - Sub-thought: Simple Moving Average (SMA) over 5, 10, and 20 days

- Sub-thought: Evaluate Volume Changes
  - Sub-thought: Volume Delta (volume - prev_volume)
  - Sub-thought: Volume Ratio (volume / prev_volume)

Thought: Analyze Market Sentiment
- Sub-thought: Calculate Accumulation/Distribution Line (A/D Line)
  - Sub-thought: Money Flow Multiplier ([(close - low) - (high - close)] / (high - low))
  - Sub-thought: Money Flow Volume (money_flow_multiplier * volume)
  - Sub-thought: Accumulation/Distribution Line (cumulative sum of money_flow_volume)
- Sub-thought: Determine On-Balance Volume (OBV)
  - Sub-thought: OBV Calculation (if close > prev_close, OBV += volume; if close < prev_close, OBV -= volume)

  - Sub-thought: 3-day EMA of A/D Line
  - Sub-thought: 10-day EMA of A/D Line
  - Sub-thought: Chaikin Oscillator (3-day EMA - 10-day EMA)

Thought: Measure Momentum
- Sub-thought: Calculate Relative Strength Index (RSI)

  - Sub-thought: %K (100 * (close - lowest_low) / (highest_high - lowest_low))
  - Sub-thought: %D (3-period simple moving average of %K)
- Sub-thought: Calculate Rate of Change (ROC)
  - Sub-thought: ROC (100 * (close - close_n_days_ago) / close_n_days_ago)

Thought: Investigate Trading Volume
- Sub-thought: Calculate Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)

  - Sub-thought: Volume Spike (if volume > 2 * average_volume, 1; else 0)
- Sub-thought: Determine Volume-Price Trend (VPT)
  - Sub-thought: VPT (VPT_prev + (current_volume * (close - prev_close) / prev_close))

Thought: Assess Market Dynamics
- Sub-thought: Calculate Williams %R
  - Sub-thought: Williams %R (-100 * (highest_high - close) / (highest_high - lowest_low))
- Sub-thought: Determine Money Flow Index (MFI)
  - Sub-thought: Positive and Negative Money Flow (positive if typical_price > prev_typical_price, negative otherwise)
  - Sub-thought: MFI (100 - (100 / (1 + (positive_money_flow / negative_money_flow))))
- Sub-thought: Calculate Balance of Power (BOP)
  - Sub-thought: BOP ((close - open) / (high - low))
