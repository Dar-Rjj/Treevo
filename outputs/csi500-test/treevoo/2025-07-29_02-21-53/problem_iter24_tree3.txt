```txt
Performing Alpha Mining
- Generate Novel and Interpretable Alpha Factors
  - Trend Analysis
    - Calculate Moving Averages
      - Simple Moving Average (SMA) for Close Prices (window sizes: 5, 10, 20, 50)
      - Exponential Moving Average (EMA) for Close Prices (window sizes: 5, 10, 20, 50)
      - Compare SMA and EMA Crossovers
        - Short-term EMA crossing above Long-term EMA (Golden Cross)
        - Short-term EMA crossing below Long-term EMA (Death Cross)
      - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
        - MACD Line (12-day EMA - 26-day EMA)
        - Signal Line (9-day EMA of MACD Line)
        - MACD Histogram (Difference between MACD Line and Signal Line)
  - Momentum Indicators
    - Rate of Change (ROC) for Close Prices (window sizes: 5, 10, 20, 50)
    - Relative Strength Index (RSI) for Close Prices (window size: 14)
      - Bullish RSI (RSI < 30)
      - Bearish RSI (RSI > 70)
    - Stochastic Oscillator (K, D, and Slow K, Slow D)
      - %K (14-period)
      - %D (3-period moving average of %K)
      - Overbought Condition (K > 80 or D > 80)
      - Oversold Condition (K < 20 or D < 20)
  - Volume Analysis
    - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
      - Cumulative Volume Based on Price Movement
    - Money Flow Index (MFI)
      - Typical Price (Average of High, Low, and Close Prices)
      - Positive and Negative Money Flow
      - MFI Calculation (14-period)
  - Volatility Indicators
    - Average True Range (ATR) (14-period)
    - Bollinger Bands
      - Middle Band (20-day SMA of Close Prices)
      - Upper Band (Middle Band + 2 * Standard Deviation)
      - Lower Band (Middle Band - 2 * Standard Deviation)
  - Market Sentiment Indicators
    - Accumulation/Distribution Line (A/D Line)
      - Cumulative Flow of Buying and Selling Pressure
    - Chaikin Oscillator
      - Difference between 3-day and 10-day EMA of A/D Line
  - Pattern Recognition
    - Candlestick Patterns
      - Bullish Reversal Patterns (Hammer, Inverted Hammer, Bullish Engulfing, Morning Star)
      - Bearish Reversal Patterns (Hanging Man, Shooting Star, Bearish Engulfing, Evening Star)
    - Price Patterns
      - Head and Shoulders
      - Double Top/Bottom
      - Triangle (Ascending, Descending, Symmetrical)
  - Custom Indicators
    - Custom Combination of Indicators
      - Combine SMA, EMA, MACD, and RSI into a Composite Score
    - Custom Thresholds
      - Define Custom Thresholds for Indicator Values to Generate Buy/Sell Signals
```
