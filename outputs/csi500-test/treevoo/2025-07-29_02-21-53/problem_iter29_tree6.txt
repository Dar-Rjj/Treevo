    - High to Low
    - Day-to-Day Close

    - Moving Averages (Short and Long)

      - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)

  - Volume Trends
    - Average Volume Over Periods
    - Volume Changes
    - Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
  - Amount Analysis
    - Total Amount Traded
    - Amount Changes
    - Accumulation/Distribution Line
- Combine Price and Volume
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
  - Money Flow Index (MFI) without normalization
  - Volume-Price Trend (VPT)
- Detect Market Sentiment
  - High-Low Spread
  - Volatility Measures
    - Bollinger Bands Width
    - Average True Range (ATR)
    - Keltner Channels
- Evaluate Technical Indicators
  - Stochastic Oscillator
  - MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
  - Parabolic SAR
- Consider Order Book Imbalance
  - Bid-Ask Spread
  - Volume at Price Levels
  - Order Imbalance Ratio
