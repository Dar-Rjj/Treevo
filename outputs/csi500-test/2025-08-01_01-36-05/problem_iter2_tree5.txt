```txt
Intraday Price Ratio with Volume-Weighted Average
├── Calculate Intraday Price Ratio
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
│       ├── Compute High / Low
│       └── Subtract 1 to get the percentage change
├── Adjust by Volume-Weighted Average
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Compute Rolling Sum of Volume over 5 days
│       ├── If today is t, use volume from (t-4) to (t)
│   ├── Compute Rolling Sum of (Price * Volume) over 5 days
│       ├── If today is t, use (price * volume) from (t-4) to (t)
│   └── Divide Rolling Sum of (Price * Volume) by Rolling Sum of Volume
└── Multiply Intraday Price Ratio by Volume-Weighted Average
    └── (High / Low - 1) * (Rolling Sum of (Price * Volume) / Rolling Sum of Volume)
```
