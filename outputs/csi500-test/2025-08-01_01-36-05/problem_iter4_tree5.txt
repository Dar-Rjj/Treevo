Intraday Price Ratio with Volume-Weighted Average and Momentum
├── Calculate Intraday Price Ratio
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
│       ├── Compute (High / Low) - 1 to get the percentage change
├── Adjust by Volume-Weighted Average
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Compute Rolling Sum of Volume over 5 days
│       ├── If today is t, use volume from (t-4) to (t)
│   ├── Compute Rolling Sum of (Close * Volume) over 5 days
│       ├── If today is t, use (close * volume) from (t-4) to (t)
│   └── Divide Rolling Sum of (Close * Volume) by Rolling Sum of Volume
├── Incorporate Price Momentum
│   ├── Compute Simple Moving Average of Close Price (SMA)
│   │   ├── Use Close price for the past 14 days
│   └── Subtract SMA from Current Close Price to get Momentum
└── Combine Intraday Price Ratio, Volume-Weighted Average, and Momentum
    └── (High / Low - 1) * (Rolling Sum of (Close * Volume) / Rolling Sum of Volume) + Momentum
