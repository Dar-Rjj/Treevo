```txt
Relative Strength Indicator (RSI) Based on Volume-Weighted Price
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Average Price
│   ├── Multiply Close price with Volume
│   └── Sum the products and divide by total volume
├── Compute Daily Change in Volume-Weighted Price
│   ├── Use Today's Volume-Weighted Price
│   └── Subtract Yesterday's Volume-Weighted Price
├── Separate Positive and Negative Changes
│   ├── Identify Positive Changes
│   └── Identify Negative Changes
├── Compute 14-Day Averages
│   ├── Sum 14 Days of Positive Changes and divide by 14
│   └── Sum 14 Days of Negative Changes and divide by 14
└── Calculate RSI
    ├── Compute Relative Strength: 14-Day Average of Positive Changes / 14-Day Average of Negative Changes
    └── Apply RSI Formula: 100 - (100 / (1 + Relative Strength))
```
