```txt
Volume-Averaged High-Low Spread to Close Ratio Factor
├── Calculate High-Low Spread
│   ├── Subtract Low from High price
└── Compute Volume-Weighted Close Price
    ├── Sum of (Close price * Volume)
    └── Total Volume
└── Compute Adjusted Volume-Weighted Close Price
    ├── If Volume-Weighted Close Price is 0, use Close price
└── Compute Ratio
    ├── Divide High-Low Spread by Adjusted Volume-Weighted Close Price
    └── Output: Volume-Averaged High-Low Spread to Close Ratio
```
