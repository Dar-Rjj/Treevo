Volume-Weighted Intraday and Price Momentum Synthesis with Adjusted Volatility
├── Intraday Return
│   ├── High - Low
│   └── (High - Low) / Close
├── Intraday Volatility
│   ├── |High - Low|
│   └── Sum(|High - Low|) over 20 days
├── Intraday Momentum
│   ├── Intraday Return * Volume
│   └── Sum(Intraday Return * Volume) over 5 days
├── Volume-Weighted Intraday Reversal
│   ├── -(Intraday Return * Volume)
│   └── Sum(-(Intraday Return * Volume)) over 10 days
├── Daily Returns
│   ├── Use Close price of current day
│   └── Use Close price of previous day
├── Long-Term Volume-Weighted Average Return (Momentum Component)
│   ├── Sum of (Daily Returns * Volume) for the last 100 days
│   └── Divide by the total volume over the 100 days
├── Short-Term Volume-Weighted Average Return (Reversal Component)
│   ├── Sum of (Daily Returns * Volume) for the last 5 days
│   └── Divide by the total volume over the 5 days
├── Volume Adjusted Volatility
│   ├── Standard Deviation of (High - Low) over n days
│   └── Standard Deviation / Volume
├── Combined Factor
    ├── (Intraday Momentum + Volume-Weighted Intraday Reversal)
    ├── Multiplied by (Long-Term Volume-Weighted Average Return - Short-Term Volume-Weighted Average Return)
    └── Adjusted by Volume Adjusted Volatility
