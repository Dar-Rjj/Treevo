```txt
Volume-Adjusted Composite Alpha Factor with Trend and Momentum
├── Calculate 50-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Closing Prices
│   ├── Output: 50-day SMA
└── Generate Short-Term Trend Signal
    ├── 1 if the current close is above the 50-day SMA, 0 otherwise
    └── Output: Short-Term Trend Signal

├── Calculate 200-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Closing Prices
│   ├── Output: 200-day SMA
└── Generate Long-Term Trend Signal
    ├── 1 if the current close is above the 200-day SMA, 0 otherwise
    └── Output: Long-Term Trend Signal

├── Calculate High-Low Spread
│   ├── Subtract Low from High price
│   └── Output: High-Low Spread

├── Calculate Volume-Average Price
│   ├── Sum of (Close price * Volume)
│   └── Total Volume
│   └── Divide Sum by Total Volume
│   └── Output: Volume-Average Price

├── Compute Ratio
│   ├── Divide High-Low Spread by Volume-Average Price
│   └── Output: Volume-Averaged High-Low Spread Ratio

├── Calculate Momentum Score
│   ├── Difference between today’s high and the previous day’s high
│   ├── Difference between today’s low and the previous day’s low
│   ├── Sum the differences
│   └── Output: Momentum Score

├── Calculate Daily Volume Percentage Change
│   ├── Calculate the percentage change in volume from the previous day
│   └── Output: Daily Volume Percentage Change

├── Multiply Return by Volume
│   ├── Calculate the return (close to close)
│   ├── Multiply the return by the volume traded
│   └── Output: Market Participant Intensity

├── Combine Factors into a Composite Alpha Factor
│   ├── Weighted sum of:
│   │   ├── Short-Term Trend Signal
│   │   ├── Long-Term Trend Signal
│   │   ├── Momentum Score
│   │   ├── Market Participant Intensity
│   └── Adjust the final alpha factor using Volume-Averaged High-Low Spread Ratio
│   └── Output: Advanced Composite Alpha Factor
```
