Volume-Weighted Intraday Momentum with Breakout, Return Adjustments, and Price Volatility
├── Calculate Daily Returns

│   ├── Current Day Volume

    └── Sum the Volume-Weighted Breakout Ratios
├── Calculate Intraday Return
    └── (High price - Low price) / Open price
├── Adjust for Volume
    └── (Current Volume - 10-Day Moving Average of Volume) * Intraday Return
├── Incorporate Price Volatility
    ├── Calculate Standard Deviation of Close price over 10 days
    ├── If Standard Deviation is high, multiply by 1.5; Else, multiply by 0.7
├── Generate Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Combine Volume-Weighted Moving Average, Aggregate Breakout, and Volume-Adjusted Intraday Return
    └── Apply Moving Average (Define Window Size)
└── Adjust Final Alpha Factor for Price Volatility
    └── Multiply Final Alpha Factor by Volatility Adjustment Factor
