Volume-Adjusted Smoothed Momentum (VASM)
├── Calculate Raw Price Momentum
│   ├── Use Close price
│   │   ├── Get the last 14 days' closing prices
│   │   └── Calculate the difference between today's close and the close 14 days ago
│   └── Smooth the Difference
│       ├── Apply a 14-day Simple Moving Average to the differences
│       └── Output: smoothed momentum
├── Incorporate Volume Dynamics
│   ├── Get the last 14 days' volume
│   └── Calculate Volume Variability
│       ├── Find the standard deviation of the volumes over the last 14 days
│       └── Output: volume variability
└── Combine Momentum and Volume
    ├── Multiply smoothed momentum by volume variability
    ├── Adjust for Volume Trend
    │   ├── Calculate the 14-day Simple Moving Average of volume
    │   └── Divide the volume variability by the 14-day SMA of volume
    └── Output: VASM
        ├── This factor combines price momentum with dynamic volume measures, 
        └── enhancing the predictive power for future returns by considering both price and volume dynamics.
