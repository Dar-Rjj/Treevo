```txt
Weighted Cumulative Directional Movement Adjusted (WCDMA)
├── Calculate Upward and Downward Movements
│   ├── Compute High Minus Prior Close
│   └── Compute Low Minus Prior Close
├── Determine Net Directional Movement
│   ├── If Upward > Downward, Assign Value = 1
│   ├── If Upward < Downward, Assign Value = -1
│   └── Otherwise, Assign Value = 0
├── Weight by Volume and Return
│   ├── Multiply Net Directional Movement by Volume
│   ├── Multiply Result by Absolute Daily Return
└── Calculate WCDMA
    ├── Sum Weighted Net Directional Movements Over a Period
    └── Use Adjusted Cumulative Sum as Factor
```
