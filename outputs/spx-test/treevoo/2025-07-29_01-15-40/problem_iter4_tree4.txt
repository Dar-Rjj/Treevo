Cumulative Directional Movement Indicator (CDMI)
├── Calculate Net Movements
│   ├── Compute High Minus Prior High as Upward
│   ├── Compute Low Minus Prior Low as Downward
│   └── If Upward > Downward, Use Upward; Otherwise, Use Downward
└── Calculate CDMI
    └── Sum Net Movements Over a Period
