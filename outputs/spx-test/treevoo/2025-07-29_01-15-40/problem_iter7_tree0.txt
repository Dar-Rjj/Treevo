```txt
Cumulative Directional Movement Adjusted (CDMA)
├── Calculate Net Directional Movements
│   ├── Compute High Minus Prior Close (Upward Movement)
│   ├── Compute Low Minus Prior Close (Downward Movement)
│   └── Assign Value Based on Comparison
│       ├── If Upward > Downward, Assign 1
│       ├── If Upward < Downward, Assign -1
│       └── Otherwise, Assign 0
└── Calculate CDMA
    ├── Sum Net Directional Movements Over a Period
    └── Multiply by Volume
```
