Thought: Crossover of Price and Volume Shock-Adjusted Momentum with Intraday Volatility and Range and Momentum, VWAP, ATR, and Moving Averages

├── Daily Price Range
│   ├── High - Low
├── N-Day Avg Price Range
│   ├── Sum of Daily Price Ranges over N days
│   └── Divide by N
├── N-Day RSI
│   ├── Upward Change: If Close > Previous Close, then Close - Previous Close
│   └── Downward Change: If Close < Previous Close, then Previous Close - Close
│   ├── Avg Up: Sum Upward Changes over N days / N
│   └── Avg Down: Sum Downward Changes over N days / N
│   ├── RS = Avg Up / |Avg Down|
│   └── RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
├── Intraday Return
│   ├── Close - Open
├── Intraday Volatility
│   ├── High - Low
├── Adjusted Intraday Return
    ├── Intraday Return / Intraday Volatility
├── Volume and Amount Shock
    ├── Volume Change: Current Volume - Previous Volume
    └── Amount Change: Current Amount - Previous Amount
├── N-Day Avg Volume
    ├── Sum of Volumes over N days
    └── Divide by N
├── Volume Trend
    ├── 1 if Today's Volume > N-Day Avg Volume
    └── -1 if Today's Volume < N-Day Avg Volume
├── Volume-Weighted Intraday Return
    ├── Volume * Adjusted Intraday Return
├── Momentum
    ├── 5-Day MA of Intraday Returns
    ├── 20-Day MA of Intraday Returns
    └── 5-Day MA - 20-Day MA
├── Adjusted Factors
    ├── N-Day Avg Price Range * Volume Shock
    ├── N-Day RSI * Volume Shock
    ├── Intraday Range * Volume Shock
    ├── Intraday Range * Amount Shock
├── Combined Adjusted Factors
    ├── Sum of all Adjusted Factors
    ├── Add Volume-Weighted Intraday Return
    └── Multiply by Volume Trend
├── Calculate the difference between today's close and the previous day's close (Momentum)
    ├── Subtract yesterday's close from today's close
├── Calculate the rate of change over N days (RateOfChange)
    ├── Divide the difference between today's close and the close N days ago by the close N days ago
├── Determine the average gain or loss over N days (AverageGainLoss)
    ├── Sum the gains or losses over the last N days and divide by N
├── Calculate the typical price (TypicalPrice)
    ├── Add high, low, and close prices and divide by 3
├── Calculate the cumulative volume weighted typical price (CumulativeVWAP)
    ├── Multiply the typical price by the volume for each day and sum these values
├── Calculate the cumulative volume (CumulativeVolume)
    ├── Sum the volume over the period of interest
├── Compute VWAP
    ├── Divide the cumulative volume weighted typical price by the cumulative volume
├── Calculate the True Range (TR)
    ├── Calculate the maximum of (high - low), (high - previous close), (previous close - low)
        ├── Find the highest value among the three differences
├── Calculate the Average True Range (ATR) over N days
    ├── Sum the true range values over the last N days and divide by N
├── Calculate the Simple Moving Average (SMA) over N days
    ├── Sum the closing prices over the last N days and divide by N
├── Calculate the Exponential Moving Average (EMA) over N days
    ├── Apply the EMA formula to the closing prices with a smoothing factor of 2 / (N + 1)
├── Integrate Momentum, RateOfChange, and ATR
    ├── Sum the unnormalized Momentum, RateOfChange, and ATR values
├── Compare the current price to the VWAP and the SMA
    ├── If the current price is above both, it signals a buy, and if below both, it signals a sell
├── Create a final composite alpha score
    ├── Combine the integrated Momentum, RateOfChange, and ATR with the VWAP and SMA signals
