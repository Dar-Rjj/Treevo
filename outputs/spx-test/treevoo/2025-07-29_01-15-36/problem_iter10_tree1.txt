Volume-Adjusted Momentum and Breakout with RSI Integration
├── Calculate Daily Price Range
│   ├── High price - Low price

│   └── Apply Exponential Weighting Factor

│   ├── Close price / Daily Price Range

│   └── Sum the Volume-Weighted Close-to-Range Momentum over the Window

│   ├── Upward Price Change: If today's close > yesterday's close, use today's close - yesterday's close
│   ├── Downward Price Change: If today's close < yesterday's close, use yesterday's close - today's close
│   ├── Exponential Moving Average of Upward Change
│   │   ├── Sum Upward Price Changes over N days
│   │   └── Apply Exponential Weighting Factor
│   ├── Exponential Moving Average of Downward Change
│   │   ├── Sum Downward Price Changes over N days
│   │   └── Apply Exponential Weighting Factor
│   ├── RS (Relative Strength): EMA(Upward Change) / |EMA(Downward Change)|
│   └── RSI: 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
├── Calculate Volume-Adjusted Momentum
│   ├── N-Day EMA of Price Range * Volume
│   ├── N-Day RSI * Volume
│   └── Combine: Volume-Adjusted EMA of Price Range + Volume-Adjusted RSI
└── Combine Volume-Adjusted Breakout and Momentum
    ├── Summarized Volume-Weighted Close-to-Range Momentum
