Volume Adjusted Momentum Breakout
├── Calculate High-Low Range

│   └── Use Low price
│       └── Compute difference between High and Low
├── Compute Average True Range
│   ├── Use High, Low, and Close prices from last n days
│   │   ├── Calculate True Range for each day
│   │   └── Sum up the True Ranges
│   └── Divide by n (number of days)
├── Determine Volume Trend
│   ├── Use Volume
│   │   ├── Sum volume over the last m days
│   │   └── Compare with sum of volume from the previous m days
│   └── Determine if volume is increasing or decreasing
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Use Close prices
│   │   ├── Sum close prices over the last p days
│   │   └── Divide by p to get average
│   └── Subtract the average close price from current close price
└── Combine Factors
    ├── Multiply High-Low Range by Average True Range
    ├── Adjust by Volume Trend (multiply by 1 if increasing, -1 if decreasing)
    └── Add Price Momentum to the result
