Price and Volume Shock-Adjusted Momentum with Intraday Volatility and Range
├── Daily Price Range
│   ├── High - Low
├── N-Day Avg Price Range
│   ├── Sum of Daily Price Ranges over N days / N
├── N-Day RSI
│   ├── Upward Change: If Close > Previous Close, then Close - Previous Close
│   └── Downward Change: If Close < Previous Close, then Previous Close - Close
│   ├── Avg Up: Sum Upward Changes over N days / N
│   ├── Avg Down: Sum Downward Changes over N days / N
│   ├── RS = Avg Up / |Avg Down|
│   └── RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
├── Intraday Return
│   ├── Close - Open
├── Intraday Volatility
│   ├── High - Low
├── Adjusted Intraday Return
    ├── Intraday Return / Intraday Volatility
├── Volume and Amount Shock
    ├── Volume Change: Current Volume - Previous Volume
    └── Amount Change: Current Amount - Previous Amount
├── N-Day Avg Volume
    ├── Sum of Volumes over N days / N
├── Volume Trend
    ├── 1 if Today's Volume > N-Day Avg Volume
    └── -1 if Today's Volume < N-Day Avg Volume
├── Volume-Weighted Intraday Return
    ├── Volume * Adjusted Intraday Return
├── Momentum
    ├── 5-Day MA of Intraday Returns
    ├── 20-Day MA of Intraday Returns
    └── 5-Day MA - 20-Day MA
├── Combined Adjusted Factors
    ├── Sum of (N-Day Avg Price Range * Volume Shock,
                N-Day RSI * Volume Shock,
                Intraday Range * Volume Shock,
                Intraday Range * Amount Shock)
    ├── Add Volume-Weighted Intraday Return
    └── Multiply by Volume Trend
