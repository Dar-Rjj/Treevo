```txt
Volume-Adjusted Momentum with Intraday Factors
├── Calculate Daily Price Range
│   ├── Subtract Low price from High price
├── Calculate N-Day Moving Average of Price Range
│   ├── Sum Daily Price Ranges over N days
│   └── Divide by N
├── Calculate Intraday Return
│   ├── (Close price - Open price) / Open price
├── Calculate Intraday Volatility
│   ├── High-Low Difference: High price - Low price
│   ├── Mean of Recent High-Low Differences
│   └── Standard Deviation of Recent High-Low Differences
├── Adjust Intraday Return by Intraday Volatility
│   ├── Intraday Return / Intraday Volatility
├── Determine Range-to-Range Ratio
│   ├── Current Day's High-Low Range / Previous Day's High-Low Range
├── Integrate Volume Sensitivity
│   ├── Volume Ratio: Current Day's Volume / Previous Day's Volume
│   └── Adjusted Range-to-Range Ratio: Range-to-Range Ratio * Volume Ratio
├── Combine Adjusted Intraday Return and Adjusted Range-to-Range Ratio
│   ├── Adjusted Intraday Return * Adjusted Range-to-Range Ratio
├── Detect Volume Shock
│   ├── Volume Change: Current Volume - Previous Volume
│   └── Identify Sudden Increase in Volume: If Volume Change > Threshold
├── Calculate N-Day Relative Strength Index (RSI)
│   ├── Upward and Downward Price Changes
│   │   ├── Upward: If today's close > yesterday's close, upward change = today's close - yesterday's close
│   │   └── Downward: If today's close < yesterday's close, downward change = yesterday's close - today's close
│   ├── Average Upward and Downward Changes
│   │   ├── Avg Upward: Sum Upward / N
│   │   └── Avg Downward: Sum Downward / N
│   ├── RS: Avg Upward / Abs(Avg Downward)
│   └── RSI: 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
├── Volume-Adjusted Momentum
    ├── (N-Day Moving Average of Price Range * Volume) + (N-Day RSI * Volume)
└── Final Factor Calculation
    ├── Volume-Adjusted Momentum * Combined Intraday Factors
    │   ├── High value indicates strong intraday momentum, breakout, and sudden volume increase
    │   └── Low value indicates weak intraday momentum or no significant volume change
```
