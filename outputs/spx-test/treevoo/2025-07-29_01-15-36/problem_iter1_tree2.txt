Volume-Weighted Momentum with Trading Range Indicator
├── Calculate Daily Return
│   ├── Use Close price (day t)
│   └── Use Close price (day t-1)
├── Volume-Weighted Moving Average of Returns
│   ├── Calculate N-day Moving Sum of Volume
│   │   ├── Use Volume
│   │   └── Sum over N days
│   └── Calculate N-day Sum of Volume-Weighted Returns
│       ├── Multiply each Daily Return by corresponding Volume
│       └── Sum over N days
├── Normalize by Volume Sum
│   ├── Divide Volume-Weighted Moving Sum by Volume Sum
│   └── Output: Volume-Weighted Moving Average (VWMA)
└── Incorporate Trading Range
    ├── Calculate True Range
    │   ├── Use High (day t)
    │   ├── Use Low (day t)
    │   ├── Use Close (day t-1)
    │   └── Compute max(High-Low, abs(High-Close(t-1)), abs(Low-Close(t-1)))
    ├── Calculate N-day Moving Average of True Range
    │   ├── Sum True Range over N days
    │   └── Divide by N
    ├── Standardize VWMA
    │   ├── Subtract VWMA from its Mean (over N days)
    │   └── Divide by its Standard Deviation (over N days)
    └── Combine Indicators
        ├── If VWMA is positive and True Range MA is above threshold
        │   └── Output: Positive Signal
        └── If VWMA is negative and True Range MA is below threshold
            └── Output: Negative Signal
