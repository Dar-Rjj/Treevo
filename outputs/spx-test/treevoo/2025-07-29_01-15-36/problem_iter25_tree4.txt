Volume-Weighted Momentum with Enhanced Intraday and Long-Term Factors
├── Calculate Adjusted Intraday Return
│   ├── (Close price - Open price) / Open price
│   └── Adjust by Intraday Volatility
│       ├── High-Low Difference: High price - Low price
│       ├── Mean of Recent High-Low Differences
│       └── Standard Deviation of Recent High-Low Differences
├── Determine Adjusted Range-to-Range Ratio
│   ├── Current Day's High-Low Range / Previous Day's High-Low Range
│   └── Adjust by Volume Ratio: Current Day's Volume / Previous Day's Volume
├── Detect Sudden Increase in Volume
│   ├── Volume Change: Current Volume - Previous Volume
│   └── Identify Sudden Increase: If Volume Change > Threshold
├── Calculate N-Day Relative Strength Index (RSI)
│   ├── Upward and Downward Price Changes
│   │   ├── Upward: If today's close > yesterday's close, upward change = today's close - yesterday's close
│   │   └── Downward: If today's close < yesterday's close, downward change = yesterday's close - today's close
│   ├── Average Upward and Downward Changes
│   │   ├── Avg Upward: Sum Upward / N
│   │   └── Avg Downward: Sum Downward / N
│   ├── RS: Avg Upward / Abs(Avg Downward)
│   └── RSI: 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
├── Calculate Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Close prices
│   ├── Use Last 20 Day's Close Prices
├── Calculate Momentum Factor
│   ├── Subtract Latest Close Price from EMA
├── Calculate On-Balance Volume (OBV)
│   ├── If Today's Close > Yesterday's Close: OBV += Today's Volume
│   ├── If Today's Close < Yesterday's Close: OBV -= Today's Volume
│   └── If Today's Close = Yesterday's Close: OBV = Yesterday's OBV
├── Combined Weighted Momentum
    ├── (N-Day RSI * Volume) + (Adjusted Intraday Return * Volume Ratio) + (Momentum Factor * Combined Volume & Price Volatility) + (OBV * Adjusted Range-to-Range Ratio)
    │   ├── Combine Volume and Price Volatility
    │   │   ├── Calculate Average Volume Over 20 Days
    │   │   └── Calculate Average High-Low Range Over 20 Days
└── Final Factor Calculation
    ├── Combined Weighted Momentum * Adjusted Intraday Return
    │   ├── High value indicates strong intraday momentum, breakout, and sudden volume increase
    │   └── Low value indicates weak intraday momentum or no significant volume change
