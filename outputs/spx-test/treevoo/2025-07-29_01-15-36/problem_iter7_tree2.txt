Volume-Adjusted Momentum with Enhanced Price Range and RSI Integration
├── Calculate Daily Price Range
│   ├── High price - Low price

│   └── Determine Downward Price Change

│   │   ├── Apply Exponential Weighting Factor to Upward Price Changes over N days
│   └── Calculate Exponential Moving Average of Downward Change
│       ├── Apply Exponential Weighting Factor to Downward Price Changes over N days
│   ├── Compute RS (Relative Strength)
│   │   ├── EMA of Upward Change / Absolute(EMA of Downward Change)
│   └── Calculate RSI
│       ├── 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
└── Calculate Volume-Adjusted Momentum
    ├── Volume * (N-Day EMA of Price Range + N-Day RSI)
