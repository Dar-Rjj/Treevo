```txt
Volume-Adjusted Intraday and Short-Term Momentum with Price Range Filter
├── Calculate Combined Returns
│   ├── Intraday Returns = High_t - Low_t
│   ├── Open-to-Close Returns = Close_t - Open_t
│   └── (Intraday Returns + Open-to-Close Returns)
├── Calculate Volume-Adjusted Short-Term Momentum
│   ├── Average Close = Average(Close prices of last 5 days)
│   ├── Relative Volume = Vol_t / Average(Vol of last 5 days)
│   └── Average Close * Relative Volume
├── Weight Combined Returns by Volume Trend
│   ├── Volume Trend = If(Vol_t > Vol_(t-1), 1, -1)
│   └── Combined Returns * Volume Trend
├── Integrate Volume-Adjusted Short-Term Momentum and Weighted Returns
│   └── (Volume-Adjusted Short-Term Momentum + Weighted Combined Returns)
└── Apply Price Range Filter
    ├── Daily Price Range = High_t - Low_t
    ├── AvgRange_20 = Sum(Daily Price Ranges of past 20 days) / 20
    └── If (Daily Price Range > 1.5 * AvgRange_20), set factor value to 0; otherwise, use the integrated momentum
```
