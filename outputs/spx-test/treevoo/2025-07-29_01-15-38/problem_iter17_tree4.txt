Synthesize Volume-Adjusted Short-Term Momentum with Price and High-Low Range Analysis
├── Calculate Short-Term Price Momentum

│   ├── Calculate Daily Price Range (High_t - Low_t)

│   ├── Calculate Daily Return (Close_t - Close_t-1) / Close_t-1
│   ├── Calculate Standard Deviation of Daily Returns over past 20 days
│   │   ├── Sum of squared daily returns over past 20 days
│   │   └── Divide by 20, then take square root (StdDev_DailyReturns_20)

│   ├── Subtract Low from High
│   └── Output the range
├── Calculate Short-Term High-Low Range Momentum
│   ├── Take the difference with the previous day's range
│   └── Sum over the past 5 days
├── Calculate Long-Term High-Low Range Momentum
│   ├── Take the difference with the previous day's range
│   └── Sum over the past 20 days
├── Calculate High-Low Range Momentum Oscillator
│   ├── Subtract Long-Term High-Low Range Momentum from Short-Term High-Low Range Momentum

│   │   ├── Use Day t Volume
│   │   └── Calculate Average of the past 10 days

│   ├── If current volume > previous 10-day average and <= 1.5 * previous 10-day average, assign weight 1.2
│   └── If current volume <= previous 10-day average, assign weight 1
├── Filter by Close Price Increase
│   ├── Check if current day's close is higher than the previous day's close
│   └── If true, apply the momentum oscillator value; if false, set to 0
└── Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Multiply Volume-Filtered Price Momentum by High-Low Range Momentum Oscillator
    ├── Multiply Result by Volume Weight
    └── Output the final factor value
