Volume-Adjusted Short-Term Momentum with Enhanced Price Range and Volume Filter
├── Calculate Volume-Adjusted Short-Term Momentum
│   ├── Short-Term Momentum = Average(Close prices of last 5 days)
│   └── Relative Volume = Vol_t / Average(Vol of last 5 days)
│       └── Adjusted Momentum = Short-Term Momentum * Relative Volume
└── Apply Enhanced Price Range and Volume Filter
    ├── Daily Price Range = High_t - Low_t
    ├── AvgRange_20 = Sum(Daily Price Ranges of past 20 days) / 20
    ├── If (Daily Price Range > 1.5 * AvgRange_20), set factor value to 0; otherwise, continue
    ├── Volume Ratio = Vol_t / Average(Vol of last 20 days)
    └── If (Volume Ratio < 0.8), set factor value to 0; otherwise, use the adjusted momentum
