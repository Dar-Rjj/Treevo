Weighted Intraday Momentum Oscillator (WIMO)
├── Calculate Simple Moving Average (SMA) of High Prices
│   ├── Use High price for the last 14 days
│   └── Compute SMA
├── Calculate Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Low Prices
│   ├── Use Low price for the last 14 days
│   └── Compute SMA
├── Calculate Momentum Oscillator
│   ├── Subtract SMA of Low Prices from SMA of High Prices
│   └── Divide by the absolute difference between SMA of High and Low Prices
├── Calculate Intraday Trading Volume Fluctuation
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Compute 5-day Rolling Standard Deviation of Volume
└── Weight the Momentum Oscillator with Volume Fluctuation
    ├── Use the Resulting Intraday Trading Volume Fluctuation
    └── Multiply Momentum Oscillator by Volume Fluctuation
