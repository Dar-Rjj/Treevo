```txt
Weighted Intraday Momentum Oscillator (WIMO)
├── Compute SMA of High Prices
│   └── Use High prices for the last 14 days
├── Compute SMA of Low Prices
│   └── Use Low prices for the last 14 days
├── Calculate Momentum Oscillator
│   └── Subtract SMA of Low from SMA of High, then divide by their absolute difference
├── Calculate 5-day Rolling Standard Deviation of Volume
└── Weight the Momentum Oscillator
    └── Multiply Momentum Oscillator by 5-day Rolling Standard Deviation of Volume
```
