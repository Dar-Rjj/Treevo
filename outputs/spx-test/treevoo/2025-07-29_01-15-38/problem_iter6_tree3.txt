```txt
Weighted Intraday Momentum and Volume Oscillator (WIMVO)
├── Compute EMA of High Prices
│   └── Use High prices for the last 20 days
├── Compute EMA of Low Prices
│   └── Use Low prices for the last 20 days
├── Calculate Intraday Momentum Oscillator
│   └── Subtract EMA of Low from EMA of High, then divide by their absolute difference
├── Calculate 10-day Rolling Standard Deviation of Volume
└── Weight the Intraday Momentum Oscillator
    └── Multiply Intraday Momentum Oscillator by 10-day Rolling Standard Deviation of Volume
```
