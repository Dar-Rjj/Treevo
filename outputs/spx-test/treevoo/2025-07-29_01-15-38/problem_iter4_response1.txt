```python
def heuristics_v2(df):
    # Calculate Short-Term Price Momentum
    short_term_momentum = df['close'].rolling(window=5).mean()
    
    # Calculate Average Volume of last 5 days
    avg_volume_5_days = df['volume'].rolling(window=5).mean()
    
    # Adjust for Volume Fluctuations
    adjusted_momentum = short_term_momentum * (df['volume'] / avg_volume_5_days)
    
    return adjusted_momentum
```
