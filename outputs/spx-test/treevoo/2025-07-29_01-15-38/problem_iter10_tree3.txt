├── Short-Term Adjusted Momentum

    │   ├── Current 10-day average volume
    │   └── Previous 10-day average volume
    │       ├── If current > 1.5 * previous, weight = 1.5
    │       ├── If current > previous, weight = 1.2
    │       └── Otherwise, weight = 1
    └── Daily Price Range

        ├── Low price on day t
        ├── 20-Day Average Price Range
        └── If (Price Range t > 1.5 * 20-Day Avg Range), set factor value to 0; otherwise, use (Oscillator * Volume Surge Weight)
