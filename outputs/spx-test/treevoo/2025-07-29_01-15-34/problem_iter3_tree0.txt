```txt
Dynamic Momentum Strength Index with Volume Trend Adjustment
├── Calculate Daily Returns
│   ├── Use Close price on day t and day (t-1)
│   └── Compute (Close price t / Close price (t-1)) - 1
├── Compute Moving Average of Returns
│   ├── Select a period for the moving average, e.g., 20 days
│   └── Calculate the simple moving average over the selected period
├── Calculate Volatility
│   ├── Select a volatility period, e.g., 20 days
│   └── Calculate the standard deviation of daily returns over the period
├── Determine Momentum Strength
│   ├── Divide the moving average of returns by the volatility
│   └── Interpret as a measure of risk-adjusted momentum
├── Apply Volume Trend Adjustment
    ├── Calculate Volume Trend
    │   ├── Compute the difference between volume on day t and volume on day (t-1)
    │   └── Calculate the 20-day moving average of the volume differences
    ├── Adjust the Momentum Strength
    │   ├── If Volume Trend > 0, multiply the Momentum Strength by (1 + Volume Trend / 100)
    │   └── If Volume Trend <= 0, multiply the Momentum Strength by (1 - Volume Trend / 100)
└── Smooth the Adjusted Momentum Strength
    ├── Apply a simple moving average to the adjusted momentum strength
    └── Choose a smoothing period, e.g., 5 days
```
