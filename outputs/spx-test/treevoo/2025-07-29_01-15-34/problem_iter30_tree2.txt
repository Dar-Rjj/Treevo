Alpha Mining Thought Tree
- Price-Based Factors
  - Moving Averages
    - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
      - SMA_5: 5-day simple moving average
      - SMA_10: 10-day simple moving average
      - SMA_20: 20-day simple moving average
    - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
      - EMA_5: 5-day exponential moving average
      - EMA_10: 10-day exponential moving average
      - EMA_20: 20-day exponential moving average
  - Momentum Indicators
    - ROC (Rate of Change)
      - ROC_5: 5-day rate of change
      - ROC_10: 10-day rate of change
      - ROC_20: 20-day rate of change
    - RSI (Relative Strength Index)
      - RSI_5: 5-day relative strength index
      - RSI_10: 10-day relative strength index
      - RSI_20: 20-day relative strength index
- Volume-Based Factors
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
    - OBV_Simple: Cumulative OBV
    - OBV_Trend: 5-day trend of OBV
  - Volume Change
    - Vol_Change_5: 5-day percentage change in volume
    - Vol_Change_10: 10-day percentage change in volume
    - Vol_Chance_20: 20-day percentage change in volume
- Combined Factors
  - Price-Volume Interaction
    - PV_Ratio: Daily closing price divided by daily volume
    - PV_Ratio_MA: 5-day moving average of the price-volume ratio
  - High-Low Range
    - High_Low_Range: High price minus low price
    - High_Low_Range_MA: 5-day moving average of the high-low range
- Custom Factors
  - Open-Close Spread
    - Open_Close_Spread: Difference between open and close prices
    - Open_Close_Spread_MA: 5-day moving average of the open-close spread
  - Close-Volume Correlation
    - Close_Vol_Correl: 5-day correlation between closing price and volume
    - Close_Vol_Correl_10: 10-day correlation between closing price and volume
    - Close_Vol_Correl_20: 20-day correlation between closing price and volume
