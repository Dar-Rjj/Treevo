Alpha Mining for Predicting Future Stock Returns
- Novel and Interpretable Alpha Factors
  - Price-based Factors
    - Moving Averages
      - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
        - SMA(5)
        - SMA(10)
        - SMA(20)
      - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
        - EMA(5)
        - EMA(10)
        - EMA(20)
    - Price Change
      - Day-to-Day Price Change
      - Week-to-Week Price Change
      - Month-to-Month Price Change
    - High-Low Range
      - True Range
        - Maximum of (High(t) - Low(t), |High(t) - Close(t-1)|, |Low(t) - Close(t-1)|)
  - Volume-based Factors
    - Volume Change
      - Day-to-Day Volume Change
      - Week-to-Week Volume Change
      - Month-to-Month Volume Change
    - Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
      - VWAP over a 5-day period
      - VWAP over a 10-day period
      - VWAP over a 20-day period
  - Momentum and Trend Factors
    - Rate of Change (ROC)
      - ROC(5)
      - ROC(10)
      - ROC(20)
    - Relative Strength Index (RSI)
      - RSI(14)
    - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
      - MACD(12, 26, 9)
  - Volatility Factors
    - Historical Volatility
      - HV(5)
      - HV(10)
      - HV(20)
    - Bollinger Bands
      - Middle Band: 20-day SMA
      - Upper Band: 20-day SMA + 2 * Standard Deviation
      - Lower Band: 20-day SMA - 2 * Standard Deviation
  - Pattern Recognition
    - Candlestick Patterns
      - Bullish Engulfing
      - Bearish Engulfing
      - Hammer
      - Doji
  - Combination Factors
    - Price and Volume Indicators
      - SMA(5) and Day-to-Day Volume Change
      - EMA(10) and VWAP(10)
    - Trend and Momentum Indicators
      - MACD and ROC(20)
      - RSI(14) and HV(10)
