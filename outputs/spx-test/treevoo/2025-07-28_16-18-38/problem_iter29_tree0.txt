      - EMA ratio: Close / EMA(Close)

    - Daily Volume Change: (Volume_t - Volume_t-1) / Volume_t-1
    - Volume Ratio: Current Volume / SMA(Volume)
    - Large Trade Indicator: If (Volume > 2 * SMA(Volume), 1, 0)
    - 5-day Moving Average of Volume
  - Construct Focused Composite Factors
    - Price-Volume Momentum: (Close - Open) * Volume

    - Enhanced Momentum Indicators

      - 21-day EMA of High-to-Low Range

      - Multiply by Intraday Movement

      - Volume-Weighted High-Low Difference: (High - Low) * Volume

      - Subtract SMA from Current Day's VWHL
      - Smoothed Raw Momentum: Apply Exponential Weighting
      - Divide by Combined Momentum Signals
    - Enhanced Components
      - Intraday Volatility: |Close - Open|
      - Volume Trend: Volume - 5-day MA
      - Combine Enhanced Components: Sum Intraday Volatility and Volume Trend
  - Final Alpha Factor
    - Adjusted Momentum * Combined Momentum
    - Add Result to Combined Enhanced Components
  - Evaluate Factor Performance
