Momentum-Based Alpha Factor Combining Price, Volume, and High-to-Low Range
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Determine the Average Daily Return
│   │   ├── Use Close price of current day (t)
│   │   └── Subtract from it the Close price of previous day (t-1)
│   └── Smooth the Average Daily Return
│       ├── Apply a Rolling Mean of n days (e.g., 10, 20, 50) on the Average Daily Return
│       └── Obtain the smoothed value as Price Momentum
├── Calculate Volume Momentum
│   ├── Determine the Average Daily Volume Change
│   │   ├── Use Volume of current day (t)
│   │   └── Subtract from it the Volume of previous day (t-1)
│   └── Smooth the Average Daily Volume Change
│       ├── Apply a Rolling Mean of n days (e.g., 10, 20, 50) on the Average Daily Volume Change
│       └── Obtain the smoothed value as Volume Momentum
├── Combine Momentum Indicators
│   ├── Multiply Price Momentum by Volume Momentum
│   └── Adjust for the sign of the Volume Momentum
│       ├── If Volume Momentum is positive, result is positive
│       └── If Volume Momentum is negative, invert the sign of the result
├── Calculate High-to-Low Range
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
├── Compute Range Momentum
    ├── Calculate 7-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of High-to-Low Range
    │   ├── Use High-to-Low Range
    │   └── Use 7-day Span
    └── Calculate 21-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of High-to-Low Range
        ├── Use High-to-Low Range
        └── Use 21-day Span
└── Integrate Momentum Factors
    ├── Compute the Ratio of 7-day EMA to 21-day EMA
    │   ├── Use 7-day EMA
    │   └── Use 21-day EMA
    └── Multiply the Combined Momentum Indicator by the Ratio
        ├── Use the result from the Combined Momentum Indicator
        └── Use the calculated Ratio
