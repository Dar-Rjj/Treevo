Volume Weighted Exponential Moving Average of Close Price Changes with Momentum Adjustments
├── Calculate Daily Close Price Changes
│   ├── Use Close price (t)
│   └── Subtract Close price (t-1)
├── Calculate Exponentially Weighted Moving Average (EWMA) of Close Price Changes
│   ├── Initialize EWMA value
│   │   ├── Set initial value as the first day's close price change
│   │   └── Set smoothing factor (alpha)
│   └── Iteratively update EWMA value
│       ├── Multiply previous EWMA by (1 - alpha)
│       └── Add current day's close price change multiplied by alpha
├── Volume Weight the EWMA of Close Price Changes
│   ├── Calculate Volume Multiplier
│   │   ├── Use Volume (t)
│   │   └── Divide by Sum of Volumes over the period
│   └── Multiply EWMA by Volume Multiplier
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Determine the Average Daily Return
│   │   ├── Use Close price of current day (t)
│   │   └── Subtract from it the Close price of previous day (t-1)
│   └── Smooth the Average Daily Return
│       ├── Apply a Rolling Mean of n days (e.g., 10, 20, 50) on the Average Daily Return
│       └── Obtain the smoothed value as Price Momentum
├── Calculate Volume Momentum
│   ├── Determine the Average Daily Volume Change
│   │   ├── Use Volume of current day (t)
│   │   └── Subtract from it the Volume of previous day (t-1)
│   └── Smooth the Average Daily Volume Change
│       ├── Apply a Rolling Mean of n days (e.g., 10, 20, 50) on the Average Daily Volume Change
│       └── Obtain the smoothed value as Volume Momentum
└── Combine Adjusted EWMA and Momentum Indicators
    ├── Multiply Volume-Weighted EWMA by Price Momentum
    └── Adjust for the sign of the Volume Momentum
        ├── If Volume Momentum is positive, result is positive
        └── If Volume Momentum is negative, invert the sign of the result
