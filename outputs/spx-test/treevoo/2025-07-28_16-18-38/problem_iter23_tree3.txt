  - Calculate daily return: (close_t - close_t-1) / close_t-1
  - Identify trend using moving averages

    - Long-term trend: 20-day MA of close prices
    - Trend indicator: 5-day MA - 20-day MA
  - Analyze daily volatility
    - Daily volatility: (high_t - low_t) / close_t-1
  - Examine trading volume
    - Daily volume change: (volume_t - volume_t-1) / volume_t-1
  - Evaluate high and low range
    - Daily price range: (high_t - low_t)
- Construct focused composite factors
  - Price-volume momentum: (daily_return + daily_volatility) * daily_volume_change
  - Trend confirmation: trend_indicator * (close_t * volume_t - close_t-1 * volume_t-1) / (close_t-1 * volume_t-1)
  - Volatility-adjusted momentum: (daily_return / daily_volatility) * daily_volume_change
  - Range-adjusted volume: (daily_price_range / (high_t + low_t)) * (volume_t - volume_t-1)
- Incorporate open and close prices
  - Open-close price difference: (close_t - open_t) / open_t
  - High-low spread: (high_t - low_t) / (open_t + close_t)
- Composite factors combining multiple indicators
  - Integrated momentum: (price_volume_momentum + volatility_adjusted_momentum) / 2
  - Combined trend and range: (trend_confirmation + range_adjusted_volume) / 2
- Evaluate factor performance
  - Correlation with future returns
  - Backtest on historical data
  - Out-of-sample testing
