```txt
Volume-Weighted True Range Momentum with Exponential Smoothing and Volume Trend
├── Calculate Day-to-Day Log Return of Close Price
│   ├── ln(Close_t / Close_(t-1))
├── Compute Volume-Weighted Moving Sum of Log Returns
│   ├── (Log Return * Volume) summed over [t-n, t]
├── Compute Volume-Adjusted Factor
│   ├── (Sum of (Log Return * Volume)) / (Sum of Volume) over [t-n, t]
├── Compute Average True Range (ATR)
│   ├── (Sum of True Ranges) / n
├── Incorporate Volume-Weighted ATR
│   ├── ATR * Volume-Adjusted Factor
├── Apply Exponential Smoothing
│   ├── (Volume-Weighted ATR * exp(-alpha * (t - i))) summed over [t-n, t]
└── Incorporate Volume Trend
    ├── (Current Volume / Average Volume over [t-n, t])
    ├── Exponentially Smoothed Result * Volume Trend
```
