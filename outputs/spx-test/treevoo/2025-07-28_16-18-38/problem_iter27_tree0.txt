  - Examine trading volume

  - Price-volume momentum: (logarithmic_daily_return + intraday_volatility) * daily_volume_change
  - Trend confirmation: trend_indicator * (close_t * volume_t - close_t-1 * volume_t-1) / (open_t * volume_t-1)
- Assess factor effectiveness
  - Correlation with future returns
  - Backtest on historical data
