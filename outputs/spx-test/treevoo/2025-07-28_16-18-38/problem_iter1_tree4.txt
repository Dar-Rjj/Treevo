```txt
Relative Strength and Volume Momentum Indicator
├── Calculate Daily Return
│   ├── Use Close price at day t
│   └── Subtract Close price at day (t-1)
├── Calculate Relative Strength
│   ├── Sum of Positive Returns
│   │   ├── Filter Daily Returns > 0 for last 14 days
│   │   └── Sum filtered positive returns
│   └── Sum of Negative Returns
│       ├── Filter Daily Returns < 0 for last 14 days
│       └── Absolute value of the sum of filtered negative returns
│   └── Calculate Relative Strength Ratio
│       └── Divide Sum of Positive Returns by Sum of Negative Returns
├── Calculate RSVMI
│   ├── Add 100 to the product of 100 and the natural log of the Relative Strength Ratio
│   └── Store as RSVMI
└── Volume Momentum
    ├── Calculate Volume Change
    │   ├── Use Volume at day t
    │   └── Subtract Volume at day (t-1)
    └── Smooth Volume Change
        ├── Sum of Volume Change for last 14 days
        └── Divide by 14
    └── Multiply RSVMI by Smoothed Volume Change
```
