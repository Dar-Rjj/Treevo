- Explore core price and volume dynamics
  - Calculate daily return: (close_t - close_t-1) / close_t-1
  - Identify trend using moving averages
    - Short-term trend: 5-day MA of close prices
    - Long-term trend: 20-day MA of close prices
    - Trend indicator: 5-day MA - 20-day MA
  - Analyze daily volatility
    - Daily volatility: (high_t - low_t) / close_t-1
  - Examine trading volume
    - Daily volume change: (volume_t - volume_t-1) / volume_t-1
- Construct composite factors
  - Price-volume momentum: (daily_return + daily_volatility) * daily_volume_change
  - Trend confirmation: trend_indicator * (close_t * volume_t - close_t-1 * volume_t-1) / (close_t-1 * volume_t-1)
- Assess factor effectiveness
  - Correlation with future returns
  - Backtest on historical data
