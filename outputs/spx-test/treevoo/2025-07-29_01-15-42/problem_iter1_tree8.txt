```txt
Dynamic Momentum-Volume Oscillator
├── Calculate Momentum Component
│   ├── Compute Simple Moving Averages (SMA)
│   │   ├── SMA Short-term (e.g., 5 days)
│   │   └── SMA Long-term (e.g., 20 days)
│   ├── Subtract SMA Long-term from SMA Short-term
│   └── Identify Trend Direction
│       ├── Positive if SMA Short > SMA Long
│       └── Negative if SMA Short < SMA Long
├── Calculate Volume Component
│   ├── Compute Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Volume
│   │   ├── EMA Short-term (e.g., 3 days)
│   │   └── EMA Long-term (e.g., 10 days)
│   ├── Subtract EMA Short-term from EMA Long-term
│   └── Determine Volume Trend
│       ├── Increasing if EMA Short > EMA Long
│       └── Decreasing if EMA Short < EMA Long
└── Combine Momentum and Volume Components
    ├── Multiply Momentum Difference by Volume Difference
    ├── Interpret Combined Signal
    │   ├── Buy Signal if Trend is Positive and Volume is Increasing
    │   └── Sell Signal if Trend is Negative and Volume is Decreasing
    └── Generate Alpha Factor
        ├── Sum of Buy Signals
        └── Subtract Sum of Sell Signals
```
