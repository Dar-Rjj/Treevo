```txt
- Explore Trend Indicators
  - Calculate Moving Averages
    - Simple Moving Average (SMA) over different windows (e.g., 5, 10, 20 days)
    - Exponential Moving Average (EMA) over different windows (e.g., 5, 10, 20 days)
  - Compute Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
    - MACD Line: 12-day EMA - 26-day EMA
    - Signal Line: 9-day EMA of MACD Line
    - MACD Histogram: Difference between MACD Line and Signal Line
  - Evaluate Momentum Indicators
    - Rate of Change (ROC) over different periods (e.g., 10, 20 days)
    - Relative Strength Index (RSI) over different periods (e.g., 14 days)
- Analyze Volatility
  - Historical Volatility
    - Standard Deviation of daily returns over different windows (e.g., 20, 50 days)
  - True Range and Average True Range (ATR) over different windows (e.g., 14 days)
- Investigate Volume-Price Relationship
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
  - Money Flow Index (MFI) over different periods (e.g., 14 days)
- Examine Price Patterns
  - Identify Bullish/Bearish Engulfing Patterns
  - Identify Doji Patterns
  - Identify Hammer/Hanging Man Patterns
- Assess Market Sentiment
  - Compare Opening and Closing Prices
    - Open-Close Spread: Close - Open
    - Percentage Change: (Close - Open) / Open * 100
- Evaluate Liquidity
  - Trade Volume Analysis
    - Average Daily Volume over different windows (e.g., 30, 90 days)
  - Dollar Volume: Volume * Close
- Analyze Financial News and Events
  - Event Impact Score based on news sentiment
  - Event Frequency: Number of significant events in a given period
- Consider Economic Indicators
  - GDP Growth Rate
  - Inflation Rate
  - Interest Rates
- Incorporate Technical Indicators
  - Bollinger Bands
    - Upper Band: SMA + 2 * Standard Deviation
    - Lower Band: SMA - 2 * Standard Deviation
  - Stochastic Oscillator
    - %K: 100 * (Close - Low) / (High - Low)
    - %D: 3-day SMA of %K
- Evaluate Market Breadth
  - Advance-Decline Line
  - New Highs-New Lows
- Analyze Sector and Industry Performance
  - Relative Strength to S&P 500
  - Relative Strength to Peer Group
- Consider Seasonal and Cyclical Factors
  - Monthly and Quarterly Seasonality
  - Business Cycle Phases
- Incorporate Macro-Economic Data
  - Unemployment Rate
  - Consumer Confidence Index
  - Housing Starts
- Assess Market Sentiment through Social Media
  - Sentiment Analysis of Twitter and Stock Forums
  - Sentiment Score: Positive Tweets - Negative Tweets
- Examine Market Anomalies
  - January Effect
  - Turn of the Month Effect
  - Small Firm Effect
```
