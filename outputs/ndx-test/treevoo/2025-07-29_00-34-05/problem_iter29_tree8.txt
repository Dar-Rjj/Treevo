```txt
[Factor: Momentum]
- [Calculate: Simple Moving Average (SMA)]
  - [Input: Close Price]
  - [Period: 14 days]
- [Calculate: Exponential Moving Average (EMA)]
  - [Input: Close Price]
  - [Period: 28 days]
- [Compute: Momentum Factor]
  - [Formula: SMA - EMA]

[Factor: Volatility]
- [Calculate: True Range (TR)]
  - [Input: High, Low, Close]
  - [Formula: max(High - Low, abs(High - Previous Close), abs(Low - Previous Close))]
- [Calculate: Average True Range (ATR)]
  - [Input: TR]
  - [Period: 14 days]
- [Compute: Volatility Factor]
  - [Formula: ATR / Close]

[Factor: Volume Strength]
- [Calculate: Volume Moving Average (VMA)]
  - [Input: Volume]
  - [Period: 14 days]
- [Compute: Volume Strength Factor]
  - [Formula: (Volume - VMA) / VMA]

[Factor: Combined Momentum and Volatility]
- [Input: Momentum Factor]
- [Input: Volatility Factor]
- [Compute: Combined Factor]
  - [Formula: (Momentum Factor * Volatility Factor) / 2]

[Factor: Combined Momentum and Volume Strength]
- [Input: Momentum Factor]
- [Input: Volume Strength Factor]
- [Compute: Combined Factor]
  - [Formula: (Momentum Factor + Volume Strength Factor) / 2]

[Factor: Integrated Alpha]
- [Input: Combined Momentum and Volatility]
- [Input: Combined Momentum and Volume Strength]
- [Compute: Final Alpha Factor]
  - [Formula: (Combined Momentum and Volatility + Combined Momentum and Volume Strength) / 2]
```
