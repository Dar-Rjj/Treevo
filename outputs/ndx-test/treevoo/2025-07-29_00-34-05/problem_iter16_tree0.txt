Thought: Identify key price and volume characteristics
  - Sub-Thought: Calculate the return characteristics
    - Sub-Thought: Daily return
      - Calculation: (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1}
    - Sub-Thought: Intraday return
      - Calculation: (Close_t - Open_t) / Open_t
  - Sub-Thought: Analyze price movement patterns
    - Sub-Thought: True range
      - Calculation: max(High_t - Low_t, abs(High_t - Close_{t-1}), abs(Low_t - Close_{t-1}))
    - Sub-Thought: Price volatility
      - Calculation: Standard deviation of daily returns over a rolling window (e.g., 20 days)
  - Sub-Thought: Volume-related signals
    - Sub-Thought: Volume change
      - Calculation: (Volume_t - Volume_{t-1}) / Volume_{t-1}
    - Sub-Thought: Volume-price relationship
      - Calculation: (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) * (Volume_t - Volume_{t-1})
  - Sub-Thought: High and low price movements
    - Sub-Thought: High-Low range
      - Calculation: (High_t - Low_t)
    - Sub-Thought: Close to high/low ratio
      - Calculation: Close_t / High_t, Close_t / Low_t
  - Sub-Thought: Money flow indicators
    - Sub-Thought: Money flow
      - Calculation: (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) * Volume_t
    - Sub-Thought: Money flow ratio
      - Calculation: Sum of money flow on up days / Sum of money flow on down days over a rolling window (e.g., 20 days)
