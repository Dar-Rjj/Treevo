Volume Adjusted Momentum and Composite Volatility Alpha Factor
├── Calculate Volume Adjusted Price (VAP)
│   ├── Typical Price = (High + Low + Close) / 3
│   └── VAP = Typical Price * Volume
├── Calculate Volume Adjusted Momentum
│   ├── 20-day EMA of VAP
│   └── Momentum = Current VAP - 20-day EMA of VAP
├── Incorporate Recent Volatility
│   ├── Daily Returns = (Close_t - Close_(t-1)) / Close_(t-1)
│   ├── 10-day Standard Deviation of Daily Returns
│   └── Inverse Volatility = 1 / 10-day Standard Deviation
│       └── Adjusted Momentum = Momentum * Inverse Volatility
├── Calculate High-Low Range Volatility
│   ├── Daily High-Low Range = High - Low
│   └── 20-Day Moving Standard Deviation of High-Low Ranges
├── Calculate Open-Close Range Volatility
│   ├── Daily Open-Close Range = |Open - Close|
│   └── 20-Day Moving Standard Deviation of Open-Close Ranges
└── Combine Adjusted Momentum and Composite Volatility
    ├── Average Volatility = (High-Low Range Volatility + Open-Close Range Volatility) / 2
    └── Alpha Factor = Adjusted Momentum - Average Volatility
