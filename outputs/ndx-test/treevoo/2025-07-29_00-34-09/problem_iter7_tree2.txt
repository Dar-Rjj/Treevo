Synthesis of Intraday and Volume-Weighted Momentum with RSI
├── Calculate Intraday Price Change Ratio
│   ├── Compute High-Low Ratio
│   │   ├── (High - Low) / Low
│   └── Multiply by Intraday Trading Activity
│       ├── Volume / (High + Low)
├── Measure Sustained Momentum
│   ├── (Close - Previous Close) / Previous Close
├── Calculate Volume Weighted Momentum
│   ├── Sequential Momentum * Volume
├── Calculate Upward and Downward Volume-Weighted Returns
│   ├── Calculate Positive Volume-Weighted Returns
│   │   ├── Filter for positive returns
│   │   └── Sum over 14 days
│   └── Calculate Negative Volume-Weighted Returns
│       ├── Filter for negative returns
│       └── Sum absolute values over 14 days
├── Compute RSI
│   ├── Relative Strength = Sum of Positive Returns / Sum of Absolute Negative Returns
│   └── RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + Relative Strength))
└── Combine Factors
    ├── Intraday Price Change Ratio + Sustained Momentum + Volume Weighted Momentum + RSI
