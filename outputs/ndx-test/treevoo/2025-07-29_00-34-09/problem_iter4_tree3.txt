Relative Strength Index based on Volume-Weighted Returns
├── Calculate Daily Volume-Weighted Return
│   ├── Compute Logarithmic Return using Close price
│   └── Multiply by Volume
├── Aggregate Upward and Downward Volume-Weighted Returns
│   ├── Sum Positive Volume-Weighted Returns over 14 days
│   └── Sum Absolute Values of Negative Volume-Weighted Returns over 14 days
├── Compute RSI
│   ├── Calculate Relative Strength
│   │   ├── Divide Sum of Positive Volume-Weighted Returns
│   │   └── by Sum of Absolute Negative Volume-Weighted Returns
│   └── Apply RSI Formula: 100 - (100 / (1 + Relative Strength))
└── Interpret Factor
    ├── High RSI indicates overbought, low RSI indicates oversold
    └── Use as a predictive signal for future stock returns
