Price Momentum Indicators Integration
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Compute Close Price Change
│       └── Day t's Close - Day t-1's Close
│   └── Sum over N periods
├── Calculate Volume Adjusted Momentum
│   ├── Compute Weighted Close Price Change

│   └── Sum over N periods
└── Integrate Momentum with Volatility
    ├── Calculate True Range
    │   └── Max(High - Low, Abs(High - Previous Close), Abs(Low - Previous Close))
    └── Normalize Momentum by Volatility
        └── Volume Adjusted Momentum / True Range
