Average Momentum with Volume Adjustment
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── 1-day Price Return: (Close price (t) - Close price (t-1)) / Close price (t-1)
│   ├── 5-day Price Return: (Close price (t) - Close price (t-5)) / Close price (t-5)
│   └── 20-day Price Return: (Close price (t) - Close price (t-20)) / Close price (t-20)
├── Average the Momentum
│   └── (1-day Price Return + 5-day Price Return + 20-day Price Return) / 3
└── Adjust for Volume
    └── Daily Volume Percentage Change: (Volume (t) - Volume (t-1)) / Volume (t-1)
    └── Final Factor: Average Momentum * (1 + Daily Volume Percentage Change)
