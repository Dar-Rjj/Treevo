Crossover of Average Momentum with Volume Adjustment and Combined Price and Volume Momentum with Weighted Volatility
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── 1-day Price Return: (Close price (t) - Close price (t-1)) / Close price (t-1)
│   ├── 5-day Price Return: (Close price (t) - Close price (t-5)) / Close price (t-5)
│   └── 20-day Price Return: (Close price (t) - Close price (t-20)) / Close price (t-20)
├── Average the Momentum
│   └── (1-day Price Return + 5-day Price Return + 20-day Price Return) / 3
├── Adjust for Volume
│   └── Daily Volume Percentage Change: (Volume (t) - Volume (t-1)) / Volume (t-1)
│   └── Volume Adjusted Momentum: Average Momentum * (1 + Daily Volume Percentage Change)
├── Calculate Adjusted Price Difference
│   ├── Use Close price on day t
│   └── Subtract Close price on day t-10
│       └── Multiply by Logarithm of (Close price on day t / Close price on day t-1)
├── Calculate Rolling High-Low Differential
│   ├── Determine 20-day Rolling High
│   │   └── Use High price
│   └── Subtract 20-day Rolling Low
│       └── Use Low price
├── Weight by Volume Volatility
│   ├── Calculate Volume Volatility
│   │   ├── Use Volume on days t-1 to t-10
│   │   └── Calculate standard deviation
│   └── Multiply Rolling High-Low Differential by Volume Volatility
├── Calculate Volume Adjusted Momentum
│   ├── Use Today's Volume
│   └── Subtract Previous Day Volume
│       └── Multiply by (Today's Close - 10-Day Moving Average of Close)
├── Aggregate the Signals
│   ├── Add Weighted Rolling High-Low Differential
│   ├── Subtract Adjusted Price Difference
│   └── Add Volume Adjusted Momentum
└── Final Factor
    └── (Volume Adjusted Momentum + Aggregated Signal) / Total Volume
