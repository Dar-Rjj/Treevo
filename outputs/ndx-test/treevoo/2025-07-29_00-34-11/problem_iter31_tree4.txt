Synthesized Volume-Weighted Intraday Sentiment and Price Action Analysis
├── Compute Volume-Weighted Ratios
│   ├── High-Low Ratio: (High - Low)
│   ├── Open-Close Ratio: (Open - Close)
│   └── Weighted Ratios: (High-Low Ratio + Open-Close Ratio) * Volume
├── Measure Price-Volatility Alignment
│   ├── Intraday Volatility: (High - Low)
│   └── Sentiment: If (Weighted Ratios > 0) Positive, else Negative
├── Incorporate Momentum
│   ├── Close-to-Close Return: (Close[t] - Close[t-1]) / Close[t-1]
│   └── Final Factor: Weighted Ratios * Close-to-Close Return
├── Analyze Price Action Patterns
│   ├── Relative Closing Price Change: (Close[t] - Close[t-1]) / Close[t-1]
│   ├── True Range: max(High[t], Close[t-1]) - min(Low[t], Close[t-1])
│   ├── Upper Shadow Length: High[t] - max(Open[t], Close[t])
│   └── Lower Shadow Length: min(Open[t], Close[t]) - Low[t]
└── Investigate Volume-Related Factors
    ├── Volume Ratio: Volume[t] / Volume[t-1]
    └── Volume-Weighted Close Price: Close[t] * Volume[t]
