```txt
Intraday Sentiment and Momentum Indicator
├── Calculate Intraday Price Movement
│   ├── Compute High-Low Ratio: (High - Low) / Low
│   ├── Compute Open-Close Ratio: (Close - Open) / Open
│   └── Average Ratios: (High-Low Ratio + Open-Close Ratio) / 2
└── Measure Price-Volatility Alignment
    ├── Calculate Intraday Volatility: (High - Low) * Volume
    └── Evaluate Alignment
        ├── Compare Intraday Movement with Volatility: Intraday Price Movement / Intraday Volatility
        ├── Determine Sentiment
        │   ├── Positive if High Alignment
        │   └── Negative if Low Alignment
        └── Incorporate Momentum
            ├── Calculate Momentum: (Close - Close[t-1]) / Close[t-1]
            └── Adjust Sentiment
                ├── If Momentum > 0, Strengthen Positive Sentiment
                └── If Momentum < 0, Strengthen Negative Sentiment
```
