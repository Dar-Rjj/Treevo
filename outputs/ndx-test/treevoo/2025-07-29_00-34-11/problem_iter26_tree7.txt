```txt
Intraday Sentiment and Volume Indicator
├── Calculate Intraday Price Movement
│   ├── Compute High-Low Ratio: (High - Low) / Low
│   ├── Compute Open-Close Ratio: (Close - Open) / Open
│   └── Average Ratios: (High-Low Ratio + Open-Close Ratio) / 2
└── Measure Price-Volatility-Volume Alignment
    ├── Calculate Intraday Volatility: (High - Low) * Volume
    └── Evaluate Alignment
        ├── Compare Intraday Movement with Volatility: Intraday Price Movement / Intraday Volatility
        └── Determine Sentiment
            ├── Positive if High Alignment
            └── Negative if Low Alignment
    └── Incorporate Volume Impact
        ├── Calculate Volume Adjusted Intraday Volatility: (High - Low) * sqrt(Volume)
        └── Evaluate Volume Alignment
            ├── Compare Intraday Movement with Volume Adjusted Volatility: Intraday Price Movement / Volume Adjusted Intraday Volatility
            └── Determine Volume Sentiment
                ├── Positive if High Volume Alignment
                └── Negative if Low Volume Alignment
```
