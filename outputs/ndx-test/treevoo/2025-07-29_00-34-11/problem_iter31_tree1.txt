```txt
Intraday Sentiment Indicator
├── Calculate Intraday Price Movement Ratios
│   ├── High-Low Ratio: (High - Low) / Low
│   └── Open-Close Ratio: (Close - Open) / Open
│   └── Combined Ratio: Average(High-Low Ratio, Open-Close Ratio)
└── Measure Price-Volatility Alignment
    ├── Intraday Volatility: (High - Low) * Volume
    └── Evaluate Alignment
        ├── Compare Combined Ratio with Intraday Volatility
        │   ├── Positive if Combined Ratio > Intraday Volatility
        │   └── Negative if Combined Ratio < Intraday Volatility
```
