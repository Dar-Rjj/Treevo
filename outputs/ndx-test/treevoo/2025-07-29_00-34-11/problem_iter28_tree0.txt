Synthesized Volume-Weighted Intraday Sentiment and Price Action Analysis
├── Compute Volume-Weighted Ratios
│   ├── High-Low Ratio: (High - Low) / (High + Low)
│   └── Open-Close Ratio: (Open - Close) / (Open + Close)
│   └── Weighted by Volume: (High-Low Ratio + Open-Close Ratio) * Volume
├── Measure Price-Volatility Alignment
│   ├── Intraday Volatility: High - Low
│   └── Sentiment: 
│       └── If Averaged Ratios > Volatility: Positive, else: Negative
├── Incorporate Momentum
│   ├── Close-to-Close Return: (Close[t] - Close[t-1]) / Close[t-1]
│   └── Final Adjustment: Weighted Ratios * Close-to-Close Return
├── Analyze Price Action Patterns
│   ├── Relative Closing Price Change: (close[t] - close[t-1]) / close[t-1]
│   ├── True Range: max(high[t], close[t-1]) - min(low[t], close[t-1])
│   ├── Upper Shadow Length: high[t] - max(open[t], close[t])
│   └── Lower Shadow Length: min(open[t], close[t]) - low[t]
├── Investigate Volume-Related Factors
│   ├── Volume Ratio: volume[t] / volume[t-1]
│   └── Volume-Weighted Close: close[t] * volume[t]
└── Derive Momentum Indicators from Price Data
    ├── Simple Moving Average (SMA): sum(close[t-i] for i in range(0, n)) / n
    ├── Rate of Change (ROC): (close[t] - close[t-n]) / close[t-n]
    └── Directional Movement: close[t] - close[t-1]
├── Integrate Amount into Analysis
    ├── Daily Amount to Volume Ratio: amount[t] / volume[t]
    └── Adjusted Price: (amount[t] / volume[t]) * close[t]
