Combining Intraday Dynamics, Reversal, and Momentum for Enhanced Alpha Generation
├── Calculate Intraday Volatility
│   ├── (High - Low) / Low
├── Calculate Close-to-Open Return
│   ├── (Close - Open) / Open
├── Weighted Sum of Intraday and Close-to-Open Returns
│   ├── Weight Intraday Volatility by its absolute value
│   ├── Weight Close-to-Open Return by its absolute value
│   └── Sum the weighted returns to get Preliminary Factor
├── Calculate True Range (TR)
│   ├── Max(High - Low, abs(High - Previous Close), abs(Low - Previous Close))
├── Calculate Positive Directional Movement (+DM)
│   ├── If Current High > Previous High and (Current High - Previous High) > (Previous Low - Current Low)
│   │   └── Current High - Previous High
│   └── Otherwise 0
├── Calculate Negative Directional Movement (-DM)
│   ├── If Previous Low > Current Low and (Previous Low - Current Low) > (Current High - Previous High)
│   │   └── Previous Low - Current Low
│   └── Otherwise 0
├── Calculate +DI
│   ├── Smooth +DM and divide by TR
├── Calculate -DI
│   ├── Smooth -DM and divide by TR
├── Calculate ADMI
│   ├── (+DI - -DI) / (+DI + -DI)
└── Multiply Preliminary Factor by ADMI
    └── Result is the new alpha factor
