Average Directional Movement Indicator (ADMI)
├── Calculate True Range (TR)
│   ├── Absolute High-Low for each day
│   │   ├── Use High price
│   │   └── Use Low price
│   ├── Previous Close-High for each day
│   │   ├── Use Previous Close price
│   │   └── Use High price
│   └── Previous Close-Low for each day
│       ├── Use Previous Close price
│       └── Use Low price
├── Calculate Average True Range (ATR)
│   ├── Sum of TR over n periods
│   └── Divide by n
├── Calculate Positive Directional Movement (+DM)
│   ├── If Current High > Previous High and Current Low < Previous Low, then 
│   │   └── Current High - Previous High
│   └── Otherwise 0
├── Calculate Negative Directional Movement (-DM)
│   ├── If Current Low < Previous Low and Current High > Previous High, then
│   │   └── Previous Low - Current Low
│   └── Otherwise 0
├── Calculate +DM Smoothed Over n Periods
│   ├── Sum of +DM over n periods
│   └── Divide by n
├── Calculate -DM Smoothed Over n Periods
│   ├── Sum of -DM over n periods
│   └── Divide by n
├── Calculate +DI
│   ├── Divide +DM Smoothed by ATR
└── Calculate -DI
    ├── Divide -DM Smoothed by ATR
├── Calculate ADMI
│   ├── Difference between +DI and -DI
│   └── Divide by sum of +DI and -DI
└── Output ADMI
