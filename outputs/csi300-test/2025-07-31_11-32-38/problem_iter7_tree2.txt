```txt
Combining Intraday Momentum, Reversal, and Directional Movement for Enhanced Alpha Generation
├── Calculate Intraday Return
│   ├── (High - Low) / Low
├── Calculate Close-to-Open Return
│   ├── (Close - Open) / Open
├── Calculate Daily Volume Change
│   ├── (Volume - Previous Day's Volume) / Previous Day's Volume
├── Combine Intraday and Close-to-Open Returns
│   ├── Weight Intraday Return by its absolute value
│   └── Weight Close-to-Open Return by its absolute value
├── Integrate Volume Change
│   ├── Adjust weights by multiplying them with the Daily Volume Change
│   └── Sum the adjusted weights to get a Preliminary Factor
├── Calculate True Range (TR)
│   ├── Max(High - Low, abs(High - Previous Close), abs(Low - Previous Close))
├── Calculate Average True Range (ATR)
│   ├── Sum of TR over n periods
│   └── Divide by n
├── Calculate Positive Directional Movement (+DM)
│   ├── If Current High > Previous High and (Current High - Previous High) > (Previous Low - Current Low), then
│   │   └── Current High - Previous High
│   └── Otherwise 0
├── Calculate Negative Directional Movement (-DM)
│   ├── If Previous Low > Current Low and (Previous Low - Current Low) > (Current High - Previous High), then
│   │   └── Previous Low - Current Low
│   └── Otherwise 0
├── Calculate +DM Smoothed Over n Periods
│   ├── Sum of +DM over n periods
│   └── Divide by n
├── Calculate -DM Smoothed Over n Periods
│   ├── Sum of -DM over n periods
│   └── Divide by n
├── Calculate +DI
│   ├── Divide +DM Smoothed by ATR
├── Calculate -DI
│   ├── Divide -DM Smoothed by ATR
├── Calculate ADMI
│   ├── (+DI - -DI) / (+DI + -DI)
└── Synthesize Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Multiply Preliminary Factor by ADMI
    └── The result is the new alpha factor
```
