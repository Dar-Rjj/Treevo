Momentum and Volume Breakout Factor
├── Calculate Daily Momentum
│   ├── Use Close price
│   └── Compute as (Close - Close(t-20)) / Close(t-20)
├── Calculate Volume Surge
│   ├── Use Volume
│   ├── Compute Median Volume over last 20 days
│   │   ├── Sort Volume for the past 20 days
│   │   └── Find the middle value, if even take the average of two middle values
│   ├── Determine if Today's Volume is a Surge
│   │   ├── Compare Today's Volume with Median Volume
│   │   └── If Today's Volume > 2 * Median Volume, set to 1, else 0
├── Calculate Price Breakout
│   ├── Use High price
│   ├── Compute Highest High over last 20 days
│   │   ├── Find the maximum High price in the past 20 days
│   ├── Determine if Today's High Price is a Breakout
│   │   ├── Compare Today's High Price with Highest High
│   │   └── If Today's High Price > Highest High, set to 1, else 0
└── Combine Momentum, Volume Surge, and Price Breakout
    ├── Multiply Daily Momentum by Volume Surge
    ├── Multiply the result by Price Breakout
    └── Interpretation: A strong move over a longer period combined with significant volume and a new high might signal a breakout continuation.
