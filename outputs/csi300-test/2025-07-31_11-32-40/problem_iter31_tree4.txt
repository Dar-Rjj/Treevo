```txt
Weighted Cumulative Directional Price Movement Adjusted by Volume
├── Calculate High-Low Percentage Change
│   └── (High - Low) / Low
├── Compute Weighted Directional Volume
│   ├── Sum (Close - Open) * Volume * (Close / Open) if Close > Open (Accumulated Upward)
│   ├── Sum (Open - Close) * Volume * (Open / Close) if Close < Open (Accumulated Downward)
│   └── Net Weighted Directional Volume = Accumulated Upward - Accumulated Downward
└── Multiply High-Low Percentage Change with Net Weighted Directional Volume
    ├── Use High-Low Percentage Change
    └── Use Net Weighted Directional Volume
```
