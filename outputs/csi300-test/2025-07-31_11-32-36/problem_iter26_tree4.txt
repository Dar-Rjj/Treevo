```txt
Combining Intraday Volatility, Momentum, and Volume Influence
├── Compute Intraday Range
│   └── (High - Low)
├── Calculate Momentum
│   └── (Close(t) / Close(t-1) - 1)
├── Combine Intraday Range and Momentum
│   └── (Intraday Range * Momentum)
└── Adjust for Volume
    └── (Combined Factor * (Volume(t) / Volume(t-1)))
```
