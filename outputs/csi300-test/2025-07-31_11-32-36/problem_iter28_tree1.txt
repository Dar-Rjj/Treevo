Integrated and Enhanced Intraday and Daily Momentum, Volatility, and Volume Adjustments with EMA Integration
├── Intraday Momentum Score
│   ├── Sum of Deltas = (High - Low) + (Close - Open)
│   └── Weighted Momentum = Sum of Deltas * (Volume / MAV)
├── Intraday Volatility Score
│   ├── Average of Deltas = (High - Close + Low - Close) / 2
│   └── Weighted Volatility = Average of Deltas * (Amount / MAA)
├── Price Trend Factor
│   ├── Close-Open Ratio = Close / Open
│   └── Smoothed Ratio = EMA(Close-Open Ratio, EMA Span)
├── Daily Momentum and Volatility Components
│   ├── Daily Returns = (Close - Previous Day's Close) / Previous Day's Close
│   ├── EMA Returns = EMA(Daily Returns, suitable lookback)
│   ├── EMA Squared Returns = EMA(Daily Returns^2, same lookback)
│   └── Weighted Divergence = |EMA Returns - EMA Squared Returns| * (Volume / High-Low Range)
├── Combined Scores
│   ├── Total Momentum = Weighted Momentum + Smoothed Ratio
│   └── Net Score = Total Momentum - Weighted Volatility + Weighted Divergence
├── Integrated Momentum
│   ├── Volume Weighted Price Change = ((Close * Volume + Open * Volume) / (2 * Volume)) - Yesterday's Volume Weighted Price
│   └── Integrated Momentum = EMA(Weighted Momentum + Volume Weighted Price Change, 30)
├── Volatility Ratio
│   ├── Long-Term Volatility = EMA(EMA Squared Returns, 50 days)
│   ├── Short-Term Volatility = EMA(EMA Squared Returns, 10 days)
│   └── Volatility Ratio = Short-Term Volatility / Long-Term Volatility
└── Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Core Factor = (Net Score + Integrated Momentum) * Volatility Ratio
    └── Volume Trend = (Volume today - Volume yesterday) / (Lookback Window Days * 2)
        └── Final Alpha = Core Factor + Volume Trend
