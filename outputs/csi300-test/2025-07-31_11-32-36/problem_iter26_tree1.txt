Integrated and Enhanced Intraday and Daily Momentum, Volatility, and Volume Adjustments with EMA Integration and Crossover Considerations
├── Intraday Momentum Score
│   ├── High-Low Delta = High - Low
│   ├── Close-Open Delta = Close - Open
│   ├── Sum of Deltas = High-Low Delta + Close-Open Delta
│   ├── MAV = Moving Average of Volume (suitable lookback)
│   └── Weighted Momentum = Sum of Deltas * (Volume / MAV)
├── Intraday Volatility Score
│   ├── High-Close Delta = High - Close
│   ├── Low-Close Delta = Low - Close
│   ├── Average of Deltas = (High-Close Delta + Low-Close Delta) / 2
│   ├── MAA = Moving Average of Amount (suitable lookback)
│   ├── Standard Deviation of Intraday High-Low Range (last 30 days)
│   └── Enhanced Volatility = Average of Deltas * Standard Deviation * (Amount / MAA)
├── Price Trend Factor
│   ├── Close-Open Ratio = Close / Open
│   └── Smoothed Ratio = EMA(Close-Open Ratio, EMA Span)
├── Daily Momentum and Volatility Components
│   ├── Daily Returns = (Close - Previous Day's Close) / Previous Day's Close
│   ├── EMA Returns = EMA(Daily Returns, suitable lookback)
│   ├── EMA Squared Returns = EMA(Daily Returns^2, same lookback)
│   ├── Divergence = |EMA Returns - EMA Squared Returns|
│   ├── High-Low Range = High - Low
│   └── Weighted Divergence = Divergence * (Volume / High-Low Range)
├── Combined Intraday and Daily Scores
│   ├── Total Momentum = Weighted Momentum + Smoothed Ratio
│   └── Net Score = Total Momentum - Enhanced Volatility + Weighted Divergence
├── Volume and Price Adjusted Momentum
│   ├── Volume Weighted Price = (Close * Volume + Open * Volume) / Total Volume
│   ├── Volume Weighted Price Change = Volume Weighted Price - Yesterday's Volume Weighted Price
│   ├── Integrated Momentum = EMA(Weighted Momentum + Volume Weighted Price Change, 30)
│   └── Momentum Reversal = (Weighted Momentum + Volume Weighted Price Change) - Integrated Momentum
├── Long-Term and Short-Term Volatility
│   ├── Long-Term Volatility = EMA(EMA Squared Returns, 50 days)
│   ├── Short-Term Volatility = EMA(EMA Squared Returns, 10 days)
│   └── Volatility Ratio = Short-Term Volatility / Long-Term Volatility
├── Volume and Amplitude Confirmation
│   ├── Calculate Volume Confirmation
│   │   ├── If Volume of day t > Volume of day (t-1), set to 1
│   │   └── Otherwise, set to 0
│   ├── Calculate Amplitude Confirmation
│   │   ├── Calculate Daily Amplitude
│   │   │   ├── Subtract Low from High of day t

│   │   └── Otherwise, set to 0
│   └── Combined Confirmation = (Volume Confirmation + Amplitude Confirmation) * 0.5
├── Final Alpha Factor

    └── Final Alpha = Core Factor + Volume Trend + (Price Momentum Over Volume Trend * Combined Confirmation)
