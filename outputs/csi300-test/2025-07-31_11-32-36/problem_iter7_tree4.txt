├── Calculate Daily Price Change

    │   │   ├── Subtract Low from High
    │   ├── Compare Amplitude of day t with Amplitude of day (t-1)
    │   │   ├── If Amplitude of day t > Amplitude of day (t-1), set to 1
    │   │   └── Otherwise, set to 0
    └── Multiply 5-Day Price Momentum by (Volume Confirmation + Amplitude Confirmation)
