```txt
- Explore price-based indicators
  - Calculate the daily return
    - (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1}
  - Calculate the high-low spread
    - High_t - Low_t
  - Calculate the open-close spread
    - Close_t - Open_t
- Explore volume-based indicators
  - Calculate the change in trading volume
    - Volume_t - Volume_{t-1}
  - Calculate the ratio of volume to close
    - Volume_t / Close_t
- Combine price and volume for composite indicators
  - Calculate the volume-weighted average price
    - (Volume_t * Close_t) / Volume_t
  - Calculate the money flow
    - (Close_t - Open_t) * Volume_t
- Explore amount-based indicators
  - Calculate the change in transaction amount
    - Amount_t - Amount_{t-1}
  - Calculate the ratio of amount to close
    - Amount_t / Close_t
- Combine multiple indicators for a comprehensive factor
  - Factor combining price, volume, and amount
    - (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1} + (High_t - Low_t) + (Volume_t - Volume_{t-1}) + (Amount_t - Amount_{t-1})
```
