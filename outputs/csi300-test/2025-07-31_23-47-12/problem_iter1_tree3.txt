```txt
Cumulative Directional Movement Factor
├── Calculate Daily Directional Movement
│   ├── Compute Up Move
│   │   ├── Use High price
│   │   └── Use Low price of previous day
│   └── Compute Down Move
│       ├── Use Low price
│       └── Use High price of previous day
├── Determine Positive and Negative Directional Indicators
│   ├── Sum Positive DMs over period
│   │   ├── Use Up Move if positive, else 0
│   └── Sum Negative DMs over period
│       ├── Use Down Move if positive, else 0
├── Calculate True Range
│   ├── Compute High-Low Difference
│   ├── Compute High-Close Difference (previous day)
│   └── Compute Close-Low Difference (previous day)
│       ├── Use the maximum of the three differences
├── Compute Directional Indicator
│   ├── Divide sum of Positive DMs by period's sum of True Ranges
│   └── Divide sum of Negative DMs by period's sum of True Ranges
├── Derive Cumulative Directional Movement
│   ├── Subtract Negative Directional Indicator from Positive Directional Indicator
│   └── Multiply difference by Volume
└── Generate Alpha Factor
    ├── Use final Cumulative Directional Movement as the alpha factor
    └── Interpret as a measure of volume-weighted trend strength
```
