- Explore Basic Price and Volume Indicators
  - Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Close Prices
    - SMA(5)
    - SMA(10)
    - SMA(20)
  - Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Close Prices
    - EMA(5)
    - EMA(10)
    - EMA(20)
  - Relative Strength Index (RSI) of Close Prices
    - RSI(14)
    - RSI(28)
  - On Balance Volume (OBV)
- Analyze Price Movement Patterns
  - Price Difference (Close - Open)
  - High-Low Range (High - Low)
  - True Range (Max[High - Low, Abs(High - Previous Close), Abs(Low - Previous Close)])
  - Percentage Change in Close Prices
    - Daily
    - Weekly
    - Monthly
- Explore Volume-based Indicators
  - Volume Rate of Change (ROC)
  - Volume-SMA
    - SMA(5)
    - SMA(10)
    - SMA(20)
- Combine Indicators for New Insights
  - SMA Crossover
    - SMA(5) over SMA(10)
    - SMA(10) over SMA(20)
  - EMA Crossover
    - EMA(5) over EMA(10)
    - EMA(10) over EMA(20)
  - RSI with Moving Averages
    - RSI(14) over SMA(10)
    - RSI(28) over SMA(20)
  - OBV with Volume-SMA
    - OBV over Volume-SMA(5)
    - OBV over Volume-SMA(10)
- Evaluate Trend Strength
  - Average Directional Index (ADX)
    - ADX(14)
    - ADX(28)
  - Plus Directional Indicator (+DI)
    - +DI(14)
    - +DI(28)
  - Minus Directional Indicator (-DI)
    - -DI(14)
    - -DI(28)
- Incorporate Momentum and Volatility
  - Momentum
    - Close - Close(n periods ago)
    - n = 5, 10, 20
  - Bollinger Bands
    - Upper Band: SMA(Close, 20) + 2 * StdDev(Close, 20)
    - Lower Band: SMA(Close, 20) - 2 * StdDev(Close, 20)
    - Bandwidth: (Upper Band - Lower Band) / SMA(Close, 20)
  - Average True Range (ATR)
    - ATR(14)
    - ATR(28)
- Consider Intraday Price Dynamics
  - Intraday Momentum
    - Close - Open
    - High - Low
  - Intraday Volatility
    - True Range
- Integrate Multi-Timeframe Analysis
  - Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Timeframes
    - SMA, EMA, RSI, OBV
    - Cross-validation across timeframes
- Develop Custom Composite Factors
  - Combine SMA, RSI, and OBV
  - Combine EMA, ADX, and Volume ROC
  - Create a custom factor using high, low, close, and volume
  - Example: (Close - Open) / (High - Low) * Volume
- Evaluate Seasonality and Cyclicality
  - Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly Returns
  - Seasonal Trends
- Implement Machine Learning Techniques
  - Feature Engineering
  - Model Training
  - Model Evaluation
  - Factor Importance
- Backtest and Validate
  - Historical Performance
  - Out-of-Sample Testing
  - Robustness Check
  - Risk Management
- Continuous Monitoring and Adjustment
  - Regular Factor Re-evaluation
  - Adaptive Strategy
  - Real-time Data Integration
