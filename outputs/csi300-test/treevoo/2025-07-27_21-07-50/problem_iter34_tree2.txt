```txt
- Analyze price patterns
  - Calculate daily return
    - (close[t] - open[t]) / open[t]
  - Examine momentum
    - Calculate simple moving average (SMA) of close prices over a short period
      - SMA_short = sum(close[t-i] for i in range(7)) / 7
    - Calculate simple moving average (SMA) of close prices over a long period
      - SMA_long = sum(close[t-i] for i in range(21)) / 21
    - Compare short-term and long-term SMAs
      - SMA_diff = SMA_short - SMA_long
  - Identify trend strength
    - Calculate the average true range (ATR) over a period
      - ATR = (sum((high[t-i] - low[t-i]) for i in range(14)) + sum(abs(high[t-i] - close[t-i-1]) for i in range(14)) + sum(abs(low[t-i] - close[t-i-1]) for i in range(14))) / 42
- Evaluate trading volume
  - Measure volume change
    - Volume_change = (volume[t] - volume[t-1]) / volume[t-1]
  - Combine with price movement
    - Volume_price_signal = (close[t] - close[t-1]) * Volume_change
- Assess volatility
  - Calculate historical volatility
    - Volatility = std_dev(close[t-i] for i in range(20))
- Consider market depth
  - Analyze bid-ask spread
    - Bid_ask_spread = high[t] - low[t]
- Integrate multiple factors
  - Create a composite score
    - Composite_score = (SMA_diff * 0.3) + (Volume_price_signal * 0.2) + (Volatility * 0.2) + (Bid_ask_spread * 0.3)
```
