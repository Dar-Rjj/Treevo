Volume Adjusted Momentum Indicator (VAMI)
├── Calculate Daily Price Change
│   ├── Subtract Open from Close
│   └── Use (Close - Open) as Daily Price Change
├── Calculate Volume Adjusted Price Change
│   ├── Multiply Daily Price Change by Volume
│   └── Sum over a 20-day Window
├── Calculate Moving Average of Volume
│   ├── Use Volume Data
│   └── Compute 20-day Moving Average
├── Adjust for Volatility
│   ├── Calculate True Range
│   │   ├── Max(High - Low, High - Previous Close, Previous Close - Low)
│   │   └── Use True Range as Measure of Volatility
│   ├── Calculate Average True Range
│   │   ├── Compute 20-day Moving Average of True Range
│   │   └── Use as Smoothed Volatility Measure
│   ├── Divide Volume Adjusted Price Change by Average True Range
│   └── Output Adjusted VAMI
└── Interpret Adjusted VAMI
    ├── Higher Adjusted VAMI indicates strong momentum adjusted for volatility
    └── Lower Adjusted VAMI indicates weak momentum adjusted for volatility
