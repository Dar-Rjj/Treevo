```python
import pandas as pd

def heuristics_v2(df):
    """
    This function generates a novel and interpretable alpha factor based on the given market features.
    The factor is calculated as the ratio of the average of (high - open) and (close - low) to the 5-day moving average of volume.
    A higher value of this factor could indicate a stronger buying pressure relative to trading volume, potentially leading to positive future returns.
    
    Parameters:
    df (pd.DataFrame): A DataFrame with columns ['open', 'high', 'low', 'close', 'amount', 'volume'] and index as date.
    
    Returns:
    pd.Series: A Series representing the factor values, indexed by date.
    """
    # Calculate the difference between high and open, and close and low
    high_open_diff = df['high'] - df['open']
    close_low_diff = df['close'] - df['low']
    
    # Calculate the average of these differences
    avg_diff = (high_open_diff + close_low_diff) / 2
    
    # Calculate 5-day moving average of volume
    vol_5d_ma = df['volume'].rolling(window=5).mean()
    
    # Alpha factor as the ratio of the average difference to 5-day volume MA
    alpha_factor = avg_diff / vol_5d_ma
    
    return alpha_factor
```
