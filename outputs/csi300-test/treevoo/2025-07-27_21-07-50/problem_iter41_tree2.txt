```txt
Calculate High-Low Spread Ratio
├── (High - Low) / (High + Low)

Calculate Volume-Weighted Close-Open Difference
├── (Close - Open) * Volume

Integrate Both Factors
├── (High-Low Spread Ratio) + (Volume-Weighted Close-Open Difference)

Composite Alpha Factor
├── (Volume-Weighted Close - EMA)
│   ├── EMA of Close prices (21-day period)
│   └── Volume-Weighted Close Price
│       └── Close * Volume
└── Add Medium-Term Reversal
    ├── Daily Returns (Close/Open - 1)
    └── Aggregate Past 10-Day Returns

Short-Term Momentum
├── Rate of change over 5 days
└── (Current Close - Close 5 days ago) / (Close 5 days ago)

Medium-Term Trend
├── Rate of change over 20 days
└── (Current Close - Close 20 days ago) / (Close 20 days ago)

Volatility and Mean Reversion Indicator
├── Average daily range over 20 days
└── Sum(High - Low) over 20 days / 20

Institutional Activity Indicator
├── 20-day VWAP
└── Sum(Close * Volume) over 20 days / Total Volume over 20 days

Liquidity Proxy
├── Average daily amount traded over 20 days
└── Sum(Daily Amount Traded) over 20 days / 20

Corrected Intraday Momentum with Adjustments
├── Volume-Weighted Intraday Return
│   └── (Close - Open) * sqrt(Volume)
├── Price Volatility
│   └── Standard deviation of close price returns over 20-day lookback
├── Volume Volatility
│   └── Standard deviation of volume over 20-day lookback
└── Adjusted Intraday Momentum
    └── Volume-Weighted Intraday Return - Price Volatility - Volume Volatility

Generate Adjusted Return
├── Intraday Log-Return (ln(Close/Open))
└── Incorporate Intraday Volatility
    ├── True Range (max(High, PrevClose) - min(Low, PrevClose))
    └── Scale Log-Return by True Range
└── Incorporate Volume Impact
    ├── Volume Change ((Current Volume - Previous Day's Volume) / Previous Day's Volume)
    └── Multiply Volume Change with Adjusted Return

Combined Alpha Factor
├── Combine Core Factors
│   └── Short-Term Momentum + Medium-Term Trend + Volatility and Mean Reversion Indicator + Institutional Activity Indicator + Liquidity Proxy + Corrected Intraday Momentum + Integrate Both Factors + Composite Alpha Factor
└── Trend-Adjusted Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Apply Long-Term and Short-Term Trend Adjustment
    │   ├── Long-Term Trend (100-day MA)
    │   ├── Short-Term Trend (20-day EMA)
    │   ├── If (Short-Term Trend - Long-Term Trend > 0), multiply by 1.5
    │   └── If (Short-Term Trend - Long-Term Trend < 0), multiply by 0.5
└── Add Adjusted Return
    └── (Combined Alpha Factor) + Adjusted Return
```
