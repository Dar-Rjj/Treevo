│   ├── Multiply Momentum Component and Reversal Component
├── Volume Adjusted Factor
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Divide by Moving Average of Volume

└── Final VAIMRF

    ├── Divide by Volume Adjusted Factor

        └── Lower VAIMRF indicates weak momentum with or without reversal
