Crossover of Integrated Relative Price Movement, Volume-Adjusted Momentum, Intraday Volatility, and SMA-based Trend Analysis

├── Calculate High-Low Spread Ratio
│   ├── Use High price
│   ├── Use Low price
│   └── Calculate (High - Low) / (High + Low)

├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Close-Open Difference
│   ├── Use Open price
│   ├── Use Close price
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Multiply (Close - Open) by Volume

├── Combine Initial Factors
│   ├── Compute the Sum of High-Low Spread Ratio and Volume-Weighted Close-Open Difference
│   └── Store as Combined_Factors

├── Compute Adjusted Close Price
│   ├── Multiply Close price by Volume

├── Subtract EMA from Adjusted Close
│   ├── Calculate Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Close prices
│   │   ├── Use Close price
│   │   └── Set EMA period (e.g., 21 days)
│   └── Subtract EMA from Volume-Weighted Close Price
│       └── Store as EMA_Adjusted_Close

├── Add Medium-Term Reversal
│   ├── Compute past m-day (e.g., 10 days) returns
│   │   └── Use Open and Close prices to compute daily returns
│   └── Aggregate past m-day (e.g., 10 days) returns
│       └── Store as Medium_Term_Reversal

├── Generate Intraday Log-Return
│   ├── (Close - Open) / Open
│   └── Store as Intraday_Log_Return

├── Incorporate Volume Impact
│   ├── Compute Volume Change
│   │   ├── (Current Volume - Previous Day's Volume) / Previous Day's Volume
│   └── Add Volume Change to Intraday Log-Return
│       └── Store as Volume_Adjusted_Intraday_Log_Return

├── Integrate Composite Alpha Factor
│   ├── Add the Sum of Initial Factors
│   └── Add the Result of Subtraction of EMA from Adjusted Close
│       └── Add Medium-Term Reversal
│           └── Store as Composite_Alpha_Factor

├── Analyze Momentum
│   ├── Calculate Short-Term SMA
│   │   └── Set SMA period (e.g., 7 days)
│   ├── Calculate Long-Term SMA
│   │   └── Set SMA period (e.g., 21 days)
│   └── SMA_diff = SMA_short - SMA_long
│       └── Store as SMA_Diff

├── Evaluate Volume and Price Movement
│   ├── Volume_change = (Volume[t] - Volume[t-1]) / Volume[t-1]
│   └── Volume_price_signal = (Close[t] - Close[t-1]) * Volume_change
│       └── Store as Volume_Price_Signal

├── Assess Volatility
│   └── Calculate Historical Volatility
│       └── Volatility = std_dev(Close[t-i] for i in range(20))
│           └── Store as Volatility

├── Create Final Composite Score
│   ├── Composite_score = (SMA_diff * 0.3) + (Volume_Price_Signal * 0.2) + (Volatility * 0.2)
│   └── Store as Composite_Score

├── Final Integration
│   ├── Combine the Integrated Factors and the Composite Alpha Factor
│   │   ├── Add the sum of High-Low Spread Ratio and Volume-Weighted Close-Open Difference
│   │   └── Add the Composite Alpha Factor
│   └── Combine with Adjusted Return
│       └── Add Adjusted Return to the combined factors
│           └── Store as Final_Integrated_Factor

├── Incorporate Long-Term Momentum
│   ├── Calculate Long-Term Momentum
│   │   ├── Use Close price
│   │   └── Calculate simple moving average (SMA) of close prices over a long period (e.g., 200 days)
│   └── Integrate with Combined Factors
│       └── Add Long-Term Momentum to the final integrated factor
│           └── Store as Long_Term_Momentum

└── Final Alpha Factor
