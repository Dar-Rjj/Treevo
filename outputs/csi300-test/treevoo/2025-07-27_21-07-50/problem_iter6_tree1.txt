```txt
Relative Price Movement and Volume Momentum
├── Calculate High-Low Spread Intensity
│   └── (High - Low) / Close
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Average Price Change
│   ├── (Close + Open) / 2
│   └── Multiply by Volume
├── Evaluate Price Trend Reversal Indicator
│   ├── (Close - Open) / Open
│   └── If > 0, then 1; else -1
└── Integrate All Factors
    └── Sum of (High-Low Spread Intensity * Volume-Weighted Average Price Change) and (Price Trend Reversal Indicator * Volume-Weighted Average Price Change)
```
