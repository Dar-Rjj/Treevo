```txt
Alpha Factor Exploration
- Price-Based Indicators
  - Momentum Indicators
    - Simple Moving Average (SMA) Crossover
      - Calculate 5-day SMA
      - Calculate 20-day SMA
      - Generate signal when 5-day SMA crosses above 20-day SMA
    - Rate of Change (ROC)
      - Calculate ROC for the last 10 days
      - Use ROC as a factor to capture momentum
  - Volatility Indicators
    - True Range
      - Calculate true range for each day
      - Calculate 14-day average true range (ATR)
      - Use ATR as a factor to measure volatility
- Volume-Based Indicators
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
    - Calculate OBV based on volume and close price
    - Use changes in OBV as a factor
  - Volume Price Trend (VPT)
    - Calculate VPT using volume and percentage change in price
    - Use VPT as a factor
- Combined Factors
  - Relative Strength Index (RSI)
    - Calculate 14-day RSI
    - Use RSI as a factor to indicate overbought/oversold conditions
  - Accumulation/Distribution Line (A/D Line)
    - Calculate A/D line using high, low, close, and volume
    - Use A/D line to assess buying and selling pressure
- Advanced Indicators
  - Bollinger Bands
    - Calculate 20-day moving average
    - Calculate 2 standard deviations from the 20-day moving average
    - Use Bollinger Bands to identify breakouts and reversals
  - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
    - Calculate 12-day EMA
    - Calculate 26-day EMA
    - Calculate MACD line (12-day EMA - 26-day EMA)
    - Calculate 9-day EMA of MACD line as the signal line
    - Generate signals when MACD line crosses the signal line
- Custom Composite Factors
  - Price and Volume Momentum
    - Combine ROC and OBV
    - Generate a composite factor to capture both price and volume momentum
  - Volatility and Sentiment
    - Combine ATR and RSI
    - Generate a composite factor to capture volatility and market sentiment
```
