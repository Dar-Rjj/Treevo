Alpha Mining for Predicting Future Stock Returns
- Technical Indicators
  - Moving Averages
    - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
      - SMA(Close, 20)
      - SMA(Close, 50)
    - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
      - EMA(Close, 20)
      - EMA(Close, 50)
  - Momentum Indicators
    - Relative Strength Index (RSI)
      - RSI(Close, 14)
    - Rate of Change (ROC)
      - ROC(Close, 12)
- Volume-Based Indicators
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
    - OBV(Close, Volume)
  - Chaikin Money Flow (CMF)
    - CMF(High, Low, Close, Volume, 20)
- Volatility Indicators
  - Bollinger Bands
    - BBands(Close, 20, 2)
  - Average True Range (ATR)
    - ATR(High, Low, Close, 14)
- Price Patterns
  - Inside Bar
    - InsideBar(Open, High, Low, Close)
  - Outside Bar
    - OutsideBar(Open, High, Low, Close)
- Custom Factors
  - Price-Volatility Ratio
    - (Close / ATR(High, Low, Close, 14))
  - Volume-Weighted Close
    - (Close * Volume) / SUM(Volume, 20)
  - Momentum-Volume Composite
    - (ROC(Close, 12) + OBV(Close, Volume)) / 2
