Volume Adjusted High-Low Momentum
├── Calculate Intraday Price Range
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
│       └── Subtract Low from High to get Range
├── Calculate Intraday Price Range Change
│   ├── Use Previous Day's High and Low
│   │   ├── Use High price of t-1
│   │   └── Use Low price of t-1
│   │       └── Subtract Low from High to get Previous Day's Range
│   └── Compute the Difference
│       └── Subtract Previous Day's Range from Current Day's Range
├── Volume Momentum Indicator
│   ├── Use Current Day's Volume
│   └── Use Previous Day's Volume
│       └── Divide Current Volume by Previous Volume
└── Combine Indicators
    ├── Multiply Intraday Price Range Change by Volume Momentum
    └── Result is the Volume Adjusted High-Low Momentum
