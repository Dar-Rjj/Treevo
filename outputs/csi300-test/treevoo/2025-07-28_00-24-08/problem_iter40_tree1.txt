```txt
- Construct Alpha Factors from OHLC, Volume, and Amount Data
  - Price Movement Analysis
    - Daily Price Change: Close(t) - Open(t)
    - High-Low Spread: High(t) - Low(t)
    - Close-High Gap: High(t) - Close(t)
  - Volume Analysis
    - Volume Change: Volume(t) - Volume(t-1)
    - Volume-to-Average Volume Ratio: Volume(t) / Average(Volume(t-n), ..., Volume(t-1))
  - Amount Analysis
    - Amount Change: Amount(t) - Amount(t-1)
    - Amount-to-Average Amount Ratio: Amount(t) / Average(Amount(t-n), ..., Amount(t-1))
  - Momentum and Reversal Indicators
    - Short-Term Momentum: (Close(t) - Close(t-n)) / n
    - Long-Term Momentum: (Close(t) - Close(t-m)) / m
    - Short-Term Reversal: (Low(t) - High(t-1)) / (High(t-1) - Low(t-1))
  - Market Sentiment Indicators
    - Up-Down Ratio: (High(t) - Open(t)) / (Open(t) - Low(t))
    - Buying Pressure: (Close(t) - Open(t)) / (High(t) - Low(t))
  - Liquidity Indicators
    - Price-to-Volume Correlation: Corr(Close, Volume)(t)
    - Price-to-Amount Correlation: Corr(Close, Amount)(t)
  - Volatility Indicators
    - Historical Volatility: StdDev(Close(t-n), ..., Close(t-1))
    - Intraday Volatility: StdDev(Open, High, Low, Close)(t)
```
