```txt
Intraday Momentum Adjusted for Volume and Price Spikes
├── Compute Intraday Momentum
│   └── (High - Low) + (Close - Open)
└── Adjust for Volume and Price Spike Factors
    ├── Volume Spike Factor
    │   └── Current Volume / Previous Volume
    ├── Price Spike Factor
    │   └── |Current Price Change| / |Previous Price Change|
    └── Final Adjusted Momentum
        └── Intraday Momentum * min(Volume Spike Factor, Price Spike Factor)
```
