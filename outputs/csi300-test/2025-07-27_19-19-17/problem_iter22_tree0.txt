Crossover of ATR, Intraday Momentum, and Volume Confirmation
├── Calculate Average True Range (ATR) with Intraday Return
│   ├── Compute True Range (TR)
│   │   ├── TR = max(High - Low, abs(High - Previous Close), abs(Low - Previous Close))
│   └── ATR = Average TR over 14 days
├── Incorporate Intraday and Open-to-Close Returns
│   ├── Intraday Return = High - Low
│   ├── Open-to-Close Return = Close - Open
│   └── Integrated Return = Intraday Return + Open-to-Close Return
├── Adjust with Volume Factor
│   ├── Volume Difference = Current Volume - Previous Volume
│   ├── Normalized Volume = Volume Difference / Average Volume over 20 days
├── Combine ATR, Integrated Returns, and Volume Adjustment
│   ├── Adjusted ATR = (ATR * Integrated Return) + Normalized Volume
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Short-term SMA = SMA(Close, 10 days)
│   ├── Long-term SMA = SMA(Close, 50 days)
│   └── SMA Difference = Short-term SMA - Long-term SMA
├── Integrate High-Low and Close-Open for Additional Momentum
│   ├── Intraday High-Low Spread = High - Low
│   ├── Momentum Product = Intraday High-Low Spread * Open-to-Close Return
│   └── Smoothed Momentum = EMA(Momentum Product, 5 days)
├── Final Alpha Factor
│   ├── Combined Factor = Adjusted ATR + (SMA Difference * Adjusted ATR) + Smoothed Momentum
│   ├── Volume Confirmation
│   │   ├── If Volume > Previous Day's Volume
│   │   └── Adjust Combined Factor
│   │       ├── Volume-Adjusted Factor = Combined Factor / sqrt(Volume)
│   │       └── Final Factor = Volume-Adjusted Factor * (Current Volume / Max Volume over last 10 days)
└── Use this combined factor as the final alpha factor
