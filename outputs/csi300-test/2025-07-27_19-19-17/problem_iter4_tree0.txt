```txt
Average True Range Momentum
├── Calculate Average True Range (ATR)
│   ├── Compute True Range (TR) for each day
│   │   └── Take the maximum of:
│   │       ├── High - Low
│   │       ├── High - Previous Close
│   │       └── Low - Previous Close
│   └── Average TR over a window, e.g., 14 days
└── Combine with Price Momentum
    ├── Calculate Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Close price
    │   ├── Over a short-term period, e.g., 10 days
    │   └── Over a long-term period, e.g., 50 days
    ├── Compute the difference between the two SMAs
    └── Multiply the difference by the ATR
```
