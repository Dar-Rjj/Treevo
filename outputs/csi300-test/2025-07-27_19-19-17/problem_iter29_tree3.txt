Crossover and Enhanced Alpha Factor Integration with Intraday, Open-to-Close Returns, Volume, and Price Momentum
├── Calculate True Range (TR) for each day
│   ├── TR = max(High - Low, abs(High - Previous Close), abs(Low - Previous Close))
├── Compute Average True Range (ATR)
│   └── ATR = Average TR over 14 days
├── Incorporate Intraday and Open-to-Close Returns
│   ├── Intraday Return = High - Low
│   ├── Open-to-Close Return = Close - Open
│   └── Integrated Return = Intraday Return + Open-to-Close Return
├── Adjust with Volume Difference
│   ├── Volume Difference = Current Volume - Previous Volume
│   ├── Check Volume
│   │   ├── If Volume > Previous Day's Volume
│   │   │   └── Volume Adjustment = (Current Volume / Previous Volume)
│   ├── Check Amount
│   │   ├── If Amount > Previous Day's Amount
│   │   │   └── Amount Adjustment = (Current Amount / Previous Amount)
│   └── Final Volume-Amount Adjustment
│       ├── If both Volume and Amount conditions are met
│       │   └── Combined Adjustment = Volume Adjustment * Amount Adjustment
│       │       └── Else: Combined Adjustment = 1
├── Combine ATR and Integrated Returns
│   ├── ATR Integrated Return = ATR * Integrated Return
├── Integrate High-Low and Close-Open for Additional Momentum
│   ├── Intraday High-Low Spread = High - Low
│   ├── Momentum Product = Intraday High-Low Spread * Open-to-Close Return
│   └── Smoothed Momentum = EMA(Momentum Product, 5 days)
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Short-term SMA = SMA(Close, 10 days)
│   ├── Long-term SMA = SMA(Close, 50 days)
│   └── SMA Difference = Short-term SMA - Long-term SMA
├── Combine ATR, Integrated Returns, and Momentum
│   ├── Combined Factor = ATR Integrated Return + (SMA Difference * ATR) + Smoothed Momentum
├── Final Volume-Amount and Trend Confirmation
│   ├── Volume-Adjusted Factor = Combined Factor * Combined Adjustment
│   ├── Open Price Trend = Open price - 10-day moving average of Open price
│   └── Enhanced Final Alpha Factor = Volume-Adjusted Factor + Open Price Trend
└── Use this enhanced combined factor as the final alpha factor
