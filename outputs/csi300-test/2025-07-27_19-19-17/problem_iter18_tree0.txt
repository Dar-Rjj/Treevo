Intraday Momentum, Volume, and Amount Confirmation Integration
├── Calculate Intraday High-Low Spread
│   ├── Subtract Low from High
├── Calculate Close-Open Return
│   ├── Subtract Open from Close
├── Integrate Returns

│   │   ├── Assign weight to Intraday High-Low Spread
│   │   └── Assign weight to Close-Open Return
└── Adjust for Volume and Amount Confirmation
    ├── Check Volume
    │   ├── If Volume > Previous Day's Volume
    │   │   └── Multiply Integrated Returns by (Current Volume / Previous Volume)
    └── Check Amount
        ├── If Amount > Previous Day's Amount
        │   └── Multiply Integrated Returns by (Current Amount / Previous Amount)
    └── Final Adjustment
        ├── If both Volume and Amount conditions are met
        │   └── Multiply Integrated Returns by (Current Volume / Previous Volume) * (Current Amount / Previous Amount)
