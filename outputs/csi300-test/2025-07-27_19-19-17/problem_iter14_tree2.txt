Integrated Intraday and ATR Momentum with Volume, Amount, and Close-Open Confirmation
├── Calculate True Range (TR) for each day
│   └── Max of:
│       ├── High - Low
│       ├── High - Previous Close
│       └── Low - Previous Close
├── Calculate Average True Range (ATR)
│   └── Average TR over a 14-day window
├── Calculate Intraday High-Low Spread
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
│       └── Subtract Low from High
├── Calculate Close-Open Return
│   ├── Use Close price
│   └── Use Open price
│       └── Subtract Open from Close
├── Integrated Return
│   └── (High - Low) + (Close - Open)
├── Weighted Sum of Intraday High-Low Spread and Close-Open Return
│   ├── Assign weight to Intraday High-Low Spread
│   └── Assign weight to Close-Open Return
├── Adjust for Volume and Amount Confirmation
│   ├── Use Volume
│   │   └── If Volume > Previous Day's Volume
│   └── Use Amount
│       ├── If Amount > Previous Day's Amount
│   └── Adjust Integrated Returns
│       ├── Multiply by (Current Volume / Previous Volume) * (Current Amount / Previous Amount)
│       └── Add a smoothing factor to the final adjusted return
│           ├── Smoothing Factor = 0.1 * (Previous Day's Adjusted Return - Current Adjusted Return)
├── Price Momentum
│   ├── Short-term SMA (10 days) of Close price
│   ├── Long-term SMA (50 days) of Close price
│   └── Difference between Short-term SMA and Long-term SMA
└── Final Alpha Factor
    └── (Weighted Sum of Intraday High-Low Spread and Close-Open Return + Adjusted Integrated Returns) * (Difference * ATR)
