- Short-Term Momentum and Volatility
  - Calculate the Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of closing prices over a short period (e.g., 10 days)
    - EMA_Close_10 = EMA(Close, 10)
  - Compute the short-term momentum factor by comparing the current close to the EMA
    - Momentum_Factor = Close - EMA_Close_10
  - Calculate the standard deviation of daily returns over a short period (e.g., 10 days)
    - StdDev_Returns_10 = StdDev(Returns, 10)
  - Create an adaptive volatility factor
    - Adaptive_Volatility = StdDev_Returns_10

- Volume Trends
  - Calculate the EMA of volume over a short period (e.g., 10 days)
    - EMA_Volume_10 = EMA(Volume, 10)
  - Compute the volume trend factor by comparing the current volume to the EMA
    - Volume_Trend = Volume - EMA_Volume_10

- Sector Performance
  - Calculate the average return of the sector over a short period (e.g., 10 days)
    - Avg_Sector_Return = Average(Sector_Returns, 10)
  - Compute the relative sector performance factor
    - Sector_Performance = Returns - Avg_Sector_Return

- Dynamic Weights
  - Calculate the liquidity factor using the average of the last 10 days' volume
    - Liquidity_Factor = Average(Volume, 10)
  - Adjust the momentum, volatility, and volume factors based on liquidity
    - Adjusted_Momentum = Momentum_Factor * (Liquidity_Factor / Max_Liquidity)
    - Adjusted_Volatility = Adaptive_Volatility * (Liquidity_Factor / Max_Liquidity)
    - Adjusted_Volume_Trend = Volume_Trend * (Liquidity_Factor / Max_Liquidity)

- Composite Alpha Factor
  - Combine the adjusted factors into a composite alpha factor
    - Alpha_Factor = (Adjusted_Momentum + Adjusted_Volume_Trend - Adjusted_Volatility + Sector_Performance) / 4
