Intraday Price-Volume Interaction
├── Calculate Intraday Range
│   ├── Use High Price
│   └── Use Low Price
│       └── Subtract to get the range
├── Calculate Intraday Midpoint
│   ├── Use Open Price
│   └── Use Close Price
│       └── Average to get the midpoint
├── Determine Intraday Direction
│   ├── Compare Open and Close Prices
│   │   ├── If Open > Close, then Intraday is bearish
│   │   └── If Open < Close, then Intraday is bullish
│   └── Record direction as 1 for bullish, -1 for bearish
├── Compute Intraday Momentum Reversal Score
│   ├── Multiply Intraday Range by Intraday Direction
│   │   ├── If positive, implies a bearish reversal (buy signal)
│   │   └── If negative, implies a bullish continuation (sell signal)
├── Integrate Volume
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Weight the score by volume
│       └── Higher volume strengthens the signal
└── Calculate Intraday Volatility
    ├── Use High-Low Spread
    │   ├── Use High price
    │   └── Use Low price
    │       └── Subtract Low price from High price
    └── Adjust Intraday Momentum Reversal Score
        ├── Use Intraday Volatility
        └── Scale the score by Intraday Volatility
            └── Higher volatility increases the magnitude of the score
