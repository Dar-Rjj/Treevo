```txt
- Calculate momentum metrics
  - Compute simple moving average (SMA) over a 10-day window
    - SMA_10: (Sum of close prices over the last 10 days) / 10
  - Compute simple moving average (SMA) over a 30-day window
    - SMA_30: (Sum of close prices over the last 30 days) / 30
  - Compute the difference between 10-day and 30-day SMAs
    - Momentum_SMA_Diff: SMA_10 - SMA_30
  - Compute exponential moving average (EMA) over a 10-day window
    - EMA_10: Close * (2 / (10 + 1)) + Previous EMA * (1 - (2 / (10 + 1)))
  - Compute exponential moving average (EMA) over a 30-day window
    - EMA_30: Close * (2 / (30 + 1)) + Previous EMA * (1 - (2 / (30 + 1)))
  - Compute the difference between 10-day and 30-day EMAs
    - Momentum_EMA_Diff: EMA_10 - EMA_30
  - Compute rate of change (ROC) over a 10-day window
    - ROC_10: (Close - Close 10 days ago) / Close 10 days ago

- Calculate volatility metrics
  - Compute standard deviation of daily returns over a 10-day window
    - Volatility_Std_10: Standard deviation of (Close - Open) for the last 10 days
  - Compute standard deviation of daily returns over a 30-day window
    - Volatility_Std_30: Standard deviation of (Close - Open) for the last 30 days
  - Compute average true range (ATR) over a 14-day window
    - ATR_14: Average of (High - Low) for the last 14 days
  - Compute parkinson volatility over a 30-day window
    - Parkinson_Vol_30: Sqrt((1 / (4 * Ln(2) * 30)) * Sum(Ln(High / Low)^2) for the last 30 days)

- Calculate volume-weighted metrics
  - Compute volume-weighted average price (VWAP)
    - VWAP: (Sum(Close * Volume) / Sum(Volume)) over the current day
  - Compute volume-weighted simple moving average (VWSMA) over a 10-day window
    - VWSMA_10: (Sum(Close * Volume) over the last 10 days) / (Sum(Volume) over the last 10 days)
  - Compute volume-weighted exponential moving average (VWEMA) over a 10-day window
    - VWEMA_10: Close * (2 / (10 + 1)) + Previous VWEMA * (1 - (2 / (10 + 1))) * (Volume / Previous Volume)

- Calculate liquidity metrics
  - Compute turnover ratio over a 30-day window
    - Turnover_Ratio_30: (Sum(Volume) / Sum(Amount)) for the last 30 days
  - Compute liquidity index over a 30-day window
    - Liquidity_Index_30: (Sum(Volume) / Sum(Amount)) * (High - Low) for the last 30 days

- Combine metrics into a final alpha factor
  - Alpha_Factor: (Momentum_SMA_Diff + Momentum_EMA_Diff + ROC_10) / (Volatility_Std_10 + Volatility_Std_30 + ATR_14 + Parkinson_Vol_30) * (VWAP + VWSMA_10 + VWEMA_10) / (Turnover_Ratio_30 + Liquidity_Index_30)
```
