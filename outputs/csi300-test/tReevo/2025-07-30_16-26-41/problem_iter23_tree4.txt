```txt
- Dynamic, Volume-Weighted Momentum with Adaptive Volatility
  - Calculate Volume-Weighted Price (VWP)
    - VWP = (Volume * Close) / Total_Volume
  - Compute Short-Term Momentum
    - Short_Term_Momentum = (VWP_t - VWP_(t-n)) / VWP_(t-n)
      - n = 10 days
  - Compute Long-Term Momentum
    - Long_Term_Momentum = (VWP_t - VWP_(t-m)) / VWP_(t-m)
      - m = 20 days
  - Adaptive Volatility
    - Volatility = Standard_Deviation(VWP, window=30 days)
  - Combine Momentum and Volatility
    - Alpha_Factor = (Short_Term_Momentum + Long_Term_Momentum) / Volatility
  - Sector-Specific EMA
    - Compute EMA for each sector
      - EMA_Sector = Exponential_Moving_Average(Close, span=20 days, sector_specific=True)
  - Liquidity Adjustment
    - Liquidity = (Close - Open) / (High - Low)
    - Adjust Alpha Factor
      - Alpha_Factor_Adjusted = Alpha_Factor * Liquidity
  - Final Alpha Factor
    - Final_Alpha_Factor = Alpha_Factor_Adjusted
```
