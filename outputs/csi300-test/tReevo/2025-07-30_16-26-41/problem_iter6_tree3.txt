Intraday Dynamics and Volume-Weighted Momentum Reversal
├── Calculate Intraday Range
│   ├── Use High Price of day t
│   └── Use Low Price of day t
│       └── Subtract to get the range (High - Low)
├── Calculate Intraday Midpoint
│   ├── Use Open Price of day t
│   └── Use Close Price of day t
│       └── Average to get the midpoint ((Open + Close) / 2)
├── Determine Intraday Direction
│   ├── Compare Open and Close Prices of day t
│   │   ├── If Open > Close, then Intraday is bearish
│   │   └── If Open < Close, then Intraday is bullish
│   └── Record direction as 1 for bullish, -1 for bearish
├── Compute Intraday Momentum Reversal Score
│   ├── Multiply Intraday Range by Intraday Direction
│   │   ├── If positive, implies a bearish reversal (buy signal)
│   │   └── If negative, implies a bullish continuation (sell signal)
├── Weight by Volume
│   ├── Use Volume of day t
│   └── Multiply the score by Volume
├── Incorporate Volume-Based Strengthening
│   ├── Calculate Rolling Average Volume
│   │   ├── Take the average of Volume over past 20 days
│   └── Adjust the score based on recent volume
│       ├── If Volume of day t > Rolling Average Volume
│       │   └── Multiply the score by 1.5 (stronger signal)
│       └── If Volume of day t <= Rolling Average Volume
│           └── Leave the score unchanged
└── Incorporate Volatility Adjustment
    ├── Calculate 20-Day Standard Deviation of Daily Returns
    │   ├── Daily Returns: (Close of Day t - Close of Day t-1) / Close of Day t-1
    └── Adjust the score by Volatility
        ├── Divide the score by the 20-Day Standard Deviation
        └── This normalizes the score relative to volatility
