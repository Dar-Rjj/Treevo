- Integrated Alpha Factors with Sentiment, News Impact, and Sector Performance
  - Price and Volume Based Factors
    - Dynamic Window Momentum and Volatility Integration
      - Volume-Weighted Moving Averages (VWMA)
        - VWMA Short-Term (e.g., 5 days)
        - VWMA Medium-Term (e.g., 20 days)
        - VWMA Long-Term (e.g., 60 days)
      - Exponential Moving Averages (EMA)
        - EMA Short-Term (e.g., 5 days)
        - EMA Medium-Term (e.g., 20 days)
        - EMA Long-Term (e.g., 60 days)
      - Relative Strength Index (RSI) with Exponential Smoothing
        - RSI Short-Term (e.g., 5 days)
        - RSI Medium-Term (e.g., 14 days)
      - Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
        - VWAP Short-Term (e.g., 5 days)
        - VWAP Medium-Term (e.g., 20 days)
      - Volatility Metrics
        - Standard Deviation of Returns (e.g., 20 days)
        - Average True Range (ATR) (e.g., 14 days)
      - Combined Momentum and Volatility Factor
        - Calculate the difference between short-term and long-term VWMA
        - Adjust the difference by the standard deviation of returns
        - Incorporate the RSI to balance overbought/oversold conditions
        - Weight the final factor by the average true range (ATR)
      - Liquidity and Recent Market Conditions
        - Volume-Weighted Turnover (e.g., 5 days)
        - Recent Volume Ratio (today / average volume over the past 5 days)
        - Adjust the combined momentum and volatility factor by the recent volume ratio
  - Sentiment and News Impact
    - Sentiment Analysis
      - Daily Sentiment Score (based on news articles, social media, etc.)
      - Sentiment Trend (e.g., 5-day moving average of daily sentiment scores)
    - News Impact
      - News Volume (number of news articles related to the stock in the past 5 days)
      - News Sentiment (average sentiment score of news articles in the past 5 days)
  - Sector and Industry Performance
    - Sector Momentum
      - Sector Return (e.g., 20-day return of the sector)
      - Sector Volatility (e.g., 20-day standard deviation of the sector return)
    - Industry Specific Factors
      - Industry Return (e.g., 20-day return of the industry)
      - Industry Volatility (e.g., 20-day standard deviation of the industry return)
  - Integrated Alpha Factor
    - Combine price and volume based factors, sentiment, and sector performance
    - Weight the combined factor by the recent volume ratio
    - Adjust for news impact (e.g., by incorporating the news sentiment and volume)
    - Final alpha factor: weighted sum of all sub-factors
