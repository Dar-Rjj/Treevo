```txt
- Explore Short-Term Momentum
  - Calculate Price Change Factors
    - Daily Return: (Close_t - Close_{t-1}) / Close_{t-1}
    - 2-Day Return: (Close_t - Close_{t-2}) / Close_{t-2}
    - 5-Day Return: (Close_t - Close_{t-5}) / Close_{t-5}
  - Evaluate Momentum Reversal
    - Identify Extreme Movements: (High_t - Low_t) / Low_t
    - Calculate Momentum Reversal Signal: (High_t - Close_t) / (High_t - Low_t)
- Adaptive Volatility
  - Measure Historical Volatility
    - Standard Deviation of Daily Returns over 20 Days
    - Standard Deviation of Daily Returns over 50 Days
  - Dynamic Volatility Adjustment
    - Use Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Volatility
      - EMA(20-Day Volatility, alpha=0.2)
      - EMA(50-Day Volatility, alpha=0.1)
- Incorporate Volume Trends
  - Calculate Volume Change Factors
    - Daily Volume Change: (Volume_t - Volume_{t-1}) / Volume_{t-1}
    - 5-Day Volume Change: (Volume_t - Volume_{t-5}) / Volume_{t-5}
  - Integrate Volume with Price
    - Volume-Weighted Price: (Close_t * Volume_t + Close_{t-1} * Volume_{t-1}) / (Volume_t + Volume_{t-1})
- Consider Sector Performance
  - Relative Sector Strength
    - Calculate Sector Index Returns
      - Sector Index_t - Sector Index_{t-1} / Sector Index_{t-1}
    - Sector Outperformance: Stock Return - Sector Index Return
- Simplify Calculations and Adjust for Liquidity
  - Use Simple Moving Averages (SMA)
    - SMA(Close, 20)
    - SMA(Close, 50)
  - Adjust for Liquidity
    - Liquidity Factor: (Volume_t / (High_t - Low_t))
- Balance Factors to Avoid Overfitting
  - Combine Momentum and Volatility
    - Momentum-Volatility Ratio: (5-Day Return) / (20-Day Volatility)
  - Integrate Volume and Sector Performance
    - Composite Alpha Factor: (Momentum-Volatility Ratio * Volume-Weighted Price) + (Sector Outperformance * Liquidity Factor)
```
