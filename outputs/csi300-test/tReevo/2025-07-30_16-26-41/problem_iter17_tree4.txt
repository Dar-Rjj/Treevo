    - Weight the final factor by the average true range (ATR)
  - Liquidity and Recent Market Conditions
    - Volume-Weighted Turnover (e.g., 5 days)
    - Recent Volume Ratio (today / average volume over the past 5 days)
    - Adjust the combined momentum and volatility factor by the recent volume ratio
