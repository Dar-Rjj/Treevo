│   ├── Use Open price of day t

│   ├── Recent Weights (last 5 days)
│   │   ├── 0.6 for Intraday Range

│   └── Older Weights (more than 5 days ago)
│       ├── 0.5 for Intraday Range
│       └── 0.5 for Close-to-Open Return
└── Factor Calculation
    ├── For recent days
    │   ├── (0.6 * Intraday Range) + (0.4 * Close-to-Open Return)
    └── For older days
        ├── (0.5 * Intraday Range) + (0.5 * Close-to-Open Return)
