```txt
- Calculate Momentum Factors
  - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
    - 50-day SMA: (sum(close_prices[day-49:day]) / 50)
    - 200-day SMA: (sum(close_prices[day-199:day]) / 200)
  - Price Rate of Change (ROC)
    - 10-day ROC: ((close_prices[day] - close_prices[day-10]) / close_prices[day-10])
    - 30-day ROC: ((close_prices[day] - close_prices[day-30]) / close_prices[day-30])
- Calculate Volatility Factors
  - Historical Volatility
    - 20-day Historical Volatility: sqrt(sum((log(close_prices[day-19:day]) - mean(log(close_prices[day-19:day]))) ** 2) / 20)
    - 60-day Historical Volatility: sqrt(sum((log(close_prices[day-59:day]) - mean(log(close_prices[day-59:day]))) ** 2) / 60)
  - ATR (Average True Range)
    - 14-day ATR: (sum(max(high_prices[day-13:day] - low_prices[day-13:day], abs(high_prices[day-13:day] - close_prices[day-14:day]), abs(low_prices[day-13:day] - close_prices[day-14:day])) / 14
- Calculate Volume Factors
  - Volume Rate of Change (VROC)
    - 10-day VROC: ((volume[day] - volume[day-10]) / volume[day-10])
    - 30-day VROC: ((volume[day] - volume[day-30]) / volume[day-30])
  - On-Balance Volume (OBV)
    - OBV: if close_prices[day] > close_prices[day-1]: obv[day-1] + volume[day]
            elif close_prices[day] < close_prices[day-1]: obv[day-1] - volume[day]
            else: obv[day-1]
- Calculate Technical Indicators
  - RSI (Relative Strength Index)
    - 14-day RSI: rsi = 100 - (100 / (1 + (avg_gain / avg_loss)))
      - avg_gain = sum([max(close_prices[day-13:day] - close_prices[day-14:day-1], 0)] / 14)
      - avg_loss = -sum([min(close_prices[day-13:day] - close_prices[day-14:day-1], 0)] / 14)
  - MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
    - 12-day EMA: ema_12 = (close_prices[day] * 2/13) + (ema_12[day-1] * 11/13)
    - 26-day EMA: ema_26 = (close_prices[day] * 2/27) + (ema_26[day-1] * 25/27)
    - MACD Line: macd_line = ema_12 - ema_26
    - 9-day Signal Line: signal_line = 9-day EMA of macd_line
    - MACD Histogram: macd_histogram = macd_line - signal_line
```
