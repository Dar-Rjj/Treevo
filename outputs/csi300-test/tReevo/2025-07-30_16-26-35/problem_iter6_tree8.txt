│   ├── Calculate Market Return
│   │   ├── Use Close price of today

    ├── Near zero adjusted return
    │   └── Indicates no significant trend, may suggest market consolidation
    └── Low negative adjusted return
        └── Indicates a weakening trend, more affected by increased volatility
