Intraday Volatility Adjusted Return with Volume and Price Dynamics
├── Calculate Intraday Range
│   ├── Use High price of day t
│   └── Use Low price of day t
│       └── Compute Intraday Range: (High - Low)
├── Calculate Close-to-Open Return
│   ├── Use Open price of day t
│   └── Use Previous Day's (t-1) Close price
│       └── Compute Close-to-Open Return: (Close - Previous Day's Close) / Previous Day's Close
└── Combine Intraday Range and Close-to-Open Return
    ├── Weighted Combination
    │   ├── Assign weights based on recent volume trend
    │   ├── Compute Average Volume (last 20 days)
    │   ├── If Volume_t > Average Volume
    │   │   ├── Apply Recent Weights: 0.7 for Intraday Range, 0.3 for Close-to-Open Return
    │   └── Else
    │       ├── Apply Older Weights: 0.5 for Intraday Range, 0.5 for Close-to-Open Return
    └── Integrate Price Dynamics
        ├── Consider Momentum
        │   ├── Compute Momentum: (Close - Open) / Open
        │   └── Add Momentum to the Weighted Combination
        └── Consider Reversal
            ├── Compute Reversal: (Open - Close) / Open
            └── Subtract Reversal from the Weighted Combination
