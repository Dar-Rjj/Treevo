Volume Synchronized Momentum (VSM)
├── Calculate Intraday Momentum
│   ├── Use High and Low Prices for Intraday Volatility
│   │   ├── Compute Daily High-Low Spread (High - Low)
│   ├── Compare Close to Open Prices for Trend Direction
│   │   ├── Calculate Close-Open Spread (Close - Open)
│   └── Combine Intraday Volatility with Trend Direction
│       ├── Multiply Daily High-Low Spread by Close-Open Spread
├── Calculate Volume Synchronization
│   ├── Use Volume and Price Data
│   │   ├── Sum of Absolute Price Changes per Tick
│   │   │   ├── (Abs(Price_t - Price_t-1) for all t)
│   │   └── Total Volume
│   └── Generate Synchronization Metric
│       ├── Divide Sum of Absolute Price Changes by Total Volume
├── Weight Momentum by Volume Synchronization
│   ├── Use Calculated VSM
│   └── Multiply Momentum by Synchronization Metric
└── Adjust for Market Sentiment
    ├── Use Close Price and Previous Day's Close
    │   ├── Compute Market Return (Close - Previous Close)
    └── Modulate VSM with Market Return
        ├── If Market Return > 0, multiply VSM by 1.1
        └── If Market Return <= 0, multiply VSM by 0.9
