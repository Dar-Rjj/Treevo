```txt
Volume-Weighted Relative Strength Index (VRSI)
├── Calculate Price Differences
│   ├── Use Close price of day t and day t-1
│   └── Subtract Day t-1 Close from Day t Close
├── Separate Positive and Negative Gains
│   ├── Identify Positive Gains
│   │   ├── If Difference > 0, then Positive Gain = Difference
│   │   └── Else, Positive Gain = 0
│   └── Identify Negative Gains
│       ├── If Difference < 0, then Negative Gain = Absolute(Difference)
│       └── Else, Negative Gain = 0
├── Calculate Weighted Sum of Positive Gains
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Multiply each Positive Gain by corresponding Volume
├── Calculate Weighted Sum of Negative Gains
│   ├── Use Volume
│   └── Multiply each Negative Gain by corresponding Volume
├── Calculate Total Volume
│   ├── Sum of all Volumes
│   └── Divide Weighted Sum of Positive Gains by Total Volume
├── Calculate Average Gain and Loss
│   ├── Average Weighted Positive Gain over n days
│   └── Average Weighted Negative Gain over n days
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Relative Strength (VRS)
│   ├── Divide Average Weighted Positive Gain by Average Weighted Negative Gain
│   └── Add 1 to the result
└── Final VRSI Calculation
    ├── Apply VRS to RSI formula
    └── VRSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + VRS))
```
