Intraday Reversal Momentum
├── Calculate Intraday Range
│   ├── Use High price
│   └── Use Low price
├── Calculate Closing Position in Intraday Range
│   ├── Subtract Low price from Close price
│   └── Divide by Intraday Range
├── Determine Momentum Direction
│   ├── If Closing Position is greater than 0.5, mark as Bullish
│   └── Otherwise, mark as Bearish
├── Calculate Intraday Momentum Score
│   ├── If Bullish, set score to (1 - Closing Position)
│   │   └── This penalizes stocks that close near the high end of the range
│   └── If Bearish, set score to (Closing Position - 0.5)
│       └── This rewards stocks that close near the low end of the range
└── Incorporate Volume
    ├── Use Volume
    └── Multiply Momentum Score by Volume
        └── This weights the momentum score by the day's trading volume
