├── Calculate Intraday High-Low Range
│   ├── Use High price

│       └── Subtract low from high to get range

│   │   ├── Use Volume
│   │   └── Use Intraday High-Low Range
│   │       └── Divide Volume by Intraday High-Low Range
│   └── Multiply Intraday High-Low Range by Inverse
│       └── Compute Inverse of Volume-to-Range Ratio

│   ├── Calculate Price Change
│   │   ├── Use Close price of day t

│   │   └── Subtract Close price of day t-1 from Close price of day t

│   ├── Use Close price (t-1)

│       ├── Subtract Open (t-1) from Close (t-1)
│       └── Divide by Open (t-1)

    │   └── If Close (t) < Close (t-1), assign difference to Downward

        │   └── Sum Upward Changes over the last 14 days and divide by 14

        └── Compute RSI
            ├── Divide Average Upward Change by Average Downward Change
            └── Apply RSI Formula: 100 - (100 / (1 + (Average Upward / Average Downward)))
