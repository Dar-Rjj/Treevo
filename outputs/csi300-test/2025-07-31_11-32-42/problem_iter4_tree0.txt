```txt
Volume-Weighted Average Price Change over High and Low (VWAPCHL)
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Close minus Volume-Weighted Open
│   ├── Multiply each day's close price by its volume
│   └── Sum all the daily results
│   ├── Multiply each day's open price by its volume
│   └── Sum all the daily results
└── Calculate VWAPCHL
    ├── Subtract Volume-Weighted Open from Volume-Weighted Close
    └── Divide by (High - Low)
```
