Integrated Momentum-Volume Interaction and Trend Indicator (IMVITI)
├── Compute Short-Term Price Direction (SPDI)
│   ├── Calculate 5-day Exponential Moving Average of Close Prices
│   └── Compare Current Close Price to 5-day EMA
│       ├── If above, assign 1
│       └── If below, assign 0
├── Compute Medium-Term Price Range (MPR)
│   ├── Calculate 20-day Highest High
│   ├── Calculate 20-day Lowest Low
│   └── Evaluate Position in 20-day Range
│       ├── If in upper third, assign 1.5
│       ├── If in middle third, assign 1
│       └── If in lower third, assign 0.5
├── Calculate Volume Growth (VGF)
│   ├── Determine 3-day Moving Average of Volumes
│   └── Compare Current Volume to 3-day Moving Average
│       ├── If today's volume > 3-day average, assign 1
│       └── If today's volume <= 3-day average, assign 0
├── Aggregate Price Change Over N Days (APCN)
│   ├── Sum Daily Price Changes for Last N Days
│   └── Compute Average Price Change
│       ├── Use Sum of Daily Price Changes
│       └── Divide by N
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Close Price (VWCP)
│   ├── Compute Volume-Price Product
│   │   ├── Use Today's Volume
│   │   └── Multiply by Today's Close
│   └── Compute Volume-Weighted Price
│       ├── Use Volume-Price Product
│       └── Divide by Sum of Volumes for Last N Days
├── Integrate Indicators
│   ├── Sum SPDI, MPR, and VGF
│   └── Divide by 3 to Get IMVTI
├── Combine Price and Volume Factors
│   ├── Calculate Momentum Score
│   │   ├── Use APCN
│   │   └── Multiply by Scaling Factor (e.g., 1.5)
│   └── Calculate Volume Weighted Score
│       ├── Use VWCP
│       └── Multiply by Scaling Factor (e.g., 0.5)
└── Generate Final Alpha Factor (FAF)
    ├── Sum IMVTI, Momentum Score, and Volume Weighted Score
    └── Apply Exponential Smoothing
        ├── Use FAF
        └── Apply EMA with a Smoothing Factor (e.g., 0.2)
