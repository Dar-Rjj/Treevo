│   │   └── Calculate the 3-day Moving Average of Volumes
│   └── Evaluate if Volume is Increasing or Decreasing
│       ├── If volume today > 3-day moving average, assign 1
│       └── If volume today <= 3-day moving average, assign -1
├── Integrate Indicators
│   ├── Sum SPDI, MPDI, and VGF
│   └── Divide by 3 to Get EPVTI
└── Final Indicator Adjustment
    ├── If EPVTI > 0.66, assign 1
    ├── If 0.33 < EPVTI <= 0.66, assign 0.5
