Volume-Weighted Intraday Momentum Alpha
├── Calculate Intraday Momentum
│   ├── Compute Intraday High-Low Spread
│   │   ├── (High - Low)
│   └── Compute Previous Day's Open-to-Close Return
│       ├── (Close - Open[-1])
├── Calculate Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
│   ├── Daily VWAP
│   │   ├── (Open * Volume + High * Volume + Low * Volume + Close * Volume) / Total Volume
│   │   └── Total Volume: (Open * Volume + High * Volume + Low * Volume + Close * Volume) / 4
├── Combine Intraday Momentum and VWAP
│   ├── Subtract Intraday High-Low Spread from VWAP
│   │   └── (VWAP - (High - Low))
│   └── Weight by Intraday Volume
│       ├── Multiply Combined Value by Today's Volume
│       └── ((VWAP - (High - Low)) * Volume)
└── Smooth the Factor
    ├── Apply Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
    │   ├── Over the Last 7 Days
    │   └── EMA_7D(((VWAP - (High - Low)) * Volume))
