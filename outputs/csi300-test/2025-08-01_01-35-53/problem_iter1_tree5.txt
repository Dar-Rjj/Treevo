│   ├── Use High price
│   ├── Subtract Average True Range
└── Normalized Low

│   ├── If Next Day's Open is greater than Normalized High
│   │   └── Set Signal to 1
│   └── If Next Day's Open is less than Normalized Low
│       └── Set Signal to -1
└── If Signal is not 1 or -1
    └── Set Signal to 0
