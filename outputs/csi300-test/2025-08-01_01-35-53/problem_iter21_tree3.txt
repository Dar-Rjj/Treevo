Volume-Weighted Intraday Momentum Alpha
├── Calculate Intraday Momentum
│   ├── Compute Intraday High-Low Spread
│   │   ├── Use High price (High)
│   │   └── Use Low price (Low)
│   │       ├── Intraday High-Low Spread = High - Low
│   └── Compute Previous Day's Open-to-Close Return
│       ├── Use Previous Day's Open price (Open[-1])
│       └── Use Today's Close price (Close)
│           ├── Open-to-Close Return = Close - Open[-1]
├── Calculate Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
│   ├── Compute Daily VWAP
│   │   ├── Sum of (Price * Volume) / Total Volume
│   │   │   ├── Use High price (High)
│   │   │   ├── Use Low price (Low)
│   │   │   ├── Use Close price (Close)
│   │   │   ├── Use Open price (Open)
│   │   │   └── Use Volume (Volume)
│   │       ├── Daily VWAP = (Open + High + Low + Close) / 4
│   │       └── VWAP = Σ(Daily VWAP * Volume) / Σ(Volume)
├── Combine Intraday Momentum and VWAP
│   ├── Subtract Intraday High-Low Spread from VWAP
│   │   ├── Combined Value = VWAP - Intraday High-Low Spread
│   └── Weight by Intraday Volume
│       ├── Use Today's Volume (Volume)
│       └── Multiply Combined Value by Volume
│           ├── Weighted Value = Combined Value * Volume
└── Smooth the Factor
    ├── Apply Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
    │   ├── Over the Last 7 Days for Faster Response
    │   │   ├── EMA(Weighted Value, 7)
