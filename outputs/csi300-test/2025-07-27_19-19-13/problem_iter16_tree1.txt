Synthesized Intraday and Volume-Adjusted Alpha Factor
├── Intraday Volatility
│   └── (High price - Low price) / Average over Period
├── Volume-Weighted Momentum
│   └── (Close price t / Close price t-N) * Volume
├── Intraday Return
│   └── (High price - Low price) / Low price
├── Open-Close Price Movement
│   └── (Close price - Open price) / Open price
└── Combine Factors
    ├── Add Intraday Volatility
    ├── Subtract Volume-Weighted Momentum
    ├── Multiply by Intraday Return
    │   └── Adjust by Open-Close
    │       └── If Open-Close > 0, Multiply by (1 + |Open-Close|)
    │           Else, Multiply by (1 - |Open-Close|)
    └── Volume Change Adjustment
        ├── Volume Change: (Today's Volume - Previous Day's Volume) / Previous Day's Volume
        └── If Volume Change > 0, Multiply by (1 + Volume Change)
            Else, Multiply by (1 - |Volume Change|)
