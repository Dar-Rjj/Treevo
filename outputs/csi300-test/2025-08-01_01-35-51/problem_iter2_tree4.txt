Daily Implied Volatility Momentum
├── Calculate Daily Price Range
│   ├── Use High Price
│   ├── Use Low Price
│   └── Subtract to Find Daily Range
├── Calculate Average True Range (ATR)
│   ├── Calculate True Range for Each Day
│   │   ├── Maximum of (High - Low), (High - Previous Close), (Previous Close - Low)
│   ├── Select Last N Days
│   │   └── Define N (e.g., 10 days)
│   ├── Sum of Selected True Ranges
│   └── Divide by Number of Days
├── Calculate Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
│   ├── Calculate Typical Price for Each Day
│   │   └── (High + Low + Close) / 3
│   ├── Multiply Typical Price by Volume for Each Day
│   ├── Sum of (Typical Price * Volume) for Last N Days
│   └── Divide by Sum of Volumes for Last N Days
└── Combine ATR and VWAP
    ├── Normalize ATR (not normalized, just for relative comparison)
    │   └── Divide ATR by VWAP
    └── Assign Final Alpha Factor
        └── Use the Ratio of ATR to VWAP as the Alpha Factor
