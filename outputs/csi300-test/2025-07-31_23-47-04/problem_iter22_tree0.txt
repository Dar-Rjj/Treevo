├── Calculate Intraday Momentum

│   └── Subtract Close to Open Spread
│       ├── Use Close price of day t
│       └── Use Open price of day t

│   └── Intraday Momentum = High-Low Spread - Close-Open Spread

│   ├── Use Amount of day t
│   ├── Use Volume of day t
│   └── VWAP = Amount / Volume
├── Determine Volume Synchronization
│   ├── Compute Logarithmic Volume Change
│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t
│   │   └── Use Volume of day t-1
│   │   └── Logarithmic Volume Change = log(Volume_t / Volume_{t-1})
│   └── Calculate Logarithmic Return
│       ├── Use Close price of day t
│       └── Use Close price of day t-1
│       └── Logarithmic Return = log(Close_t / Close_{t-1})
├── Integrate Price and Volume Dynamics
│   ├── Combine Intraday Momentum and Logarithmic Return

│   └── Add VWAP

│   ├── Calculate Short-Term Intraday Trend
│   │   └── Short-Term Intraday Trend = (High - Low) / Low

│   │   ├── Compute Rolling Average Close
│   │   │   ├── Use Close price of day t
│   │   │   └── Use Close price of day t-1 to t-10
│   │   │   └── Rolling Average Close = Mean(Close_t, Close_{t-1}, ..., Close_{t-10})

│   ├── Compute Realized Volatility
│   │   ├── Use Close prices of day t-1 to t-10

│   ├── Compute Average Daily Volume
│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t-1 to t-10
