```txt
Relative Strength Index with Volume Adjustment (RSIVA)
├── Calculate Daily Price Change
│   ├── Use Close price for day t
│   └── Subtract Close price of day t-1
├── Separate Positive and Negative Changes
│   ├── Collect Positive Changes
│   └── Collect Negative Changes
├── Calculate Average Gain and Loss Over Lookback Period
│   ├── Sum Positive Changes over N days
│   │   ├── Weight Each Positive Change by Volume
│   │   └── Divide by N
│   └── Sum Negative Changes over N days
│       ├── Weight Each Negative Change by Volume
│       └── Divide by N (Take Absolute Value)
├── Compute RSIVA Factor
│   ├── 1 + (Average Loss / Average Gain)
│   └── Divide 1 by the Previous Computation
└── Output RSIVA
    ├── Clamp values between 0 and 100
    └── Final RSIVA Factor
```
