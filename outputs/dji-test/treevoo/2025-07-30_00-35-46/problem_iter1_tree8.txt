```txt
Dynamic Momentum and Volume Flow
├── Calculate Exponential Moving Average (EMA) Cross Signal
│   ├── EMA of Close Price over Short Period
│   │   └── Use Close price (last 5 days)
│   └── EMA of Close Price over Long Period
│       └── Use Close price (last 20 days)
├── Determine Momentum
│   ├── Compare Short and Long EMA
│   └── Categorize as Bullish or Bearish
│       ├── If Short EMA > Long EMA, mark as Bullish
│       └── If Short EMA < Long EMA, mark as Bearish
├── Calculate On-Balance Volume (OBV)
│   ├── Initialize OBV to 0
│   ├── If Close > Previous Close, increment OBV by Volume
│   ├── If Close < Previous Close, decrement OBV by Volume
│   └── If Close = Previous Close, leave OBV unchanged
└── Combine Momentum and OBV into a Factor
    ├── Weight OBV by the Difference between Short and Long EMA
    │   └── Weight = |Short EMA - Long EMA|
    └── Multiply OBV by Weight
        └── Final Factor = OBV * Weight
```
