```txt
Price and Volume Momentum with Adjusted Volatility Factor
├── Calculate Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
│   ├── Use High, Low, Close, and Volume from day t
│   └── Compute VWAP (Sum(High+Low+Close)/3 * Volume) / Sum(Volume)
├── Calculate Volume Increase Ratio
│   ├── Use Volume from day t-1
│   └── Use Volume from day t
│       └── Compute ratio (Volume_t / Volume_t-1)
├── Calculate Close Price Momentum
│   ├── Use Close price from day t-5 to t-1
│   └── Use Close price from day t
│       └── Compute average change (Close_t - Avg(Close_t-5...t-1))
├── Adjust for Price Volatility
│   ├── Use High price from day t
│   └── Use Low price from day t
│       └── Compute range (High_t - Low_t)
└── Combine Factors
    ├── Multiply Volume Increase Ratio by Close Price Momentum
    ├── Divide by Price Range
    │   └── Generate interim factor (Momentum * Ratio / Range)
    └── Adjust by VWAP
        └── Generate final factor (Interim Factor * VWAP)
```
