```txt
Volume and Momentum with Breakout Intensity and Volatility Adjustment
├── Volume-Weighted Breakout Intensity
│   ├── Compute High-Low Breakout Intensity
│   │   └── (High - Low) / Close
│   └── Weight by Volume
│       ├── If Breakout Intensity < 0, set to 0
│       └── Multiply by Volume
├── Volume-Adjusted Momentum (VAM)
│   ├── Compute Simple Moving Average (SMA) of Close Prices (20 days)
│   ├── Compute Momentum (5-day change in SMA)
│   └── Weight by Volume
│       ├── Normalize by average volume over the same momentum period
├── Price Volatility Indicator (PVI)
│   ├── Compute True Range (TR)
│   │   └── TR = max(High - Low, abs(High - Previous Close), abs(Low - Previous Close))
│   ├── Compute ATR (Average True Range, 14 days)
│   └── Adjust PVI by ATR and Smooth using EMA (7 days)
├── Volume Increase Ratio
│   └── (Volume_t / Volume_t-1)
├── Close Price Momentum
│   └── (Close_t - Avg(Close_t-5...t-1))
├── Price Range
│   └── (High_t - Low_t)
└── Combine Factors
    ├── Multiply Volume-Weighted Breakout Intensity and VAM
    ├── Divide by PVI
    ├── Multiply by Volume Increase Ratio
    ├── Multiply by Close Price Momentum
    ├── Divide by Price Range
    └── Subtract mean of the final value over a period (30 days)
```
