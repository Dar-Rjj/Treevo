```txt
Price and Volume Momentum with Adjusted Volatility Factor
├── Calculate Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)
│   └── VWAP = (Sum(High+Low+Close)/3 * Volume) / Sum(Volume)
├── Calculate Volume Increase Ratio
│   └── Volume Ratio = Volume_t / Volume_t-1
├── Calculate Close Price Momentum
│   └── Price Momentum = Close_t - Avg(Close_t-5...t-1)
├── Calculate Price Range
│   └── Price Range = High_t - Low_t
└── Generate Final Factor
    └── Final Factor = (Price Momentum * Volume Ratio) / Price Range * VWAP
```
