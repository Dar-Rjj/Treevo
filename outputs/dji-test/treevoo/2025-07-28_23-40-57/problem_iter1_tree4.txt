```txt
Advanced Momentum and Volume Trend Signal
├── Calculate Exponential Moving Average (EMA) of Close Prices
│   ├── Choose a short-term EMA (e.g., 12 days)
│   └── Choose a long-term EMA (e.g., 26 days)
└── Calculate the difference between EMAs
    ├── Subtract the long-term EMA from the short-term EMA
    └── Use the result as a trend indicator
        ├── If positive, it suggests an upward trend
        └── If negative, it suggests a downward trend
├── Calculate Volume Rate of Change (VROC)
│   ├── Use the Volume data
│   └── Calculate the rate of change over a period (e.g., 14 days)
└── Combine the trend indicator and VROC
    ├── Multiply the EMA difference by the VROC
    └── The result is the final alpha factor
        ├── High values indicate strong buying pressure
        └── Low values indicate strong selling pressure
```
