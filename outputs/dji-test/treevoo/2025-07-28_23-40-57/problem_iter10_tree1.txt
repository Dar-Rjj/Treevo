│   ├── Sum volume over n days (e.g., 10 days)

│   └── Factor 1 = Intraday Return * Volume Impact Score
├── Calculate Cumulative Volume Weighted Intraday Return

└── Combine Factors
    ├── Final Factor = Factor 1 * Factor 2
    └── Interpretation
        ├── Higher combined factor indicates stronger momentum with volume confirmation
        └── Lower combined factor suggests weaker momentum and less volume confirmation
