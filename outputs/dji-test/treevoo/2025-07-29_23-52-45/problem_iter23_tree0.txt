```txt
Crossover Volume-Weighted, Intraday Volatility-Adjusted, and Trend-Reversal Momentum with Price and Volume Indicators
├── Short-Term Return
│   ├── (Close price of day t - Close price of day (t-5)) / Close price of day (t-5)
├── Long-Term Return
│   ├── (Close price of day t - Close price of day (t-60)) / Close price of day (t-60)
├── Volume-Weighted Returns
│   ├── Volume-Weighted Short-Term
│   │   ├── Short-Term Return * Volume of day t
│   └── Volume-Weighted Long-Term
│       ├── Long-Term Return * Volume of day t
├── Intraday Volatility
│   ├── Absolute(High price of day t - Low price of day t)
├── Adjusted Volume-Weighted Returns by Intraday Volatility
│   ├── Adjusted Volume-Weighted Short-Term
│   │   ├── Volume-Weighted Short-Term / Intraday Volatility
│   └── Adjusted Volume-Weighted Long-Term
│       ├── Volume-Weighted Long-Term / Intraday Volatility
├── Momentum Factor
│   ├── Adjusted Volume-Weighted Short-Term - Adjusted Volume-Weighted Long-Term
├── Reversal Indicator
│   ├── -Momentum Factor
├── Daily Price Movement
│   ├── Close price of day t - Open price of day t
├── Uptrend Count
│   ├── Count of days in the past 10 days where Close > Open
├── Average True Range (ATR)
│   ├── ATR over the last 10 days using high, low, and close prices
├── ATR Moving Average
│   ├── 20-day moving average of ATR
├── ATR Signal
│   ├── If current ATR < 20-day moving average
└── Trading Volume Relative to Price
    ├── Positive Volume Score
    │   ├── Count of days in the past 30 days where (Close > Open) and (Volume > 30-day average volume)
├── Intraday Bullishness
│   ├── (High price of day t - Open price of day t) / (Open price of day t - Low price of day t)
├── Bullishness Ratio
│   ├── Average of Intraday Bullishness over the past 5 days
├── Combined Indicator
    ├── (Momentum Factor + Cumulative Volume-Adjusted Price Change + Uptrend Count + ATR Signal + Positive Volume Score + Bullishness Ratio) / 6
    ├── If Combined Indicator > 0
    │   └── Positive Signal
    ├── If Combined Indicator < 0
    │   └── Negative Signal
    └── Otherwise
        └── Neutral Signal
├── 5-Day Moving Average of Close Price
    ├── Compute the average of Close prices from day t-4 to day t
├── 60-Day Moving Average of Close Price
    ├── Compute the average of Close prices from day t-59 to day t
├── Trend Indicator
    ├── 5-Day Moving Average - 60-Day Moving Average
├── Final Alpha Factor
    ├── Reversal Indicator + Trend Indicator + Combined Indicator
```
