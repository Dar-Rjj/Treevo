Volume-Weighted and Price Momentum Factor with Enhanced Indicators
├── Short-Term Return
│   ├── (Close price of day t - Close price of day (t-5)) / Close price of day (t-5)
├── Long-Term Return
│   ├── (Close price of day t - Close price of day (t-60)) / Close price of day (t-60)
├── Volume-Weighted Returns
│   ├── Volume-Weighted Short-Term
│   │   ├── Short-Term Return * Volume of day t
│   └── Volume-Weighted Long-Term
│       ├── Long-Term Return * Volume of day t
├── Momentum Factor
│   ├── Volume-Weighted Short-Term - Volume-Weighted Long-Term
├── Price Change
│   ├── High price of day t - Low price of day t
├── Volume Adjusted Price Change
│   ├── Price Change * Volume of day t
├── Smoothed Momentum Calculation
│   ├── Rolling Average of Volume Adjusted Price Change (e.g., 10 days)
│   ├── Current Window Average - Previous Window Average
├── Momentum Trend
    ├── Moving Average of Momentum Factor (e.g., 20 days)
    ├── If Momentum Factor > Moving Average
    │   └── Strong Uptrend Signal
    ├── If Momentum Factor < Moving Average
    │   └── Strong Downtrend Signal
    └── Otherwise
        └── Sideways or Neutral Signal
├── Additional Factors
│   ├── Cumulative Volume
│   │   ├── Sum of volume from day (t-5) to day t
│   ├── Cumulative Price Change
│   │   ├── (High price of day t - Low price of day (t-5))
│   ├── Cumulative Volume-Adjusted Price Change
│   │   ├── (High price of day t - Low price of day (t-5)) * Cumulative Volume
│   ├── Rolling Average of Cumulative Volume-Adjusted Price Change (e.g., 10 days)
│   ├── Current Window Average - Previous Window Average
└── Combined Indicator
    ├── (Momentum Factor + Cumulative Volume-Adjusted Price Change) / 2
    ├── If Combined Indicator > 0
    │   └── Positive Signal
    ├── If Combined Indicator < 0
    │   └── Negative Signal
    └── Otherwise
        └── Neutral Signal
