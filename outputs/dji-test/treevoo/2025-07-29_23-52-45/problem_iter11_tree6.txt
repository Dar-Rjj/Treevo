│       │   └── Use SMA(Close, lookback_period)

│   └── Compute AVG(Volume, lookback_period)

│       │   ├── Use Volume

├── Calculate Short-Term Return
│   ├── Subtract Close price of day t-5 from Close price of day t
│   └── Divide by Close price of day t-5
│       └── Result: Short_term_return = (Close_t - Close_(t-5)) / Close_(t-5)
├── Calculate Long-Term Return
│   ├── Subtract Close price of day t-60 from Close price of day t
│   └── Divide by Close price of day t-60
│       └── Result: Long_term_return = (Close_t - Close_(t-60)) / Close_(t-60)

│   ├── Multiply Short-Term Return by Volume of day t

│   └── Multiply Long-Term Return by Volume of day t
│       └── Result: Volume_weighted_long_return = Long_term_return * Volume_t
├── Intraday Volatility
│   └── Absolute Value of (High - Low) of day t
│       └── Result: Intraday_volatility = |High_t - Low_t|
├── Adjusted Returns
│   ├── Divide Volume-Weighted Short-Term Return by Intraday Volatility
│   │   └── Result: Adjusted_short_return = Volume_weighted_short_return / Intraday_volatility
│   └── Divide Volume-Weighted Long-Term Return by Intraday Volatility
│       └── Result: Adjusted_long_return = Volume_weighted_long_return / Intraday_volatility
├── Combined Adjusted Momentum Factor
│   └── Subtract Adjusted Short-Term Return from Adjusted Long-Term Return
│       └── Result: Combined_adjusted_momentum = Adjusted_long_return - Adjusted_short_return
├── Enhanced Combined Momentum Factor
│   ├── Use Combined Adjusted Momentum Factor
