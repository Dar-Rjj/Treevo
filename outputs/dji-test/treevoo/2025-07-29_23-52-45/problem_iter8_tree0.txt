Volume-Adjusted Momentum, Returns, and Price Range Alpha Factors
├── Calculate Short-Term Return
│   ├── Use Close price of day t
│   └── Use Close price of day t-n (n=5)
│       └── Subtract and divide by Close price of day t-n
│           └── Result: Short_term_return = (Close_t - Close_(t-5)) / Close_(t-5)
├── Calculate Long-Term Return
│   ├── Use Close price of day t
│   └── Use Close price of day t-m (m=60)
│       └── Subtract and divide by Close price of day t-m
│           └── Result: Long_term_return = (Close_t - Close_(t-60)) / Close_(t-60)
├── Volume-Weighted Short-Term Return
│   ├── Use calculated Short-Term Return
│   └── Weight by Average Volume
│       ├── Use average Volume from day t to day t-n (n=5)
│       └── Multiply Short-Term Return by Average Volume
│           └── Result: Volume_weighted_short_return = Short_term_return * AVG(Volume_t to Volume_(t-5))
├── Volume-Weighted Long-Term Return
│   ├── Use calculated Long-Term Return
│   └── Weight by Average Volume
│       ├── Use average Volume from day t to day t-m (m=60)
│       └── Multiply Long-Term Return by Average Volume
│           └── Result: Volume_weighted_long_return = Long_term_return * AVG(Volume_t to Volume_(t-60))
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Define lookback period (e.g., 20 days)
│   └── Compute SMA(Close, lookback_period)
│       ├── Subtract SMA from Current Close Price
│       │   ├── Use Close price
│       │   └── Use SMA(Close, lookback_period)
│       └── Result: Momentum_signal = Close - SMA(Close, lookback_period)
├── Weight Momentum Signal by Volume
│   ├── Define lookback period (e.g., 20 days)
│   └── Compute AVG(Volume, lookback_period)
│       ├── Divide Volume by Average Volume
│       │   ├── Use Volume
│       │   └── Use AVG(Volume, lookback_period)
│       ├── Multiply Momentum Signal by Normalized Volume
│       │   ├── Use Momentum_signal
│       │   └── Use Normalized Volume
│       └── Result: Volume_adjusted_momentum = Momentum_signal * (Volume / AVG(Volume, lookback_period))
├── Combined Momentum Factor
│   ├── Use Volume-Weighted Short-Term Return
│   ├── Subtract Volume-Weighted Long-Term Return
│   └── Add Volume-Adjusted Momentum
│       └── Result: Combined_momentum_factor = Volume_weighted_short_return - Volume_weighted_long_return + Volume_adjusted_momentum
├── Reversal Indicator
│   ├── Use Combined Momentum Factor
│   └── Invert the Sign
│       └── Create contrarian signal
│           └── Result: Reversal_indicator = -Combined_momentum_factor
├── Calculate Price Range
│   ├── Use High price of day t
│   └── Subtract Low price of day t
│       └── Result: Price_range = High_t - Low_t
├── Volume-Adjusted Price Range

│   │   ├── Use Volume of day t
│   │   └── Multiply Price Range by Volume
│   └── Result: Adjusted_price_range = Price_range * Volume_t
└── Smoothed Adjusted Price Range
    ├── Rolling Sum of Adjusted Price Range
    │   ├── Set window (e.g., 10 days)
    │   └── Compute Rolling Sum
    └── Compute First Difference
        ├── Current Window Sum
        └── Previous Window Sum
            └── Result: Smoothed_adjusted_price_range = Current_window_sum - Previous_window_sum
