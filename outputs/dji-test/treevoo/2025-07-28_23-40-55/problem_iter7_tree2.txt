Price Momentum Adjusted for Volume and Amount
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Use Close price on day t
│   └── Use Close price on day t-20
│       ├── Calculate the difference
│       └── Forms a simple momentum indicator
├── Adjust for Volume Impact
│   ├── Compare day t's volume to 20-day average
│   │   ├── If above, multiply momentum by 1.1
│   │   └── If below, keep original momentum value
└── Adjust for Amount Impact
    ├── Compare day t's amount to 20-day average
    │   ├── If above, multiply adjusted momentum by 1.05
    │   └── If below, keep momentum value after volume adjustment
