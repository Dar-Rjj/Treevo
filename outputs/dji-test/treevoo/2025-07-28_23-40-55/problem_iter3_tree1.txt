Price Momentum with Volume and Amount Influence
├── Calculate Price Momentum
│   ├── Use Close price on day t
│   └── Use Close price on day t-20
│       ├── Calculate the difference
│       └── This forms a simple momentum indicator
├── Adjust for Volume Impact
│   ├── Use Volume on day t
│   └── Compare to Average Volume
│       ├── Calculate the average volume over the past 20 days
│       └── Determine if day t's volume is above or below this average
│           ├── If above, multiply momentum by 1.1
│           │   └── Represents a stronger signal due to higher trading activity
│           └── If below, keep the original momentum value
│               └── Indicates a neutral or weaker signal due to lower trading activity
└── Adjust for Amount Impact
    ├── Use Amount on day t
    └── Compare to Average Amount
        ├── Calculate the average amount over the past 20 days
        └── Determine if day t's amount is above or below this average
            ├── If above, multiply adjusted momentum by 1.05
            │   └── Represents an even stronger signal due to higher transaction amount
            └── If below, keep the momentum value after volume adjustment
                └── Indicates a neutral or weaker signal due to lower transaction amount
