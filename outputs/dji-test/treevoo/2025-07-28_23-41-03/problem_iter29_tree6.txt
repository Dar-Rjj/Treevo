Thought: Develop Enhanced Alpha Factors for Predicting Stock Returns
- Thought: Utilize Price-based Indicators
  - Thought: Calculate Momentum
    - Thought: Use Close Prices
      - Action: Compute the difference between today's close and the close n days ago.
  - Thought: Identify Trend Reversals
    - Thought: Analyze Highs and Lows
      - Action: Create a factor that signals when the low of today is higher than the high n days ago, suggesting an upward trend reversal.
  - Thought: Calculate Simple Moving Averages (SMA)
    - Action: Compute 50-day, 100-day, and 200-day SMA.
  - Thought: Intraday Return
    - Action: Compute (Close - Open) / Open.
  - Thought: Reversal Signal
    - Action: If Intraday Return > 0, Assign -1; If Intraday Return <= 0, Assign 1.
- Thought: Exploit Volume Information
  - Thought: Evaluate Buying/Selling Pressure
    - Thought: Measure Volume Change
      - Action: Calculate the change in volume from one day to the next as a raw signal for liquidity shifts.
  - Thought: Assess Strength of Moves
    - Thought: Combine Volume with Price Moves
      - Action: Multiply the percentage change in closing prices by the daily volume, indicating the strength of price moves on high-volume days.
  - Thought: Volume Adjustment
    - Action: Sum Volume over past n days and divide sum by n.
- Thought: Incorporate Transaction Amount Analysis
  - Thought: Gauge Financial Flows
    - Thought: Compare Today's Transaction Amount
      - Action: Determine if today's transaction amount is significantly larger than the average over the past n days, highlighting increased interest or activity.
- Thought: Generate Composite Alphas
  - Thought: Blend Price and Volume Signals
    - Action: Add the momentum indicator and the strength-of-moves metric for a hybrid alpha factor.
  - Thought: Mix in Transaction Insights
    - Action: Integrate the financial flows insight into the combined price and volume signal, creating a more comprehensive measure of future stock return predictability.
  - Thought: Combined Momentum and Reversal
    - Action: (100-day SMA - 200-day SMA) * Reversal Signal; (50-day SMA - 100-day SMA) * Reversal Signal.
  - Thought: High-Low Range Momentum
    - Action: (Today's High - Today's Low); (Today's High - Today's Low) - (Yesterday's High - Yesterday's Low).
  - Thought: Adjusted High-Low Range Momentum
    - Action: High-Low Range Momentum / Average Volume.
  - Thought: Combined Price and Volume Momentum
    - Action: (Price Change Momentum) * (Volume Adjustment).
- Thought: Final Alpha Factor
  - Action: (Combined Momentum and Reversal) + (Adjusted High-Low Range Momentum) + (Combined Price and Volume Momentum).
